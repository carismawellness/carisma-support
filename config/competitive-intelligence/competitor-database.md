## Cross-Brand Seasonal Intelligence

### High-Activity Windows (all 3 brands increase competitive spend)

These are the periods when the most competitor ads run simultaneously and CPL is most affected. Carisma must plan ahead:

1. **January (New Year)** — All categories surge. Weight loss and skin most aggressive.
2. **February (Valentine's Day)** — Couples spa and aesthetics confidence angles dominate.
3. **March (International Women's Day)** — All wellness categories activate female empowerment messaging.
4. **May–June (Summer Ready)** — Aesthetics (laser, body treatments) and slimming peak.
5. **November (Black Friday / Cyber Monday)** — Largest single promotional week of the year for all categories.
6. **December (Christmas)** — Gift voucher and package promotions dominate.
