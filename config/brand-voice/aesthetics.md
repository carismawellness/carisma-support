## Big Ideal
Carisma Aesthetics believes the world would be a better place if people feel confident and comfortable in their own skin.

## Brand Purpose
Our purpose is to help you look and feel confident in your own skin. By personalising cutting-edge technology and advanced medical treatments, we deliver safe and effective results that reveal the best version of yourself.

1. **Expert Medical Team** — Highly skilled and experienced medical professionals ensuring the highest quality of care — safe, effective, and trustworthy.
2. **Seamless Patient Experience** — Simple experience with easy appointment scheduling, online booking, virtual consultations, easy parking, convenient hours, and central locations.
3. **Best-in-Class Technology** — Only the best treatment modalities and brands, incorporating the latest innovations (e.g., Alma lasers).
4. **Deep Personalisation** — Tailoring offers, communications, and services to the unique needs of each individual patient.

## Brand Personality
Graceful · Honest · Natural · Beautiful · Elegant · Reliable · Popular · Modern · Confident

## Core Tone
Warm and reassuring, but with a confident edge.

> "Step into your best self with treatments designed just for you."
> "We're here to make confidence your everyday glow."

## Tone of Voice Guidelines

### DO:
- Use warm, empowering language that invites the audience in
- Emphasise personalisation and natural beauty over drastic transformation
- Blend confidence with grace — sound assured but never arrogant
- Keep it modern and concise, with a touch of elegance ("radiant" vs. "shiny")
- Focus on the patient's journey and experience, not just the outcome

### DON'T:
- Avoid aggressive sales pitches or over-the-top promises
- Steer clear of negative framing ("fixing flaws") — focus on enhancement instead
- Don't lean hard into trendy slang or hype — it risks feeling fleeting rather than reliable
- Avoid generic or impersonal statements — everything should feel tailored and thoughtful

## Target Audience
Women 28–55 in Malta. Results-driven, confidence-seeking. Professionals and mothers who want visible, natural-looking improvement and trust medical credibility.

## Customer Archetypes (for ads and copy)
1. **Reverse signs of aging (45–60)** — Wants to turn back the clock. Has noticed volume loss, deep wrinkles, or significant skin laxity. Motivated by looking as good as she feels inside.
2. **Slow down signs of aging (32–45)** — Proactive about her skin. Starting to see early lines, pigmentation, or texture changes. Wants to maintain her confidence and stay ahead.
3. **Prevent signs of aging (21–32)** — Skin-conscious, educated about aesthetics. Invested in her appearance. Wants professional-grade preventive care.

## Key Treatments
Botox · Dermal fillers · Skin rejuvenation · Laser treatments · HydraFacial · Lip fillers · Fat dissolving · Collagen stimulators · Mesotherapy · PRP (skin + hair) · Microneedling · Chemical peels · Profhilo

## Social Proof Assets
- 150+ 5-star reviews
- "Malta's top rated clinic"
- Award logos and credentials
- Before/after transformations (Day 1 vs Day 30 framing)

## What NOT to Write
- Never say "fix your flaws" or "fix your face" — always "enhance", "reveal", "restore"
- Never use medical terms without empathetic context
- Never promise exact results — frame around individual response
- Never make patients feel broken — they are already beautiful, we help them glow
