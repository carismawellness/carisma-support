# Carisma Spa & Wellness — Brand Voice & Tone of Voice

## Our Story
Since 1990, Carisma Spa & Wellness has provided beauty and wellness treatments to guests from all around the world. Initially starting in Turkey, and in 2010, entering Malta, the Carisma brand has operated in over 50 five-star hotels. Carisma prides itself on offering a holistic range of services, continuously investing in training its ever-growing team, and pioneering cutting-edge innovations in the health and wellness industry. Recognised by the World Luxury Spa Awards for three consecutive years. 99% customer satisfaction rate for over 30 years.

## Tone Qualities

### Peaceful & Soothing
The voice brings a sense of serenity. Every word is selected to calm the reader, like a gentle breeze through a hammam. Nothing rushes. Nothing crowds. The language breathes.

### Charming & Warm
Not distant luxury. Carisma feels approachable, human, and caring — like a trusted therapist who knows exactly how to bring you back to yourself.

### Elegant & Desirable
Without ever being flashy, the brand speaks with refinement. Premium quality is communicated through confidence and grace, never boastfulness.

### Purposeful & Honest
Every phrase has depth and meaning. No fluff or filler — just honest, soul-led language that reflects a deep respect for the guest's wellness journey.

### Relaxed & Balanced
Nothing is rushed or overwhelming. The pace is slow, the message is clear, and the focus is always on helping the guest feel centred.

## Writing Style

**Simple yet poetic** — Short, elegant sentences. Metaphorical when needed, but never abstract for the sake of beauty. Always clear, always grounded.

**Minimal, spacious language** — Leave room for breath. Avoid clutter. Use white space — visually and emotionally.

**Second-person perspective** — Speak directly to "you", inviting the guest into a personal journey.

**Subtle persuasion** — No hard sells. Instead, gently highlight transformation, value, and emotional resonance.

## Target Audience
Women 25–55 in Malta. Wellness-minded, self-care seekers. Typically urban professionals, mothers, or women seeking restorative escapes from demanding daily lives.

## Key Services
Massages · Facials · Body treatments · Spa packages · Couples experiences · Hammam rituals · Sauna · Jacuzzi · Hot stone therapy

## What NOT to Write
- Never use aggressive urgency language ("ACT NOW", "LIMITED TIME ONLY!!!")
- Never be clinical or transactional
- Never use industry jargon without sensory grounding
- Never boast — let the experience speak
- Never rush the reader — every sentence should have space
