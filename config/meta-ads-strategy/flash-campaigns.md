## Flash Sale Structure

Every flash campaign runs in 3 phases. Never launch a flash sale without all 3 phases planned before go-live.

### Phase 1: HYPE (Awareness — Build Anticipation)
- **Goal:** Prime the audience before the offer drops. Create desire before urgency.
- **Duration:** 3–7 days before launch
- **Ad approach:** Tease the offer without revealing exact price. Create curiosity. "Something big is coming…" / "Our biggest offer of the year drops [date]"
- **Audiences:** Warm + retargeting. People who already know the brand.
- **Creative style:** Countdown graphics, teaser video, email capture for early access
- **Copy cue:** "Get on the early access list — 24 hours before everyone else"

### Phase 2: LAUNCH (Offer Goes Live — Peak Push)
- **Goal:** Maximum reach and conversions during the main window.
- **Duration:** Main flash window (typically 3–7 days depending on occasion)
- **Ad approach:** Full BHUGOS copy. Offer front and centre. All creatives live simultaneously.
- **Audiences:** Cold (broad), warm (retargeting), lookalikes
- **Creative style:** All formats — video with results hook, static with price hook, stories with swipe-up urgency
- **Copy cue:** Full offer stack. Perceived value + price + 15 spots + social proof.
- **Budget:** Maximum budget allocation during this phase. This is the peak.

### Phase 3: URGENCY (Final Reminder — Scarcity Plays Out)
- **Goal:** Convert the fence-sitters. Scarcity becomes real.
- **Duration:** Final 24–48 hours of the flash window
- **Ad approach:** Retargeting only. Show the ad to people who engaged, visited, or clicked but did not convert.
- **Audiences:** Hot retargeting only (website visitors, video viewers, lead form openers)
- **Creative style:** Countdown timer graphics, "Last chance" headlines, "Only X spots left" overlays
- **Copy cue:** "Only 3 spots left. The offer closes midnight [date]. This is your last chance."
- **Note:** Do NOT run urgency messaging to cold audiences — it will fall flat and waste spend.

## Flash Campaign Operating Rules

1. **Plan 4 weeks before launch** — Brief creative, copy, and audience segments 4 weeks out.
2. **All 3 phases must be pre-built** — Never launch Phase 1 without Phase 2 and 3 already drafted.
3. **Pause evergreen creatives during flash peak** — Avoid creative competition within the same ad account during Phase 2.
4. **Resume evergreen within 48 hours of flash close** — Do not leave the account dark after a flash ends.
5. **Log learnings after every flash** — CPL, leads, revenue, which creative performed best, what audience responded most. Feed into next year's calendar.
6. **Never fake scarcity** — If you say 15 spots, honour 15 spots. Malta is a small market. Dishonesty compounds negatively.
7. **Schedule all Phase 2 ads at 12AM** — Avoid wasting spend by activating late in the day.
