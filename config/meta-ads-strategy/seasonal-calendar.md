# Carisma Branded Seasonal Calendar
# Meta Ads — Annual Campaign Themes

---

This calendar defines the primary themed campaign angle for each month across all Carisma brands. Use this alongside `flash-campaigns.md` (which covers flash sale occasions) and brand-specific seasonal offers.

Flash campaigns sit ON TOP of this calendar. The seasonal theme is the evergreen angle; the flash campaign is the time-limited promotion layered over it.

## Seasonal Offer Timing Rule

- **Lead with the transformation, not the discount.** The seasonal occasion is the reason to act — the offer is the incentive.
- **Build 4 weeks of creative for every major seasonal moment.** One launch week is not enough.
- **Taper spend after the peak.** Do not abruptly cut budget — taper over 3–5 days to preserve Meta's learning data.
- **Bridge seasons.** The end of one seasonal theme should begin teasing the next. Never leave a "dead week" with no campaign angle.
