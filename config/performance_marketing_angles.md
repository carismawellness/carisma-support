## Copy Structure

### Primary Text (Most Important)
**First 3 lines must be hyper-optimized**

#### 3-Line Hook:
- **Line 1:** Callout ideal customer + Malta
  *Example:* "Summer ready skin for Malta moms"
- **Line 2:** Pain point solved
- **Line 3:** Perceived value + real price + scarcity
  *Example:* "Only 15 spots"

#### Body:
- **Line 1:** Agitate the pain point further and the benefits
- **Line 2:** What is included in the package (bullet points or emojis)
- **Line 3:** Perceived value + Price + Scarcity
- **Line 4:** Social proof (reviews, case studies, helped x # of clients)
- **Line 5:** CTA

#### Disclaimer:
"Spots for this offer are limited to the first 15 available spots (if you're still reading this, that means we may still have a spot available!)"

#### Contact:
Phone + Address

### Headline (Choose One):
- "Only 15 spots"
- "[LIMITED] Only €99"

## Copy Rules

### ✅ DO:
- Don't use generic words — focus on **specific issues** they're solving for
- Copy must focus on the **prospect, not the business**
- Focus on **benefits for the customer**
- Be **super specific** and focus on **PAIN POINTS**

### ✅ ALWAYS ADD:
- **Case studies** (before & afters)
- **Scarcity** (only 15 spots)
- **Social proof** (awards, #1 voted clinic, 150+ 5-star reviews, Malta's top-rated clinic)

## Ad Structure (Works for Both Video & Static)

### 1. Pattern Interrupt (First 2–3 Seconds)
- A surprising stat: "Did you know your jawline starts sagging at 25?"
- A striking before/after transformation
- Practitioner holding a device saying: "This is how we reverse 5 years of aging"

### 2. Clear Problem & Relatability
- "If you've tried creams and nothing worked…"
- "Many women notice this after 30…"

### 3. Social Proof / Transformation
- Side-by-side before/after photos
- Short testimonial: "It's the only thing that worked for my [problem]"

### 4. Authority Anchor
- Award logo, clinic exterior, practitioner credentials
- "Rated 5★ by 150+ clients in Malta"

### 5. Offer & Urgency
- "Book your free consultation this week and save €50 on your first session"

### 6. Simple Call-to-Action
- "Tap below to book instantly"

## How to Keep Creatives Fresh Without Redoing Everything

- Add **seasonal wrappers** to the creatives to make them feel contextually relevant
- **Rotate intros/hooks** every 1-2 weeks but keep the core body identical
- Swap out 1–2 **before/after photos** for new client results
- Alternate **offer wording** (€50 off vs. free add-on)
- Change **background music or captions styling** for fresh feel

## Customer Psychology

### Triggers
- Photo that "embarrassed" them
- A clothing fit crisis
- A health checkup that scared them
- Typically they've just tried and failed with diets (keto, shakes, gym-only plans) and feel fed up and hopeless
- A health scare (diabetes diagnosis)
- Milestone events (wedding, graduation, reunions)
- Life changes (new baby, job stress)
- Emotional tipping points ("I hit rock bottom")

### Avatars
- **Bride-to-be**
- **Menopause**
- **Busy mom**
- **Obese**

### Angles
- Fit into attractive clothes
- Health
- Partner attraction
- **Social anxiety:** Eat socially without guilt; The dread of being tagged in a Facebook photo at a wedding or BBQ
- **Food Freedom:** Lose inches eating favorite food

### Hidden Emotions
- **Shame:** The avatar engages in active avoidance behaviors — dodging mirrors, deleting photos where they dislike their angle, and feeling anxiety about social events where food is central
- Fear of health issues in future
- Fear of losing partner attraction
- Need for acceptance and fitting in

### Pain
Everyday struggles include:
- Avoiding cameras or mirrors
- Self-consciousness and low energy
- Common issues: breathlessness with simple tasks ("hard time getting in and out of my car")
- Joint pain, poor sleep, and embarrassment in clothes (e.g., tight waistbands)
- They hide behind baggy clothes or skip social events (one said she "never wanted to go out or try on new clothes")
- Grocery shopping or cooking can be stress points ("What's safe to eat?")
- Food cravings and feeling hungry/unsatisfied due to past yo-yo dieting intensify guilt and frustration

### Key Desires
- Fit into attractive clothes
- Eat socially without guilt
- Become active (kids/play/exercise)
- Improve medical markers
