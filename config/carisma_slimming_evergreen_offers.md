## Campaign Execution Notes

### Scarcity Mechanisms
All offers include "limited weekly capacity" and "small number of slots" language. This is:
- ✅ Truthful (doctor/equipment availability is genuinely limited)
- ✅ Creates urgency without false scarcity
- ✅ Positions service as exclusive/in-demand

### Medical Gatekeeping
"Suitability subject to Dr approval" serves multiple purposes:
- ✅ Builds credibility (medical standard, not cash grab)
- ✅ Legal protection (not everyone is suitable)
- ✅ Qualification mechanism (serious buyers only)
- ✅ Reduces refund/dissatisfaction risk

### Multi-Touchpoint Strategy
Spa & fitness access (4x) included in every package ensures:
- Client returns to facility multiple times
- Opportunity for relationship building
- Upsell conversations during visits
- Higher lifetime value capture

### Aesthetics Cross-Sell
€25 Carisma Aesthetics credit bridges slimming → aesthetics services:
- Botox, fillers, skin treatments
- Higher ticket services
- Expands customer lifetime value
