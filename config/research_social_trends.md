## Executive Summary

Weight loss conversations in 2026 are dominated by three themes: **GLP-1 medication concerns** (Ozempic/Wegovy cost, side effects, weight regain after stopping), **metabolic health frustration** ("nothing works anymore"), and **community-driven accountability**. The shift from calorie counting to metabolic reset is complete. People want **sustainable solutions that fit their lives**, not extreme protocols. Emotional drivers: validation for past failures, relief from yo-yo cycling, permission to invest in themselves, and excitement about life quality (not just scale numbers).
