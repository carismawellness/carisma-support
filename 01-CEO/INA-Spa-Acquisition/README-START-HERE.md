## 🔑 Key Scripts (Memorize These)

### **Your Opening Line**
> "Based on what the business is generating today and the investment needed to bring underutilized spaces up — I'm looking at this in the range of €40,000 to €50,000. Let me walk you through how I got there..."

### **Then: SILENCE**
Count to 10. Do not fill. Let him respond.

### **Your Sunk Cost Redirect (When He Says "I Put €1M Into This")**
> "I completely understand. You put in real capital, real work, real risk. But the market asks one question: What does this business earn TODAY? Not what it cost to build. Not what it could earn. What it earns right now. That's acquisition pricing.
>
> The €800K difference between what you invested and fair market value — that loss happened whether you sell today or wait. Selling doesn't create the loss. Selling stops the losses from continuing."

### **Your Power Phrases (3 Tactical Moves)**
1. Mirror: "Revenue dropped 40%?" [silence]
2. Label: "It sounds like this situation has taken a real toll..." [silence]
3. Reframe: "How am I supposed to make that work at the current revenue level?" [curious, not aggressive]

### **Your Exit Line (If Far Apart)**
> "I appreciate the time. I'm serious about this, but only works if numbers make sense. Right now they don't. If things shift, I'm here. But I won't stretch on price. I'll move on calmly, and so should you."
