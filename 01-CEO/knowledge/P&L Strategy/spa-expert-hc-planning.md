## 2. Seasonal Pattern

- **Peak (Aug-Oct):** +40% vs trough. Invest in flex staff.
- **Trough (Dec-Jan):** Fixed costs hurt most. Core team only.
- **Highest variance:** Odyssey (2.61x), Labranda (2.32x), Ramla (2.28x)
- **Flattest:** Hyatt (1.47x), Excelsior (1.35x)

## 3. Three-Layer Staffing Model

| Layer | Description | Coverage |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Core** (fixed, permanent) | Best therapists, sized for trough month at 35% | 70% |
| **Flex Pool** (shared, central) | 4-6 therapists deployed weekly by Ops Director | 20% |
| **Seasonal** (Jul-Oct contracts) | Recruited May, trained June, deployed July | 10% |
