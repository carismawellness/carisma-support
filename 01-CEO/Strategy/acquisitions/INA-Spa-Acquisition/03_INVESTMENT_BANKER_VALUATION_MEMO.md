## I. VALUATION METHODOLOGY FRAMEWORK

### Multiple Approaches (Triangulation)

**1. Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)**
- Review the 16-sheet enhanced model
- Validate key assumptions (growth, margins, capex, tax)
- Run DCF sensitivity at discount rates: 15%, 18%, 20%, 25%
- Calculate enterprise value for Bear/Base/Bull
- Produce valuation range (low, midpoint, high)

**2. Comparable Company Analysis (Trading Multiples)**
- Identify public/quasi-public European wellness operators
- Extract EV/EBITDA, EV/Revenue, P/E multiples
- Apply multiples to INA (Year 1 and normalized Year 3 EBITDA)
- Adjust for size, growth, profitability, risk (illiquidity discount)
- Produce implied valuation range

**3. Precedent Transactions**
- Spa/wellness acquisitions in Southern Europe (2020-2025)
- Extract transaction multiples
- Assess whether INA is premium, market, or discount
- Identify deal patterns (what buyers typically pay, when deals fail, etc.)

**4. Sum-of-Parts Valuation**
- Value spa business separately from clinic platform
- Spa: EUR 72K EBITDA at 4-5x multiple
- Clinic platform: Buyer-created value (not in seller's current EBITDA)
- Equipment value: EUR 150-200K FMV
- Lease value (13-year location premium): EUR 50-100K

**5. Return-Based Valuation (IRR Approach)**
- Define buyer's target IRR: 20% (typical for mid-market healthcare)
- What purchase price achieves 20% IRR in Base case?
- What purchase price achieves 20% IRR in Bear case?
- This is a ceiling on what buyer should pay

- At EUR 330K: Base case delivers ~20% IRR ✓
- At EUR 350K: Base case delivers ~18% IRR ✓
- At EUR 380K: Base case delivers ~15% IRR ✓

## VIII. DEAL STRUCTURE & PRICING RECOMMENDATION

### Opening Offer: EUR 280K (3.9x EBITDA)

**Rationale:**
- Below median (EUR 380K), justified by risk discount
- Leaves room to move through 3-4 Ackermann moves
- Shows you are serious but prices spa for current operations only
- Anchors negotiation at market bottom (precedent transactions)

**Expected seller response:** Rejection with EUR 450-500K counteroffer

### Target Price: EUR 340-360K (4.7-5.0x EBITDA)

**Rationale:**
- Fair market value for spa (EUR 288K at 4x EBITDA) + lease premium (EUR 40K) + execution risk premium (EUR 30-50K)
- Supports clinic platform investment (EUR 150K capex) without requiring clinic success for breakeven
- Base case IRR of 22-24% provides adequate margin over 18% hurdle
- Typical landing zone in M&A (60-70% of distance from opening to seller's initial demand)

**Recommended structure:**
- EUR 300-320K cash at close
- EUR 30-40K earnout (12 months, contingent on spa maintaining EUR 25K+/month)
- 90-day transition with seller cooperation included

### Walk-Away Maximum: EUR 420K (5.8x EBITDA)

**Rationale:**
- At EUR 420K, Base case IRR falls to ~12% (equals cost of capital, zero margin)
- Bear case (30% probability) produces 8-10% IRR (unacceptable)
- Deal becomes speculation on clinic upside, not disciplined acquisition
- Better alternatives for capital deployment

**Signal:** If seller demands EUR 420K+, politely decline and walk away. Do not hint at higher willingness.

## IX. INVESTMENT RECOMMENDATION & CONCLUSION

### Buy or Pass?

**Recommendation: PROCEED at EUR 340K + EUR 30K earnout**

**Strategic fit:** Good add-on for multi-location wellness platform. Provides clinic launch platform with lower execution risk than greenfield.

**Financial fit:** 28-31% probability-weighted IRR exceeds 18% hurdle with meaningful margin. Risk-adjusted returns are attractive.

**Risk/reward:** Balanced. Downside protected (even Bear case produces 2.89x MOIC). Upside significant (Bull case produces 17.7x MOIC).

**Market timing:** Good market, post-COVID recovery stable. Need to validate tourism recovery trajectory.

### Price Guidance Summary

| Price | IRR (Base) | Assessment | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|
| EUR 280K | 36% | Exceptional value | **OPENING** |

### Final Verdict

This valuation analysis supports proceeding with acquisition at EUR 340K target price. The deal is neither a "steal" nor a premium — it is fair market value for a proven business with genuine platform optionality. The clinic is where 70-80% of returns live, and Carisma's existing capabilities de-risk clinic execution substantially.

**Negotiation discipline is critical:** Anchor at EUR 280K, expect to land at EUR 340-360K through structured Ackermann moves, and walk away at EUR 420K without further discussion.
