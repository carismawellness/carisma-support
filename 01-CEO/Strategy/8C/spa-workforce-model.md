## Stop Leave Calendar

| Period | Policy | Staff Impact |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Jul 1 – Oct 31** | BLACKOUT — no planned leave | All staff on deck. Flex pool 100% demand-driven. |
| **Dec 20 – Jan 2** | BLACKOUT — festive period | Skeleton crew + flex. |
| **Easter week** | BLACKOUT | Full team. |
| **Jan 5 – Feb 15** | FORCED — minimum 5 days per person | Right-sizes team to trough demand. |
| **Jun 1 – Jun 14** | ENCOURAGED — approve generously | Pre-summer dip, low demand. |
| **Nov 15 – Dec 15** | ENCOURAGED | Post-peak wind-down. |
| Rest of year | OPEN — standard approval process | Max 2 people per location on leave simultaneously. |

## Career Ladder

### Clinical Track
```
Spa Helper → [get certified] → Therapist (hourly trial) → Therapist (salaried) → Senior Therapist → Rotational Senior → Regional Manager → Ops Director
   €950        3 months          €1,450                     €1,550               €1,700             €2,200-2,500      €5,000
```

### Non-Clinical Track
```
Spa Helper → Concierge → Senior Advisor → Rotational Senior (non-clinical) → Regional Manager
   €950       €1,400      €1,550          €1,700                               €2,200+
```

**Promotion criteria:**
- Helper → Therapist: Complete ITEC/CIDESCO certification
- Therapist (hourly) → Therapist (salaried): 3-month trial passed, reliable, quality confirmed
- Therapist → Senior: 12+ months, can train others, consistent quality scores
- Senior → Rotational Senior: 18+ months, demonstrated leadership, willing to rotate
- Rotational Senior → Regional Manager: 24+ months, P&L awareness, hotel relationship skills
