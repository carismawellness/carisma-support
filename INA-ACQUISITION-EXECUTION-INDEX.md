## The Meeting Flow (How to Use These Documents)

### **Before Meeting (30 min prior)**
1. Read **INA-MEETING-QUICK-REFERENCE.txt** — memorize the mindset frames
2. Skim **INA-NEGOTIATION-BRIEFING.md** Act 1 section — know your five questions
3. Print: One-page P&L from **INA-Spa-Consolidated-Model.xlsx** (Sheet 2, Annual Summary)
4. Print: Due diligence checklist to hand over
5. Bring: Notebook (show you're serious)

### **During Meeting (90 minutes)**

**ACT 1 (20-30 min) — Intelligence Gathering:**
- Reference: **INA-MEETING-QUICK-REFERENCE.txt** (The Five Questions section)
- Keep open: **INA-NEGOTIATION-BRIEFING.md** Act 1 (your full scripts)
- Goal: Extract rent level, revenue reality, his timeline, competition
- Tactical: Ask questions in order. Mirror + label. Hold silence.

**ACT 2 (20-30 min) — Reframe Value:**
- Reference: **INA-NEGOTIATION-BRIEFING.md** Act 2 (The Reframe section)
- Show: One-page P&L from the Consolidated Model
- Sunk Cost Script: "€1M loss is already gone. Selling stops further losses."
- Equipment Move: "Let's get third-party appraisal. Let market decide."

**ACT 3 (20-30 min) — The Offer:**
- Reference: **INA-MEETING-QUICK-REFERENCE.txt** (The Offer section)
- Open with: "€40-50K based on current revenue and equipment value"
- SILENCE. Do not fill. Count to 10.
- His response determines your next move (all scripted in **INA-NEGOTIATION-BRIEFING.md**)
- Use Ackermann sequence conditionally (don't lead with numbers)

**ACT 4 (10-15 min) — Close or Exit:**
- Trial close (if positive): "Sound like €100-120K lands the deal?"
- If yes: "Not closing today. Due diligence first. Fair?"
- If far apart: "Different valuations. My team will analyze. I'll be in touch."
- Hand over: Due diligence checklist

### **After Meeting (Same Day)**
- Do NOT email
- Do NOT propose new number
- Decompress. Your silence is power.

### **24-48 Hours Later**
- Brief email (3 sentences): "Thanks for time. Team pulling due diligence. Touch base early next week."
- Do NOT re-negotiate
- Let him think about your offer

### **48-72 Hours Later**
- Formal due diligence request (via email, make it official)
- Hand to solicitor / accountant
- Begin parallel vetting

### **Second Meeting (7-10 days)**
- Bring: Accountant or solicitor
- Reference: **INA-NEGOTIATION-BRIEFING.md** (now you have verified info)
- Negotiate again with new power (information validated)
