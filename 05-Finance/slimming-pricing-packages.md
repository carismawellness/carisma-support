## Extended Care Commitment (Guarantee Liability)

### How It Works
If qualifying client completes program but doesn't hit target, program extends at no extra fee.

### Targets
| Duration | Weight Loss Target |
|----------|-------------------|
| 6 weeks | 5 kg |
| 12 weeks | 10 kg |
| 20 weeks | 20 kg |

### Conditions (Must ALL Be Met)
1. Attend all scheduled sessions and weekly check-ins
2. Follow personalised food plan consistently
3. Complete agreed physical activities
4. Use only recommended treatments/medications
5. Inform of health or medication changes
6. No crash diets or outside weight loss treatments

### Financial Implication
- Selective enrollment reduces guarantee trigger risk
- "We only accept those we genuinely believe we can help"
- Limited number of guaranteed programs per month
