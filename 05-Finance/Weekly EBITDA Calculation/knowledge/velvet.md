# Velvet

## Overview

Velvet is a separate business unit tracked as its own EBITDA line in the Group total. It operates independently from the Spa and Aesthetics divisions.

## Current Status

**Dormant** — showing €0 EBITDA since November 2025.

## P&L Structure

Simple: EBITDA and EBITDA % tracked monthly. When active:
- Revenue, costs, and EBITDA are reported as a single line
- Historical EBITDA % ranged from 49% to 86% when operational
- Peak performance: Jun-Aug 2025 (€4,455–€7,533 EBITDA at 78–86%)

## In EBITDA Calculation

When calculating Group EBITDA:
- If Velvet has data for the period: include in Group Total
- If Velvet shows 0 or no data: include as €0 (do not skip the line)
- Velvet EBITDA is always shown separately before being added to Group
