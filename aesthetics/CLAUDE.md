## Brand Voice (Carisma Aesthetics)

**Persona:** Sarah — Clinical Expert with warmth
**Signature:** "Beautifully yours, Sarah"

**Essence:** Confident, warm, direct. A skilled professional who genuinely cares about your journey. Not clinical or cold. Not salesy or flashy. Like a trusted expert friend who speaks honestly and makes you feel safe.

**Key Phrases:**
- "Glow with Confidence"
- "Reveal the best version of yourself"
- "Your results. Your timeline. Your confidence."
- "We're here to enhance what's already beautiful."
- "Modern treatments. Timeless results."
- "Radiant skin. Quiet confidence. A moment just for you."
- "Results you'll notice. Results that last. Results that are yours."

### DO's
1. **Use warm, empowering language** — Invite the patient into confidence, not perfection
2. **Emphasize personalization** — Every treatment tailored to individual goals
3. **Focus on natural beauty and enhancement** — Never "fixing flaws," always "enhancing what's already there"
4. **Blend confidence with grace** — Quiet expertise, not bravado
5. **Keep it modern and concise** — Short, powerful sentences that move forward

### DON'Ts
1. **Never use aggressive sales or urgency** — No "Book now!", "Limited-time!", "Don't miss out!"
2. **Never frame beauty as "fixing flaws"** — No "erase wrinkles," "fix problem skin," "get rid of"
3. **Never use clinical jargon in patient-facing copy** — No "selective photothermolysis," "controlled thermal injury"
4. **Never use trendy slang** — No "slay," "glow up bestie," "literally life-changing"
5. **Never make unrealistic promises** — No "guaranteed results," "perfect skin," "erase all wrinkles"

### North Star
> "Will this message help a patient feel confident, informed, safe, and gently invited to enhance their natural beauty?"

## Target Audience

### Primary Archetypes
1. **First-Timer Nervous** — Safety concerns, fear of looking fake. Needs reassurance and a conservative plan. Gentle education is key.
2. **Injectable Regular** — Values consistency, technique, naturalness, convenience. Relationship-focused. Membership candidate.
3. **Skin Concern Seeker** — Acne, texture, pigmentation. Prefers "skin health" framing. Open to treatment courses.
4. **Anti-Aging Subtle Restoration** — Tired look, sagging, fine lines. Responds to "refreshed, rested, lifted" language.
5. **Bridal / Event-Driven** — Has a deadline. Wants glow and camera confidence. High anxiety about downtime. Needs clear timeline.
6. **"Fix One Thing" Micro-Goal** — Lip flip, gummy smile, chin dimpling, jawline. Knows exactly what they want. Quick conversion.
7. **Male Discreet Client** — Wants subtle results, privacy, minimal downtime, efficiency. Do not over-explain or over-sell.

### Age Bands & Typical Interests
| Age | Common Interests |
|-----|-----------------|
| 20-28 | Lips, skin quality, preventative anti-wrinkle, event glow |
| 28-40 | "Tired look," early aging, refined enhancements, skin quality |
| 40-55 | Restoration, collagen support, lifting and skin tightening, courses |
| 55+ | Conservative restoration plans, longer-term pacing, trust-heavy journey |

### Demographics
- **Age:** 25-65 (core: mid-20s to mid-40s, plus older anti-aging clients)
- **Gender:** Predominantly women, meaningful male segment
- **Location:** Malta residents and expats
- **Segments:** "Look fresher" professionals, skin quality seekers, event-driven, maintenance clients
- **Psychographics:** Wants natural/tasteful/premium results. High trust needs. Values convenience and reassurance.
- **Pain points:** Aging concerns, skin confidence, body concerns, desire for enhancement without looking "done"

### Price Sensitivity Bands
- **Low sensitivity:** Maintenance clients, expats (focus on experience and results)
- **Medium sensitivity:** First-timers, skin course clients (focus on value and outcomes)
- **High sensitivity:** Laser hair removal shoppers, deal hunters (focus on packages and entry pricing)
