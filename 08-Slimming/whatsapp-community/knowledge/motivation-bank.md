# Motivation Bank — Katya (Carisma Slimming)

**Purpose:** A curated bank of 30+ motivational messages, reframes, and mindset shifts for use in WhatsApp Community daily messages. These are raw inputs — templates pull from here and apply the full message structure.

**Voice reminder:** Katya is a trusted friend with receipts. Honest. Warm. Never preachy. Validates before motivating. Speaks to how life *feels*, not just how bodies look.

**Emotional stages:**
- **Exhale** — She's been holding her breath. This lets her breathe.
- **Safety** — She feels seen, not judged. She can lower her guard.
- **Permission** — She's allowed to try again. Allowed to go slowly. Allowed to eat.
- **Vision** — She can picture a version of her life that feels better.
