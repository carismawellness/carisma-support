## Call Script (Inbound/Outbound)

### Step 1: Greet & Confirm Caller
**What to say:** "Hi, this is Sarah calling from Carisma Spa. Is this [name]?"
**CRM Action:** Confirm name & phone number
**Scoring note:** Must use brand name "Carisma Spa", must ask to confirm caller by name. SDR should use their own name.

### Step 2: Confirm Call & Treatment History
**What to say:** "Perfect, I'm responding to your request to learn more about [Spa packages] have you been to any of our spas before?"
**CRM Action:** Note if client is new/experienced
**Scoring note:** Must reference the specific package/offer the lead enquired about. Must ask if they have visited before to gauge familiarity.

### Step 3: Ask WHY?
**What to say:** "What is it that interests you in [insert their interested package] today?"
**CRM Action:** Note main concern in CRM
**Scoring note:** Must ask an open-ended "why" question to understand the client's motivation. This drives the rest of the conversation.

### Step 4: Explain the Value
**What to say:** "I completely get it! Many of our clients visit us with [reiterate their interest they mentioned]; This is perfect timing because the [Insert offer name] seems like could be really good fit for you;

Your experience will first begin with a [insert benefits].

You'll also receive [insert additional]

Finally, you'll also get [bonus 1, bonus 2, etc] to complete your treatment

How does this sound?"
**CRM Action:** Confirm interest
**Scoring note:** Must reiterate the client's stated interest. Must walk through the full package structure: main experience, additional inclusions, bonuses. Must end with a soft close ("How does this sound?").

### Step 5: Check Location
**What to say:** "We've got 8 spas around the island which area works best for you?"
**CRM Action:** Book slot in calendar
**Scoring note:** Must mention the number of locations (8 spas). Must ask which area is most convenient. This is unique to Spa -- Aesthetics and Slimming have single locations.

### Step 6a: Book Appointment
**What to say:** "What day works best for you? - great I've got [offer timing 2 options]"
**CRM Action:** Book slot in calendar
**Scoring note:** Must ask for preferred day. Must offer 2 specific time options to make it easy for the client to choose.

### Step 6b: Get Deposit
**What to say:** "Wonderful! So just one last thing -- to get locked into our calendar with the savings, we take a [50 deposit] to reserve your spot.

You can either just tell me your card number and I can take care of it for you! Or if you don't feel comfortable with that (which I totally understand), I can send you a link to your phone, and you can do it yourself!

What works better for you [insert their name]?"
**CRM Action:** --
**Scoring note:** Must mention the deposit amount (50). Must offer TWO payment options (card over the phone OR payment link). Must normalize discomfort with card-over-phone ("which I totally understand"). Must personalize by using the client's name.

### Step 7: Confirm Time & Details
**What to say:** "Great -- I've booked you for [Day, Time]."
**CRM Action:** Collect details: email, DOB, address, etc.
**Scoring note:** Must confirm the booked day and time back to the client. Must collect personal details for CRM.

### Step 8: Send Confirmation
**What to say:** "You'll receive a confirmation text now with your appointment, our address, and contact info. Feel free to reach out anytime!"
**CRM Action:** Send confirmation text & CRM reminder
**Scoring note:** Must confirm that a text is being sent with appointment, address, and contact info.

### Step 9: Offer Further Assistance
**What to say:** "Is there anything else I can assist you with?"
**CRM Action:** --
**Scoring note:** Must ask if there is anything else they need before ending the call.

### Step 10: Close Warmly
**What to say:** "You're in great hands -- we're excited to meet you and get you started on your journey with us!"
**CRM Action:** Add call notes in CRM
**Scoring note:** Must close warmly. Must add call notes to CRM after the call.

## Script Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Used correct greeting with brand name ("Carisma Spa")
- [ ] Confirmed caller identity by name
- [ ] Asked about previous spa visits / experience
- [ ] Asked WHY they are interested (open-ended motivation question)
- [ ] Explained value proposition with full package walkthrough
- [ ] Soft-closed with "How does this sound?"
- [ ] Asked which of the 8 spa locations works best
- [ ] Offered 2 specific time options for booking
- [ ] Collected deposit (50) with choice of card-over-phone or payment link
- [ ] Confirmed appointment details (day, time)
- [ ] Collected personal details (email, DOB, etc.)
- [ ] Sent confirmation text
- [ ] Offered further assistance
- [ ] Closed warmly
- [ ] Added call notes in CRM
