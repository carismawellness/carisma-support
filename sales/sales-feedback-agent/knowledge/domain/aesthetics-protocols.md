## Factual Claims Reps Must Get Right

### Critical Facts (Incorrect = Major Penalty)

1. **Botox results timeline:** 3-7 days to show, full results at 2 weeks, lasts 3-4 months
2. **Filler results:** Immediate, but final results once swelling subsides (2-4 weeks)
3. **Skin rejuvenation timeline:** 2-4 weeks with series of treatments
4. **Body contouring results:** 4-12 weeks as body processes treated areas
5. **Cancellation window:** 24 hours free. Under 24 hours = fee equal to treatment price
6. **No-show fee:** 50% of treatment price
7. **Gift card validity:** 12 months (not unlimited)
8. **Never guarantee specific results** -- always qualify with "results vary"
9. **Never diagnose** -- always recommend consultation for medical questions
10. **Member vs Regular pricing:** Always clarify which rate applies

### Treatment Safety Claims (Incorrect = Critical Penalty)

1. **Botox is NOT permanent** -- lasts 3-4 months
2. **Fillers are NOT permanent** -- typically last 6-18 months depending on product
3. **Chemical peels require downtime** -- do not promise zero recovery
4. **PRP uses patient's own blood** -- not synthetic
5. **Consultation required** for all injectable treatments before first session
6. **Patch test required** before laser hair removal
7. **Pregnancy contraindication** -- most aesthetic treatments not suitable during pregnancy

### Pricing Claims (Incorrect = Major Penalty)

1. **Never quote a price not in the system** -- check member vs regular rate
2. **Never promise discounts** unless active promotion exists
3. **Multi-session packages exist** -- always mention when relevant (saves money)
4. **Member rates require membership** -- do not give member pricing to non-members
5. **Price ranges exist** because different product brands have different costs

## Response Protocol (For Scoring Reference)

### The 8 Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **Answer first, immediately** -- factual questions get one-sentence answers
2. **Max 2-3 sentences per response**
3. **One question only** per message
4. **No bullet lists for questions**
5. **Handle it directly** -- never refer elsewhere
6. **Skip diagnostic when customer is specific**
7. **1-2 emoji max**
8. **Warm but brief**

### Forbidden Language

- "Certainly!", "I'd be happy to", "Of course!", "Absolutely!", "Great question!"
- "Fix", "flaws", "perfect", "guaranteed"
- Clinical jargon without explanation
- Em-dashes (use periods)
- Pressure language ("Book now!", "Don't miss out", "Limited time")

### Aesthetics-Specific Language Rules

- Say "enhance" not "fix"
- Say "natural-looking results" not "perfection"
- Say "smoother" not "wrinkle-free"
- Say "refreshed" not "younger"
- Never promise permanent results for non-permanent treatments
