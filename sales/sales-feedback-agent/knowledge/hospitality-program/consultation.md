# Consultation — Hospitality Program Knowledge

> Source: Astra Culture Hospitality Program / Consultation folder

## Overview

The consultation is the critical conversion moment. It transforms a curious visitor into a committed patient. The approach is built on active listening, structured discovery, and the Good/Better/Best (GBB) framework for presenting treatment options.

## Consultation Flow (from Steps of Service Guide)

### Step 4: The Consultation
1. **Active Listening First** — Let the patient describe their concerns fully before offering solutions
2. **Open-Ended Questions** — Use discovery questions that explore the emotional and practical dimensions of their concern
3. **Investment Frame** — Position pricing as an investment in themselves, never a cost
4. **Good/Better/Best Programs** — Always present three tiers (see GBB Playbook below)
5. **PROOF Visuals** — Show before/after photos, scan results, or diagnostic imaging to build confidence

### Key Consultation Principles
- START WITH THEM — their story, their concerns, their goals
- SHOW PROOF — before/after images, diagnostic scans, testimonials
- EXPLAIN SIMPLY — avoid medical jargon, use clear language
- INVITE NEXT STEP — always close with a clear call to action

## Good/Better/Best (GBB) Playbook

### The Formula
- **Good** = Anchor Driver — One proven, results-driven treatment that stands alone. Strong enough to make the package credible.
- **Better** = Anchor + Supportive Boosters — Add 1-2 adjuncts that amplify results and create completeness. "Layered in value."
- **Best** = Anchor + Boosters + Elite Layer + Status — Advanced treatments, stacked therapies, exclusive add-ons. Includes status elements like VIP access, ongoing consults, or unique therapies.

### Avatar Match
Define the patient type by story, pain point, and identity. Make the provider able to spot them in consult immediately.

### Lab/Proof Tie-In
Connect biomarkers or diagnostics directly to the package recommendation.

### Med Spa Package Examples

**Skin Rejuvenation (Smooth and Radiating Skin Program)**
- Good: Exion RF Microneedling series (Buy 3 get 1 Free) + Follow-up Scan
- Better: Exion RF Microneedling + Exion Face + skincare kit + Follow-up Scan
- Best: Exion RF Microneedling + Exion Face + PRP/Exosomes + VIP skincare membership + Follow-up Scan
- Avatar: "I want my skin to look younger, smoother, and healthier."
- Proof: VISIA or Aura imaging or collagen scan

**Body Contouring (The Sculpted Professional)**
- Good: Emsculpt Neo series (core or glutes)
- Better: Emsculpt Neo + Exion Body tightening + vitamin injections
- Best: Emsculpt Neo + Exion Body + IV therapy (NAD+) + VIP maintenance plan
- Avatar: "I work out, but I want faster, visible results."
- Proof: InBody scan (muscle vs fat), Styku, Fit 3D

**Confidence and Feminine Wellness (The Empowered Woman)**
- Good: Emsella (pelvic floor therapy)
- Better: Emsella + EmFemme 360 + vitamin/IV support
- Best: Emsella + EmFemme 360 + PRP (O-Shot) + hormone panel + consults
- Avatar: "I want confidence back in intimacy and daily life."
- Proof: Hormone panel, lifestyle screening

### How Providers Use the Playbook
1. **Listen for the Avatar's Story** — Match patient language to package pathway
2. **Show Lab/Proof to Package Pathway** — "Your scan shows X — that's why our Y Package is right for you."
3. **Position Packages as Tiers** — Good = foundational fix, Better = fix + acceleration, Best = transformation + exclusivity

## Open House Room Micro Presentation (5-7 minutes)

For events and open houses, a structured mini-presentation format:

1. **Hook + Identity** (30-60 sec) — Open with a relatable question or bold stat. "Have you ever..." or "Did you know that..."
2. **Concerns and Outcomes First** (2 min) — Lead with what the patient cares about, not the device name
3. **Story + Before/After** (60-90 sec) — Show one strong patient transformation
4. **Why We Invested** (45-60 sec) — Explain why the clinic chose this technology
5. **How It Works** (45-60 sec) — Simple, jargon-free explanation
6. **Who It's For + CTA** (60-90 sec) — Define the ideal candidate and invite the next step
