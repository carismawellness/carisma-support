## Selection Logic

### How the agent selects content for each section:

**Recurring patterns to improve:** Rank all improvement areas from per-conversation reports by frequency. Take the top 3 that appeared in 2+ conversations. If fewer than 3 recur, fill with the highest-impact single-occurrence issues.

**Consistent strengths:** Rank all strengths from per-conversation reports by frequency. Take the top 3 that appeared in 2+ conversations.

**Focus area:** Pick the single dimension with the lowest average score, weighted by trend (declining dimensions get priority over stable low dimensions). The focus area should be specific enough to practice in one week.

**Quick wins:** Identify behaviors that (a) appeared as improvement areas, (b) require minimal effort to fix (adding a closing question, using a name, etc.), and (c) would measurably improve scores. Maximum 2.

**Trajectory:** Compare 3-week rolling averages. Improving = 3+ point increase over 3 weeks. Declining = 3+ point decrease. Stable = everything else.

---

## Delivery Rules

1. Sent every Monday at 8:00 AM Malta time.
2. Delivered via email to the rep and their manager.
3. A condensed WhatsApp version (score + trajectory + focus area + quick wins only) is also sent to the rep.
4. If a rep had 0 scored conversations that week, no report is generated. Instead, a brief note is sent: "No conversations were scored this week. If you had conversations that weren't captured, let your manager know."
5. If a rep's trajectory is "Declining" for 2+ consecutive weeks, the manager receives a separate flag.
