## What Claude Does With Seasonal Promotions

### Auto-Check (On Every Customer Message)
1. Claude reads today's date
2. Claude compares to "Valid Until" date in this file
3. If offer is expired → Claude does NOT pitch it
4. If offer is active → Claude may pitch when relevant

### When Pitching an Offer
Claude uses this framework:
1. **Validate customer need** (diagnose first, always)
2. **Paint the value** (what they'll experience)
3. **Mention the offer** (as bonus, not primary hook)
4. **Soft close** (invitation, not pressure)

**Example pitch (Carisma voice):**
"Since you're thinking about a romantic escape, we have something special happening through the end of February — a couple's experience with dinner and champagne, designed for moments just like this. [price]. Shall I check what's available for you?"

## Promotion Testing Framework

**For each promotion, test:**

✅ **Pitch success rate** — What % of customers accept when offered?
✅ **Objection patterns** — What resistance do we see?
✅ **Customer segment fit** — Who says yes most often?
✅ **Timing** — When in conversation is best moment to pitch?
✅ **Learning** — What did we learn for next time?

## Promotion Rules

### Rule 1: Never Lead With Price
❌ WRONG: "We're having a sale — €150 for €120"
✅ RIGHT: "We're creating a special couples experience through February with dinner and champagne — here's what that looks like... it's €120"

### Rule 2: One Offer Per Conversation
Don't overwhelm customer with multiple promotions. Pick the most relevant one.

### Rule 3: Archive Old Campaigns
When campaign ends, move to "Previous Campaign" section with ❌ EXPIRED clearly marked.

### Rule 4: Update Daily During Live Campaigns
If customer questions the offer validity or timing, you need accurate information immediately.
