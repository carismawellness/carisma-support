# Template: Booking Reminder (24 Hours Before)

**When to Use:** 24 hours before customer's booked appointment
**Channel:** WhatsApp (preferred), Email, SMS
**Tone:** Warm, gentle reminder, building anticipation

---

## Template Text

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

Tomorrow at this time, you'll be with us.

**[TREATMENT_NAME]**
📅 [DATE] at [TIME]
📍 [LOCATION_NAME]

A few gentle reminders:
- Arrive [ARRIVAL_MINUTES] minutes early
- Wear comfortable clothes (we provide everything else)
- [IF_FIRST_TIME: There's nothing to prepare. Just come as you are.]
- [IF_FOOD_RESTRICTION: Please let us know about any dietary needs if you've added lunch]

**Parking:** [PARKING_INFO]
**Address:** [ADDRESS]

If something's come up and you need to reschedule, just let us know. We're here to make it easy.

We're looking forward to welcoming you tomorrow.

Peacefully,
Sarah

---

## How to Use This Template

1. **Fill in all [PLACEHOLDERS]:**
   - [CUSTOMER_NAME] = "Maria"
   - [TREATMENT_NAME] = "60-min Swedish Massage"
   - [DATE] = "Saturday, 22 February"
   - [TIME] = "2:00 PM"
   - [LOCATION_NAME] = "Hyatt Regency Malta"
   - [ARRIVAL_MINUTES] = "15"
   - [PARKING_INFO] = "Free parking available in the main lot. Ask reception for guest parking."
   - [ADDRESS] = "Hyatt Regency Malta, [full address]"

2. **Remove sections that don't apply** (IF_FIRST_TIME, IF_FOOD_RESTRICTION)

3. **✏️ Personalise before sending:**
   - If you remember something they shared, add it: "I remember you mentioned you've been stressed at work — this is exactly what you need"
   - Adjust based on their communication style

4. **Send** 24 hours before appointment (automated or manual)

---

## Variations

### For First-Timer
Add: "One thing to know: you're in completely good hands. There's no right or wrong — just the experience of being cared for. See you tomorrow."

### For Repeat Customer
Add: "We're thrilled to see you again. [Therapist/staff member] has been looking forward to your visit."

### For Couples
Replace opening with: "Tomorrow, the two of you get the afternoon to yourselves with us. No distractions, no rush — just time together."

### For Group
Replace opening with: "Your group is all set for tomorrow. We've reserved our private space and everyone's excited to welcome you."

---

## Tips

✅ Send exactly 24 hours before (or morning-of if booking is same-day)
✅ Include practical info (parking, arrival time, address)
✅ Reassure first-timers that nothing special is needed
✅ Make rescheduling sound easy, not like a hassle
✅ Keep it brief — people skim texts
✅ Always end with "Peacefully, Sarah"

❌ Don't be overly promotional or salesy
❌ Don't repeat information from confirmation (keep it fresh)
❌ Don't include new upsells or add-ons (timing is wrong)
