THE THREE NUMBERS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

TARGET (Close):    €120,000   ← Where you want to land
WALK-AWAY:         €200,000   ← INTERNAL ONLY. Never mention.

ACT 1: THE FIVE QUESTIONS (In This Order)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. "What's been the biggest challenge since the Numi situation ended?"
   ➜ LISTEN: Pain points, cash burn, urgency

2. "What does the monthly picture look like — covering costs?"
   ➜ LISTEN: Revenue truth, rent level, payroll stress

3. "What would a successful resolution look like for you personally?"
   ➜ LISTEN: His definition of "win", timeline urgency, ego

4. "What's your ideal timeframe?"
   ➜ LISTEN: "ASAP" = high motivation. "No rush" = lower urgency.

5. "Have you had other conversations with interested parties?"
   ➜ LISTEN: "Just you" = zero competition. "A few" = you need to move.

TACTICAL MOVES:
• Mirror last 3 words: "Revenue dropped 40%?" [silence]
• Label emotions: "This situation has taken a real toll..."
• Always follow with silence. Don't fill it.

ACT 2: THE REFRAME (When He Brings Up €1M Investment)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
HIS ARGUMENT: "I invested €1M into this..."

YOUR RESPONSE:
"I completely understand. You put in real capital, real work, real risk.
But the market asks one question: What does this business EARN today?
Not what it cost to build. Not what it could earn. What it earns right now.
That's acquisition pricing.

The €800K difference between what you invested and fair market value —
that loss happened whether you sell today or wait. Selling doesn't create
the loss. Selling stops the losses from continuing."

THEN: Show the one-page P&L.
EQUIPMENT APPRAISAL MOVE:
"Let's get a third-party appraisal. Let the market tell us what it's worth."

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ACT 3: THE OFFER (Your Opening Statement)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
"Based on what the business is generating today and the investment needed
to bring underutilized spaces up — I'm looking at this in the range of
€40,000 to €50,000. Let me walk you through how I got there..."

[THEN: SILENCE. Count to 10. Let him respond. Do NOT negotiate against yourself.]

HIS LIKELY RESPONSES:

"That's way too low"
→ YOU: "Walk me through your number. How'd you arrive at it?" [Listen.]

"I need €200K+"
→ YOU: "If you're at breakeven today, how does €200K pencil out?"
        [Make him defend the number. It often collapses.]

"What's your maximum?"
→ YOU: "We're just starting. Need proper due diligence first.
        €45-50K is my opening."

THE THREE POWER PHRASES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. "It sounds like this situation has taken a real toll..." [silence]

2. MIRROR: Repeat last 3 words as a question, then wait for answer

3. "How am I supposed to make that work at the current revenue level?"
   [Curious tone, not aggressive. This frames his position as unrealistic.]

ACT 4: CLOSE OR EXIT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
IF POSITIVE MOMENTUM:
"It sounds like if we could land somewhere around €100-120K, we'd have a
deal. Am I reading that right?"

[If he agrees:] "I'm not closing today. My team needs to do due diligence.
24-month P&L, bank statements, landlord confirmation, liability schedule.
If those confirm what we discussed, we move fast. Fair?"

IF FAR APART:
"I think we see the value differently. Let me have my team put together a
formal analysis. If we're still far apart after that, we'll know. I'll be
in touch in a few days."

RED LINES — WALK AWAY IF:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
❌ Rent > €10K/month AND he anchored > €200K (math breaks)
❌ Undisclosed equipment financing or business debts
❌ Landlord assignment uncertain or requires premium
❌ Revenue materially below €30K/month (is he lying?)
❌ Unresolved Numi legal issues (liability exposure)
❌ After 3 moves, still above €250K (too far apart)

DUE DILIGENCE CHECKLIST (Hand This to Him)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
□ 24-month P&L (month by month)
□ 24-month bank statements
□ Full lease agreement (assignment clause critical)
□ Landlord written confirmation of assignment approval
□ Equipment inventory + service records
□ All equipment financing agreements
□ Staff contracts + payroll records (2 years)
□ Numi separation agreement (written confirmation)
□ VAT compliance (last 8 quarters)
□ Outstanding creditor schedule
□ Any pending legal claims

TIMELINE: 7-10 days. Then: second meeting with accountant + solicitor.

POST-MEETING PROTOCOL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SAME DAY:
→ Do nothing. Do not email. Your silence is power.

24-48 HOURS LATER:
→ Brief email (3 sentences max):
   "Thanks for the time. Team pulling together due diligence request.
    Happy to connect early next week."

48-72 HOURS:
→ Formal due diligence request (make it official-looking)
→ Hand to solicitor / accountant
→ Let them vet in parallel

NEXT STEP:
→ Second meeting with accountant or solicitor
→ Now you have verified information
→ Now you can move numbers
