# Aesthetics & Slimming

## Aesthetics P&L Structure

```
Revenue (VAT-exclusive)
  - Services: 18% VAT excluded → amount / 1.18
  - Doctor services: 12% VAT excluded → amount / 1.12
- COGS
= Gross Profit (target ~73% gross margin)
- Doctor Payouts (variable, based on procedures performed)
- Staff Salaries (specific employees)
- SG&A
= EBITDA
```

### VAT Treatment
Aesthetics revenue must be adjusted for VAT:
- Standard services: divide by 1.18 to get ex-VAT amount
- Doctor/medical services: divide by 1.12 to get ex-VAT amount

### Doctor Payouts
Variable cost based on procedures performed. Not a salary — this is a per-procedure fee. Tracked separately from staff salaries. Historical range: €0–€20,750/month (highly variable).

### Staff
Key employees (as of Feb 2026):
- Staff salaries tracked in the salary lines
- Historically included Leticia and Adrianne (referenced in spreadsheet notes)

### Historical Performance

- Jan 2026: €19,596 EBITDA (32%)
- Feb 2026: €14,269 EBITDA (28%)

## Slimming P&L Structure

Added Feb 2026 as a separate business unit. Same structure as Aesthetics:

```

```
