# Finance Department

Financial operations for Carisma Wellness Group. Manages budgeting, reporting, P&L tracking, ad spend analysis, and financial workflows across all three brands.

---

## Purpose

This folder contains all finance-related workflows, knowledge, and automation. Finance operations span Carisma Spa & Wellness, Carisma Aesthetics, and Carisma Slimming.

## Folder Structure

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `knowledge/` | Financial policies, budget templates, reporting standards |
| `skills/` | Finance agent playbooks — reporting, analysis, forecasting |
| `hooks/` | Triggers and templates for finance workflows |
