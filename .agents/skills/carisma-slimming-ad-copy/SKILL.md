# Carisma Slimming Ad Copy

## Overview

Provide comprehensive Meta advertising copy structure, creative frameworks, and performance marketing angles for Carisma Wellness Group's weight loss and aesthetics offers. This skill ensures all Meta ads follow proven direct-response formulas, leverage customer psychology, and incorporate scarcity, social proof, and pain-point-focused messaging.

## Meta Ad Copy Structure

### Primary Text Formula (The 3-Line Hook)

The first 3 lines are the most critical—they must be hyper-optimized:

**Line 1: Callout Ideal Customer + Malta**
- Example: "Summer ready skin for Malta moms"
- Example: "Menopause making weight loss impossible, Malta?"

**Line 2: Pain Point Solved**
- Example: "Finally lose stubborn belly fat without restrictive diets"
- Example: "Reverse sagging skin and regain confidence"

**Line 3: Perceived Value + Real Price + Scarcity**
- Example: "Worth €299, now only €99 — Only 15 spots"
- Example: "Limited to 15 spots this week"

### Body Copy (Lines 4-8)

**Line 4: Agitate the Pain + Benefits**
- Dig deeper into the problem and highlight transformation

**Line 5: What's Included**
- Use bullet points or emojis to list package contents
- Example: "✓ Body composition scan ✓ Personalized meal plan ✓ Weekly check-ins"

**Line 6: Perceived Value + Price + Scarcity (Repeated)**
- Reinforce the offer urgency

**Line 7: Social Proof**
- Example: "Helped 500+ Malta women lose 5-10 kilos"
- Example: "Rated 5★ by 150+ clients"
- Example: "Malta's #1 voted weight loss clinic"

**Line 8: Call to Action**
- "Tap below to book your free consultation"
- "Message us now to secure your spot"

### Disclaimer
"Spots for this offer are limited to the first 15 available spots (if you're still reading this, that means we may still have a spot available!)"

### Contact
Include: Phone + Address

### Headline Options
- "Only 15 spots"
- "[LIMITED] Only €99"

## Copy Rules

### ✅ DO:
- Focus on **specific issues** they're solving for (not generic language)
- Copy must focus on the **prospect, not the business**
- Be **super specific** and focus on **PAIN POINTS**
- Always include **case studies** (before & afters framed as "Day 1 and Day 30")
- Always include **scarcity** (only 15 spots)
- Always include **social proof** (awards, 150+ 5-star reviews, Malta's top-rated clinic)

### ❌ DON'T:
- Use generic words
- Talk about the business instead of customer benefits
- Skip scarcity or social proof elements

## Ad Creative Structure (6-Step Formula)

Use this structure for both video and static creatives:

### 1. Pattern Interrupt (First 2–3 Seconds)
- Surprising stat: "Did you know your jawline starts sagging at 25?"
- Striking before/after transformation
- Practitioner holding device: "This is how we reverse 5 years of aging"

### 2. Clear Problem & Relatability
- "If you've tried creams and nothing worked…"
- "Many women notice this after 30…"

### 3. Social Proof / Transformation
- Side-by-side before/after photos (Day 1 and Day 30)
- Short testimonial: "It's the only thing that worked for my [problem]"

### 4. Authority Anchor
- Award logo, clinic exterior, practitioner credentials
- "Rated 5★ by 150+ clients in Malta"

### 5. Offer & Urgency
- "Book your free consultation this week and save €50 on your first session"

### 6. Simple Call-to-Action
- "Tap below to book instantly"

**Priority Content Types:**

**Requirement:** All campaigns must have a minimum of 2-3 before/afters in:
- Ad videos
- Landing page

- All influencers must be given a brief before filming
- Format: "Had this issue, nothing solved, found this offer [package name] at Carisma, amazing results"

### Email Content
- 1-2x centered around before/afters
- 1x promotional focus on discount

### WhatsApp SMS Blast
- Use GPT template to generate SMS campaigns

## Creative Refresh Strategy

Keep ads fresh without full recreations:

- Add **seasonal wrappers** (Malta summer, wedding season, New Year)
- **Rotate intros/hooks** every 1-2 weeks, keep core body identical
- Swap out **1-2 before/after photos** for new client results
- Alternate **offer wording** (€50 off vs. free add-on)
- Change **background music or caption styling**

## Customer Psychology

### Triggers (What Made Them Search)
- Photo that embarrassed them
- Clothing fit crisis
- Health checkup scare (diabetes, blood pressure)
- Failed diet attempts (keto, shakes, gym-only)
- Milestone events (wedding, graduation, reunion)
- Life changes (new baby, job stress)
- Emotional tipping point ("I hit rock bottom")

### Avatars
- **Bride-to-be:** Wedding dress motivation, 6-week timeline
- **Menopause:** Hormonal weight gain, metabolism struggles
- **Busy mom:** No time, energy depletion, family-first mindset
- **Obese:** Health scare, mobility issues, shame avoidance

### Marketing Angles
- **Fit into attractive clothes** — clothing transformation, wardrobe goals
- **Health** — medical markers, diabetes prevention, energy levels
- **Partner attraction** — confidence in intimacy, feeling desired
- **Social anxiety** — eat socially without guilt, Facebook photo dread
- **Food freedom** — lose inches eating favorite foods

### Hidden Emotions
- **Shame:** Dodging mirrors, deleting photos, avoiding social events with food
- **Fear of health issues** — diabetes, heart disease, mobility loss
- **Fear of losing partner attraction**
- **Need for acceptance and fitting in**

### Pain Points
- Avoiding cameras or mirrors
- Breathlessness with simple tasks ("hard time getting in and out of my car")
- Joint pain, poor sleep, tight waistbands
- Hiding behind baggy clothes
- Skipping social events
- Grocery shopping stress ("What's safe to eat?")
- Food cravings from yo-yo dieting

### Key Desires
- Fit into attractive clothes
- Eat socially without guilt
- Become active (play with kids, exercise)
- Improve medical markers (blood sugar, cholesterol, blood pressure)

## Trust Factors

Build trust through four pillars:

1. **Medical Authority:** "Doctor-led" or "Clinically Proven" to counter "beauty salon" skepticism
2. **Human Accountability:** WhatsApp support access creates safety
3. **Technology:** Body composition scans prove "science, not magic"
4. **Guarantee:** No target weight requirement, free consultation

## Messaging Pillars

### Diet Approach
- No food is "off limits"
- Portion balance
- Time balance
- Cultural compatibility with Maltese food culture

### Medical Credibility
- Doctor-led programs
- Science-backed protocols
- Data-driven (body scans, measurements)

## Usage Workflow

When creating a Meta ad for Carisma:

1. **Identify the avatar** — Bride, Menopause, Busy Mom, or Obese
2. **Select the trigger** — What brought them to search today?
3. **Choose the angle** — Clothing, health, social anxiety, food freedom, partner attraction
4. **Structure primary text** — Use the 3-line hook + body formula
5. **Plan the creative** — Apply the 6-step ad structure (pattern interrupt → CTA)
6. **Add required elements** — Scarcity (15 spots), social proof (150+ reviews), before/afters (Day 1 and Day 30)
7. **Reference brand voice** — Use carisma-slimming-branding skill for tone alignment
