## 🎯 How to Use This Workbook

### **Before the Meeting**
1. **Sheet 3 (Annual Summary):** Review the €123.4K EBITDA basis for your valuation
2. **Sheet 7 (Quick Reference):** Memorize mindset frames + the five questions
3. **Sheet 5 (Negotiation & Key Notes):** Read the power phrases and sunk cost redirect
4. **Sheet 6 (Negotiation Briefing):** Focus on Acts 1-2 scripts
5. **Print:** One-page P&L from Sheet 3 (bring to meeting)

### **During the Meeting (90 minutes)**
- **Act 1 (20-30 min):** Reference Sheet 7 five questions. Keep Sheet 6 Act 1 open if possible.
- **Act 2 (20-30 min):** Use Sheet 6 Reframe Value script + sunk cost redirect
- **Act 3 (20-30 min):** Open Sheet 6 response playbook for objection handling
- **Act 4 (10-15 min):** Use trial close / exit line from Sheet 6
- **Hand over:** Due diligence checklist from Sheet 5

### **After the Meeting**
- **Same day:** Rest. Do not email.
- **Day 2:** Brief email using protocol from Sheet 8 Execution Index
- **Day 3-5:** Due diligence request (solicitor uses checklist from Sheet 5)
- **Day 7-10:** Second meeting with accountant (now you have verified info)

### **During Due Diligence**
- **Sheet 2 (Monthly P&L):** Compare seller's actual monthly revenue to your model
- **Sheet 1 (Assumptions):** Verify payroll and OpEx against seller's statements
- **Sheet 4 (Sensitivity Analysis):** Update payback if actual numbers differ from model

### **Post-Close**
- **Sheet 4 (Sensitivity Analysis):** Track actual performance vs. Conservative/Base/Optimistic
- **Sheet 3 (Annual Summary):** Compare actual Year 1 KPIs vs. projection

## 💰 The Three Prices Explained

### **€45,000 — Your Anchor (Opening Bid)**
- **Basis:** Equipment at distressed FMV (50% of cost) + lease assumption risk
- **Why start here:** Anchors negotiations low. Gives room to concede. Shows seriousness.
- **When you'd accept:** Almost never. This is a negotiation anchor.
- **Your script:** "Based on current revenue and equipment value, I'm looking at €40-50K. Let me walk you through..."

### **€120,000 — Your Target (Where You Want to Land)**
- **Basis:** 0.97x current annual EBITDA (€123.4K) + lease premium
- **Why this number:** Aggressive given seller's motivation, but fair given business reality
- **When you'd accept:** This is your goal. Close here and you've executed well.
- **Your script:** "We're very close. What gets us across the finish line?" [listen for conditions]
