03 — The Frequency Crisis

Time Is Running Out

All three accounts share the same structural problem — Malta's small addressable audience (~230K women 25+) is getting saturated. Cross-campaign overlap means people are seeing ads from multiple campaigns simultaneously.

Cross-brand blind spot: The same women in Malta may be in all 3 brand audiences simultaneously. A woman aged 25-45 seeing 3 Spa ads + 3 Aesthetics ads + 4 Slimming ads = 10 Carisma exposures per week. No single-brand frequency report captures this.
Root cause: Slimming account reach is 36,841 people, but summed campaign reach is 66,576 — meaning 44.6% of people are being hit by 2+ campaigns. Individual campaigns show healthy frequency (1.4-2.3), but the overlap is what's pushing account-level frequency to dangerous levels.

07 — Cross-Brand Patterns

What's Working Everywhere

Five creative patterns that consistently win across all three brands, backed by data from 69 ads and 17 campaigns.

1

Hybrid Format Dominance

Combining static + motion elements outperforms pure formats everywhere.
Spa: €1.54 CPL (Reels hybrid)
Aesthetics: €1.37 CPL (static hybrid)
Slimming: $1.35 CPL (hybrid)
2

Objection-Handling Hooks

Ads that name the audience's concern convert 2-3x better than feature-selling.
Best: "Not sure if GLP-1s are safe?" — $0.97 CPL
Worst: "Pain Relief" (feature-focused) — €8.08 CPL
3

Specific Occasions Win

Concrete, recognizable life moments beat abstract messaging every time.
"Birthday?" = 44 leads, €1.37 CPL
"Flower fades" = 0 leads
"One stubborn area" = 16 leads, $1.35 CPL
4

Doctor / Authority Content

Adding a credibility layer drops CPL by 25-45%.
Slimming: Doctor post $0.97 vs regular $1.36 (29% lower)
Aesthetics: Review €1.38 vs Offer €2.53 (45% lower)
5

Reels / Video Outperform

Video formats consistently beat static across all brands.
Spa: Reels 43% more efficient (€1.54 vs €2.70)
Aesthetics: Facelift video 5.53% CTR (7x min)
LHR: Video testimonial €1.52 vs static €0 (zero leads)
08 — What's Not Working

Patterns to Eliminate

Static "Offer" Format

Leads with the deal, not the person. Fails consistently. Jawline offer €2.64 vs hybrid €1.37. Facelift offer €2.53 vs video €1.27. Kill this format across all brands.

Abstract / Poetic Messaging

"Flower fades" and "Don't bottle the feeling" — combined 0 leads. The audience needs to see themselves in line 1, not interpret a metaphor.

Static Testimonial Images

LHR static testimonials: €8.81 combined spend, ZERO leads. Same testimonial as video (Ann): €1.52 CPL, 15 leads. If it's a testimonial, it must be video.

CBO Ad Starvation

Systemic across all brands. 11 of 19 Spa ads under €15 spend. Potential winners being suffocated. "Your Next Date" at €0.58 CPL has only €2.89 spend. Force-testing required.

Too Many Variants at Once

Hydrafacial launched 4 "newoffer" statics simultaneously; 3 got zero traction. Launch 1-2 at a time so the algorithm can properly test each.

22 Dead Ads Wasting Budget

Spa: 3 zero-spend ads to kill. Aesthetics: 8 dead ads (€11+ wasted). Slimming: 0 losers (every ad is profitable). Clean house this week.
