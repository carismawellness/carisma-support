# Marketing

All marketing operations for Carisma Wellness Group. Encompasses SEO, paid ads, Google Business Profile, competitor intelligence, review management, and brand-specific marketing across all three brands.

---

## Purpose

This folder organises all marketing automations, strategies, and tools into self-contained domain folders. Each sub-domain has its own workflow, skill, tools, and scheduling config.

## Sub-Domains

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `competitor-intelligence/` | Weekly competitor ad monitoring and analysis |
| `google-gmb/` | Google Business Profile post generation and management |
| `google-reviews/` | Google review monitoring and response automation |
| `seo-optimisation/` | GSC quick-win hunting and Wix SEO auto-optimisation |

## Brand-Specific Marketing

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `spa/` | Carisma Spa marketing — Meta Ads, campaigns, creative strategy |
| `aesthetics/` | Carisma Aesthetics marketing — Meta Ads, campaigns, creative strategy |
| `slimming/` | Carisma Slimming marketing — Meta Ads, email marketing, campaigns |

## Folder Structure

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `knowledge/` | Cross-domain marketing knowledge — audience research, channel strategy |
| `skills/` | Cross-domain marketing skills — reporting, analysis |
| `hooks/` | Cross-domain triggers and templates |

## Related Config

- `config/brands.json` — Ad account IDs, page IDs, targeting per brand
- `config/offers.json` — Active offers with pricing, angles, CTAs
- `config/competitors.json` — Competitor page IDs and Ad Library search terms
- `config/kpi_thresholds.json` — CPL targets, kill thresholds, winner/loser criteria
