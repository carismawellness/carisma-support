### Key Q2 Themes

**APRIL — Renewal + Bridal Season Opens**
The quarter opens with a dual narrative: Easter as a seasonal reset moment ("bloom, renew, restore") combined with the official start of bridal season. Peel season peaks for Aesthetics. Pre-summer urgency begins building for all three brands.

**MAY — Mother's Day + Summer Countdown**
The single highest-volume gifting occasion of the quarter. All three brands run coordinated Mother's Day pushes. Summer-ready messaging moves from aspirational to urgent — "6 weeks to summer" becomes "last chance before summer."

**JUNE — Summer Arrival + Male-Targeted Campaigns**
Father's Day anchors the month. All three brands pivot to men simultaneously — a rare and brand-differentiating moment in a female-dominated market. Peak tourist season adds a secondary audience for Spa. Summer maintenance and upkeep plans launch for Aesthetics.

## CROSS-BRAND COORDINATION

The following occasions require intentional coordination across brands to avoid audience fatigue, message conflict, and wasted spend. Plan these as a single team briefing.

---

### 1. Easter Weekend (Apr 3–6)
**Brands active:** Spa (CAMP-001) + Aesthetics (CAMP-002) + Slimming (CAMP-003)
**Shared theme:** Renewal, reset, fresh start
**Coordination principle:** Each brand owns a distinct lane — Spa = sensory/restorative, Aesthetics = skin reset, Slimming = body plan. No single brand should dominate the other's territory in copy or creative.
**Recommended timing:** All three launch March 24. Stagger email sends by 24 hours to avoid inbox pile-up for customers who belong to multiple lists.
**Visual cohesion:** Consider a shared colour palette or seasonal motif (spring botanicals, soft gold/green) across all three brands for the Easter window only.

---

### 2. World Health Day (Apr 7)
**Brands active:** Spa (CAMP-009) + Aesthetics (CAMP-010) + Slimming (CAMP-011)
**Shared theme:** Health as a holistic concept
**Coordination principle:** Each post should feel like part of a larger Carisma Group statement on health — not three separate brands shouting the same thing. Consider a shared Instagram story series or a combined WhatsApp community message from the group.
**Note:** Organic only. No paid spend on a 3-day window.

---

### 3. Mother's Day (May 10) — HIGHEST PRIORITY CROSS-BRAND MOMENT
**Brands active:** Spa (CAMP-016) + Aesthetics (CAMP-017) + Slimming (CAMP-018)
**Shared theme:** The gift she'd never buy herself
**Coordination principle:** Mother's Day is the single most lucrative gifting occasion in Q2. All three brands should be coordinated around a unified umbrella message ("The Carisma Mother's Day Gift Guide") with each brand featured. This is the one occasion where a group-level communication (WhatsApp blast, email from "Carisma Wellness Group") makes sense alongside brand-specific campaigns.
**Recommended format:** A combined gift guide landing page or PDF featuring all three brands, shared via email and WhatsApp. Each brand then runs its own paid campaign driving to its own offer.
**Stagger email sends:** Spa (Apr 26) → Aesthetics (Apr 28) → Slimming (Apr 30). Avoid sending all three on the same day.
**Gift card urgency window:** All three brands need a "last-minute gift card" push on May 8–9 — ensure e-vouchers are live and purchasable online before the campaign launches.

---

### 4. Summer Ready (April–June)
**Brands active:** Spa (CAMP-005, 019, 039) + Aesthetics (CAMP-008, 020, 031) + Slimming (CAMP-007, 015, 030, 047)
