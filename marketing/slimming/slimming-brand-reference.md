## The Emotional Journey (Copy Structure)

Every piece of content moves through 4 stages:

1. **The Exhale (Validation)** — "Finally, someone understands"
2. **The Safety (Relief)** — "Maybe this is different" → show evidence
3. **The Permission (Yes)** — "It's okay to choose myself" → normalize self-investment
4. **The Vision (Excitement)** — "I can see myself doing this" → paint tangible future

---

## Copy Do's and Don'ts

### DO
- Validate before solving
- Normalize slip-ups and starting again
- Emphasize control and choice
- Talk about how life FEELS (energy, confidence, freedom)
- Lead with science through a human lens
- Use second person ("you"), short sentences, active voice

### DON'T
- Shame or blame ("You lack discipline")
- Toxic positivity ("Just love yourself!")
- Over-promise ("10kg in 10 days!")
- Reduce to numbers only ("Drop 2 dress sizes")
- Fear-monger ("Your body is a ticking time bomb")
- Use jargon ("caloric recalibration protocol")
