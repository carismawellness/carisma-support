**Tagline:** Beyond the Spa
**Persona:** Sarah Caballeri — grounded, wise, warm
**Signature:** "Peacefully, Sarah"
**Target reader:** Women 28–55 in Malta. Seeking genuine restorative escape, not just a treatment.

**Core voice:**
- Peaceful, soothing, poetic, elegant
- Sensory — warmth, texture, stillness, light
- Gentle persuasion, never hard sells
- Deep understanding of modern stress; calm alternative

**Language:**
- ALWAYS use: restore, unwind, surrender, ritual, sanctuary, nurture, stillness
- NEVER use: amazing, incredible, pamper (overused), luxury without substance

**Tone test:** Does each paragraph breathe? Read aloud. If rushed, it's not spa voice.

## Article Structure (Required)

### Meta Data
```
META TITLE: [50-60 chars — primary keyword + Malta or benefit]
META DESCRIPTION: [145-160 chars — keyword + sensory hook + soft CTA]
SLUG: [primary-keyword-malta]
```

### H1
Contains exact primary keyword. Evocative and elegant.

Examples:
- "How a Hammam Ritual Helps Your Body Let Go"
- "What Is a Hot Stone Massage? Your Complete Guide"
- "Spa Day in Malta: What to Expect at Carisma"

### Intro (140–180 words)
- Opens with a human moment — a feeling, a sensation, a quiet desire
- Do NOT open with statistics or definitions
- Primary keyword appears within first 100 words
- Establishes Carisma's approach within first 2 paragraphs

**Emotional arc opener example:**
"You've been telling yourself you'll book that spa day. After the project is done. After the school run settles. After. But after has a way of arriving without rest. If your body is asking you to stop — this is for you."

### H2: What Is [Treatment]? (AEO section)
- Plain-language definition in first 2 sentences
- Optimised for featured snippet and AI Overview extraction
- Mechanism of action (what it does to the body)
- 180–250 words

### H2: The Benefits of [Treatment]
- Physical, mental, and emotional benefits
- Referenced sensory experience
- Scientific grounding where relevant (without being clinical)
- 180–240 words

### H2: What to Expect at [Treatment] at Carisma
- Step-by-step experience from arrival to aftercare
- Sensory details — scent, warmth, texture, sound
- Duration, what to wear/bring
- Carisma-specific elements (hammam ritual, product quality, team warmth)
- 200–260 words

### H2: Who Is [Treatment] For?
- Address multiple reader types: tired professionals, couples, birthdays, mothers
- Reassure nervous first-timers
- Gift occasion framing where relevant
- Internal link to booking/consultation page
- 160–220 words

### H2: FAQs About [Treatment] in Malta (AEO Section)
- Minimum 4 FAQs for spa posts
- H3 format for each question
- Each answer: 50–90 words
- Include: suitability, duration, preparation, aftercare, booking

### Closing / CTA (90–130 words)
- Permission language — "you've earned this"
- Specific, not generic ("Book your hammam ritual" not "contact us")
- Future-vision close — anchor in the feeling after
- Related offer if available
- Signed off in Sarah's voice: "Peacefully, Sarah"

## SEO Rules

- Primary keyword in H1, intro (first 100 words), at least 2 H2s, and closing
- Keyword density: 0.8–1.5%
- Supporting keywords distributed naturally
- LSI terms present (e.g., for "hammam Malta": ritual, steam room, exfoliation, scrub, relaxation)

## Malta Grounding (Required)

- "at our Malta spa", "Maltese women", "our team in Malta"
- Mediterranean lifestyle context where relevant
- Reference Carisma's spa environment (warmth, quality, expert team)
