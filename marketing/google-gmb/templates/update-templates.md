## Template 6: Results & Transformation

**Best for:** Describing outcomes and transformations without before/after images
**Content mix:** 60% (Update)
**Frequency:** Use 1 time per month per brand

### Structure

```
{COMMON_PROBLEM — Name the frustration or concern. "That stubborn area that will not budge no matter what you try." or "Lines that make you look tired even when you are not."}

{WHY_IT_PERSISTS — 1-2 sentences explaining why the problem exists. Science-based, not blame-based. "Some fat cells are genetically resistant to diet and exercise" or "Repeated facial expressions create lines that no cream can smooth."}

{HOW_WE_SOLVE_IT — 2-3 sentences on the treatment approach. What happens, how it works, why it is effective. Use named technology where applicable.}

What to expect:
{REALISTIC_OUTCOMES — 3-4 bullet points describing realistic results. Use "can", "may", "designed to" language. Never guarantee. Include timeline for results.}

{KEYWORD_1} at {BRAND_NAME} in {LOCATION}. {PRICE}.

{CTA_LINE}

{BRAND_NAME}, {LOCATION}
{PHONE}
```

**CTA Button:** Book

### Brand Voice Notes

| Brand | Tone Guidance |
|-------|---------------|
| **Spa** | Describe the feeling, not just the treatment. "You arrive tense. You leave feeling like you have been on holiday." Focus on emotional transformation: stress to stillness, tension to peace, exhaustion to restoration. |
| **Aesthetics** | Describe natural-looking results without before/after claims. "Enhancement that makes people say 'you look amazing' without guessing why." Focus on confidence, not appearance change. |
| **Slimming** | Describe life improvements, not just body changes. "Climb stairs without thinking about it. Wear the dress without checking angles." Focus on how life feels, not numbers on a scale. Always validate the journey. |

### Compliance Reminder

- NEVER use before/after images or descriptions
- NEVER guarantee specific results (no "you will lose X kg" or "guaranteed wrinkle-free")
- Use "can help", "may result in", "designed to", "clinical studies show"
- Include realistic timelines (e.g., "Results typically visible in 3-5 days for Botox, 8-12 weeks for CoolSculpting")
