# GBP Offer Post Templates

**Last updated:** 2 March 2026
**Post type:** Offer
**Usage:** GBP "Offer" posts appear prominently on the business profile and in local search results. They include a start date, end date, and optional coupon code. Use these templates for all promotional, pricing-led, and gift voucher content.

---

## Placeholders Reference

| Placeholder | Description | Example |
|-------------|-------------|---------|
| `{BRAND_NAME}` | Full brand name | Carisma Spa, Carisma Aesthetics, Carisma Slimming |
| `{LOCATION}` | Primary location reference | St Julian's, Malta / Floriana, Malta |
| `{PACKAGE_NAME}` | Name of the package or offer | Spa Day Package, Couples Spa Package, CoolSculpting Starter Pack |
| `{PRICE}` | Offer price | EUR 89, EUR 159 per couple, EUR 199 |
| `{ORIGINAL_VALUE}` | Original/full value if applicable | Total value EUR 625 |

| `{CTA_BUTTON}` | GBP CTA button type | Book, Call now, Learn more |

## Template 1: Package Deal

**Best for:** Spa day packages, couples packages, treatment starter packs, multi-session bundles
**When to use:** Week 2 or Week 4 of the monthly rotation. Use when promoting a bundled experience with clear value.

### Structure

```
{HEADLINE — Value-led opening. "{PACKAGE_NAME}: Everything You Need for {PRICE}"}

{EXPERIENCE_DESCRIPTION — 2-3 sentences painting what the experience feels like or what the package achieves. Brand-voice appropriate.}

What is included:
- {INCLUSION_1}
- {INCLUSION_2}
- {INCLUSION_3}
- {INCLUSION_4}
- {INCLUSION_5}

{VALUE_STATEMENT — "Total value {ORIGINAL_VALUE}. Your price: {PRICE}." OR "{PRICE} for the complete experience. No hidden extras."}

{KEYWORD_1} at {BRAND_NAME} in {LOCATION}.

{URGENCY — "Limited slots available {THIS_PERIOD}" or "Only {NUMBER} {DAY} spots remaining."}

{CTA_LINE — "Book your {PACKAGE_NAME} today." or "Call now to reserve."}

{BRAND_NAME}, {LOCATION}
{PHONE}

Offer valid: {OFFER_START} to {OFFER_END}
```

**CTA Button:** Book / Call now

### Brand Voice Notes

| Brand | Tone Guidance |
|-------|---------------|
| **Spa** | Paint the full-day experience. Emphasise "no hidden extras", "the whole day", "not just a treatment". Make the EUR 89 / EUR 159 feel like extraordinary value. Include sensory details: Mediterranean views, warm pools, aromatic oils. |
| **Aesthetics** | Lead with what is included beyond the injection: consultation, personalised plan, follow-up. Position the price as an investment in confidence. "From EUR 180 includes your personalised treatment plan and complimentary follow-up." |
| **Slimming** | Lead with transparent pricing. "EUR 199 for the complete starter pack. Every cost explained before you commit." Emphasise total value vs. price paid. Include doctor consultation and Tanita analysis as value builders. |

## Template 2: Limited Time Offer

**Best for:** Seasonal pricing, flash promotions, time-bound specials, introductory offers
**When to use:** During seasonal periods (Christmas, Valentine's, summer, New Year) or when launching a new service. Ideal for driving urgency.

### Structure

```
{URGENCY_HEADLINE — Time-bound opening. "{SEASONAL_ANGLE} Special: {OFFER_SUMMARY}" or "This {MONTH} Only: {OFFER_SUMMARY}"}

{CONTEXT — 1-2 sentences on why this offer exists now. Tie it to the season, occasion, or moment. "Summer is approaching. Now is the time to..." or "This {HOLIDAY}, we are offering..."}

The offer:
{OFFER_DETAILS — Clear, specific: what they get, what it costs, how long the offer lasts}

Why now:
{REASON_TO_ACT — 1-2 sentences on why waiting costs them. Not fear-based. "Results from CoolSculpting take 8-12 weeks to fully develop. Starting now means you will see results by summer." or "Slots during the {HOLIDAY} period fill quickly."}

{KEYWORD_1} at {BRAND_NAME} in {LOCATION}. {PRICE}.

{SCARCITY — "Only {NUMBER} slots available at this price." or "Offer ends {DATE}. Book before it does."}

{CTA_LINE}

{BRAND_NAME}, {LOCATION}
{PHONE}

Offer valid: {OFFER_START} to {OFFER_END}
```

**CTA Button:** Book / Call now

### Brand Voice Notes

| Brand | Tone Guidance |
|-------|---------------|
| **Spa** | Seasonal warmth. "This Christmas, give the gift of peace." "This Valentine's, choose connection over crowds." Urgency through genuine availability, not pressure. |
| **Aesthetics** | Aspirational timing. "Summer-ready skin starts now. Book your consultation this month." "New Year, refreshed you." Urgency through treatment timelines (Botox needs 3-5 days, fillers need 2 weeks to settle). |
| **Slimming** | Empowering timing. "Results take 8-12 weeks. Starting in March means confidence by summer." Never shame-based urgency. Frame time as working in their favour, not against them. |

## Template 3: Gift Voucher / Gift Card

**Best for:** Promoting gift vouchers for any occasion (Christmas, Mother's Day, Valentine's, birthdays, anniversaries)
**When to use:** Pre-holiday periods (2-3 weeks before major gifting occasions). Also effective year-round for birthday and "just because" gifting.

### Structure

```
{GIFTING_HOOK — Address the gift-giver's problem. "Still searching for the perfect gift?" or "The gift she will actually use." or "Sorted in 2 minutes."}

{WHY_THIS_GIFT — 2-3 sentences positioning the voucher as the ideal gift. What does receiving this gift feel like? What does it say about the gift-giver?}

Gift voucher options:
- {OPTION_1 — e.g., "From EUR 50 -- redeemable for any treatment"}
- {OPTION_2 — e.g., "EUR 89 -- A full Spa Day Package"}
- {OPTION_3 — e.g., "EUR 159 -- Couples Spa Package for two"}
- {OPTION_4 — e.g., "Custom amount -- you choose the value"}

{DELIVERY_OPTIONS — "Beautifully presented in a branded gift box. Or choose our instant e-voucher for last-minute gifting."}

{VALIDITY — "Valid for 12 months. Redeemable at any Carisma location."}

{KEYWORD_1} at {BRAND_NAME} in {LOCATION}.

{URGENCY — "Order by {DATE} for gift-boxed delivery before {OCCASION}." or "E-vouchers arrive instantly -- perfect for last-minute gifting."}

{CTA_LINE — "Order your gift voucher today."}

{BRAND_NAME}, {LOCATION}
{PHONE}

Offer valid: {OFFER_START} to {OFFER_END}
```

**CTA Button:** Learn more / Book

### Brand Voice Notes

| Brand | Tone Guidance |
|-------|---------------|
| **Spa** | Warm, gifting-focused. "The gift that says 'I see you.'" Position as thoughtful, personal, and guaranteed to be used. Emphasise the full-day experience, not just a monetary value. Options for every budget. |
| **Aesthetics** | Aspirational gifting. "Give the gift of confidence." Position as a premium, thoughtful gift. Emphasise that the voucher covers the consultation AND treatment, so the recipient can explore at their own pace. |
| **Slimming** | Sensitive positioning. Gift vouchers for Slimming require careful handling. Frame as "a gift of support" or "an investment in her wellbeing" -- NEVER as "she needs to lose weight." Best positioned as self-purchase ("treat yourself") rather than third-party gift. |
