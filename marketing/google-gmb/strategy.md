## 1. Strategic Goal

**Dominate the local pack for all three Carisma brands across Malta and Gozo.**

Google Business Profile (GBP) is the single most underexploited marketing channel in Malta's wellness, aesthetics, and weight management sectors. Our competitor analysis confirms that no competitor in any of our three verticals runs a systematic GBP posting strategy. Most post monthly at best. Many have abandoned their profiles entirely.

By publishing 2 keyword-rich, brand-voice-aligned posts per brand per week, Carisma will build compounding local SEO dominance that competitors cannot catch without months of consistent effort.

### Why GBP Matters

- **Local pack = purchase intent.** When someone searches "spa day Malta" or "Botox near me", the local 3-pack is the first thing they see. GBP posts directly influence local pack ranking.
- **Zero ad spend.** Every GBP post is free organic visibility. This is the highest-ROI marketing activity available.
- **Trust signals compound.** Regular posting + review responses + photos + Q&A activity all feed Google's local ranking algorithm.
- **Competitors are asleep.** Malta's wellness competitors treat GBP as a static listing, not a content channel. We treat it as a mini-blog with direct booking capability.

## 2. Posting Cadence

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Frequency** | 2 posts per brand per week (6 total across the group) |
| **Days** | Monday and Thursday |
| **Time** | 10:00 -- 12:00 Malta time (CET/CEST) |
| **Timezone** | Europe/Malta |
| **Total monthly posts** | ~24 (8 per brand) |

### Why Monday and Thursday

- **Monday:** Captures "fresh start" search intent. Women plan their week, search for self-care bookings, and respond to weekend recovery messaging.
- **Thursday:** Captures "weekend planning" search intent. Women search for Friday/Saturday activities, spa days, and last-minute bookings.
- **Tuesday-Thursday mornings 10am-12pm** are the highest-engagement windows for local GBP content in Malta based on general GBP engagement data and Malta's browsing patterns.

## 5. Multi-Location Strategy

### Carisma Spa (5 Locations)

Spa posts are written once and then **copied to all 5 GBP location profiles**. The "Copy post" workflow:

1. Draft post in the primary template (St Julian's as default reference location)
2. Publish to the primary St Julian's GBP profile
3. Copy identical post text to the remaining 4 hotel locations
4. Adjust only the location-specific reference if the post mentions a landmark (e.g., "overlooking Spinola Bay" only applies to St Julian's -- replace with the relevant local landmark for other locations)

**Important:** Most posts should use Malta-wide language ("at Carisma Spa in Malta") rather than location-specific references, to enable seamless copying across all 5 profiles.

### Carisma Aesthetics (1 Location)

Single-location posting. All posts reference St Julian's / near Sliema. No copying required.

### Carisma Slimming (1 Location)

Single-location posting. All posts reference Floriana / St Julian's. No copying required.

## 9. Compliance Rules

These rules apply to **every** GBP post across all three brands. Non-negotiable.

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **No before/after claims** | Never post before/after images or make transformation claims. Describe outcomes in general terms only. |
| **No guaranteed results** | Never guarantee specific outcomes. Use "may", "can help", "designed to" instead of "will" or "guaranteed". |
| **All pricing "from"** | Always prefix prices with "from" (e.g., "from EUR 89", "from EUR 199"). Actual pricing depends on individual assessment. |
| **No shame or fear language** | Never use guilt, shame, blame, or fear to motivate action. No "fix your body", "you let yourself go", "ticking time bomb". |
| **Consultation = no obligation** | Always position free consultations as no-obligation. Never imply commitment is required. |
| **Medical disclaimers** | Slimming: "Suitability subject to doctor approval." Aesthetics: "Results may vary. Individual consultation required." |
| **UK English** | Use UK spelling throughout (colour, centre, programme, optimise). |
| **Maltese phrases** | Use sparingly for local warmth (grazzi, mela, prosit, ghaziza, sabiha). Never overuse. |
| **No competitor disparagement** | Never name competitors. Differentiate by highlighting Carisma's strengths, not competitor weaknesses. |
| **Urgency must be genuine** | Only use scarcity language ("limited spots", "filling fast") when it reflects real availability. |

## 10. Posting Workflow

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Select template** from `templates/` based on the content calendar rotation
2. **Select keywords** from `keyword-banks/` based on keyword rotation rules (no same primary keyword within 3 posts)
3. **Fill template** with brand-specific content, seasonal angle, pricing, and CTA
4. **Run compliance check** against Section 9 rules
5. **Publish** to the brand's GBP profile via the GBP dashboard
6. **Copy to locations** (Spa only: copy to all 5 hotel locations)
7. **Log** the post in the Google Sheets post log (date, brand, locations, post type, keywords, CTA, link, status)

### Triggering the Workflow

- **Manual:** Open this strategy doc, follow the content calendar, select the next post type and template
- **Agent-assisted:** Run `/gbp-posting` skill or "run workflow 12" to have the agent draft posts for review
- **Batch mode:** Draft all posts for the upcoming week in one session, queue them for review, then publish on schedule

## 11. GBP Profile Optimisation (Beyond Posts)

Posts are one part of GBP dominance. These supporting activities amplify results:

### For All Brands

1. **Review responses:** Respond to every Google review within 24 hours in brand voice (warm, personal, specific -- not templated)
2. **Photos:** Upload 5-10 new high-quality photos per month (facilities, team, treatments, local scenery)
3. **Q&A:** Pre-populate with 10+ FAQs targeting long-tail keywords
4. **Services:** List every treatment/package as a GBP Service with keyword-rich descriptions and pricing
5. **NAP consistency:** Name, Address, Phone must be identical across GBP, website, Facebook, Instagram, TripAdvisor
6. **Business description:** Keyword-rich, brand-voice-aligned, updated quarterly
7. **Attributes:** Ensure all relevant GBP attributes are set (wheelchair accessible, free Wi-Fi, parking, etc.)
8. **Messaging:** Enable GBP messaging for instant enquiries
