## Keyword Rotation Rules

1. **Never repeat the same primary keyword in consecutive posts.** Minimum 3-post gap.
2. **Include at least 1 primary keyword and 1-2 secondary keywords per post.**
3. **Use location keywords in at least 50% of posts.**
4. **Rotate seasonal keywords into posts during the relevant period only.** Do not use Christmas keywords in July.
5. **Long-tail keywords should appear naturally in the body text**, not forced into headlines.
6. **Track which keywords have been used** in the post log (Google Sheets) to ensure even rotation.
