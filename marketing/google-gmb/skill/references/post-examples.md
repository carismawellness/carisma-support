## Usage Notes

### How to Use These Examples

1. **Tone reference:** Read examples for the target brand before generating new posts to calibrate voice and style
2. **Structure reference:** Note the opening hook, body flow, pricing placement, and CTA pattern
3. **Keyword integration:** Observe how keywords are woven naturally into the text without forcing
4. **Length calibration:** Spa posts tend to be 580-650 characters, Aesthetics 595-620, Slimming 770-875. Aim for similar ranges per brand
5. **Do not copy verbatim:** These are references, not templates to reuse. Each new post should be original content targeting different keywords or angles

### Key Patterns to Preserve

- **Spa:** Sensory opening, thermal journey description, transparent pricing, Maltese touches, Sarah's signature
- **Aesthetics:** Question-led opening, consultation-first positioning, philosophy of restraint, specific pricing with "from", Maltese touches, Sarah's tagline and signature
- **Slimming:** Validation-first opening (address past failures/frustration), science-backed explanation, named technology with data, transparent pricing with value comparison, Katya's compassionate sign-off
