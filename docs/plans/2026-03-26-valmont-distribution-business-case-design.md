# Valmont Malta Exclusive Distribution — Business Case Design

**Date:** 2026-03-26
**Objective:** Build a world-class business case for Carisma to become the exclusive Valmont distributor in Malta.
**Deliverables:** Google Sheet (3-year financial model) + Pitch document

---

## Agent Pipeline

### Wave 1 (Parallel)
- **Agent 1 — Retail Sales Analyst**: Pulls current retail data from Weekly KPIs sheet, analyzes retail performance by location, benchmarks Pure Solutions/Fitomer sales, maps distribution infrastructure.
- **Agent 2 — Market Research Analyst**: Researches Valmont brand, Malta luxury market, distributor economics, competitive landscape, comparable market benchmarks.

### Wave 2 (Sequential)
- **Agent 3 — BCG Forecast Analyst**: Reads Wave 1 outputs + existing BCG analysis. Builds 3-year financial model (Conservative/Base/Upside) and pitch narrative.

### Wave 3 (Sequential)
- **Agent 4 — QC Analyst**: Reviews all outputs for realism, consistency, data accuracy, and scenario differentiation.

### Wave 4 (Improvement)
- **Agent 5 — Review Agent**: Suggests specific improvements across all deliverables.

### Wave 5 (Final)
- Agents 1-3 incorporate relevant suggestions. Agent 4 does final QC pass.
