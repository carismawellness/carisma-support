## The Flywheel Model

### What One Flywheel (€200) Delivers

| Deliverable | Type | Use |
|-------------|------|-----|
| 1 UGC video | Ad creative | Direct response ad |
| 2 voice-over reels | Ad creative | Direct response ads |
| Before/after photos | Ad + organic | Ads, social, testimonials |
| 4 organic social reels | Organic | Instagram/Facebook/TikTok |

**Total per flywheel: 3 finished ad creatives + 4 organic reels + photo content**

### How One Flywheel Lasts a Quarter

Each evergreen campaign gets 1 dedicated flywheel. The 3 ad creatives rotate across 12 weeks:

| Weeks | Ad Running |
|-------|-----------|
| 1-4 | UGC original |
| 5-8 | Voice-over #1 |
| 9-12 | Voice-over #2 |

Winners run longer than 4 weeks (if CPL is below target and frequency < 2.5x), which stretches the rotation further. If all 3 creatives fatigue before the quarter ends, Azul creates iterations (hook/CTA swaps) from existing footage at €25/vid.

## Overflow Protocol

**When to bring in Azul (€25/vid):**
1. A winning ad fatigues mid-quarter (frequency > 2.5x) and you want a hook/CTA swap to extend its life
2. A thematic campaign performs well and you want rapid iterations before the moment passes
3. Jerry's schedule is full and you need quick re-edits from existing footage

**Expected Azul usage:** 0-5 videos/month (€0-125). Not a regular line item.

## Key Assumptions

1. One flywheel (€200) covers one campaign for one quarter (3 ad creatives rotated across 12 weeks)
2. Jerry delivers fully finished, edited content — no secondary editing needed in the regular pipeline
3. Winners run longer than 4 weeks, extending the rotation naturally
4. Evergreen campaigns are refreshed once per quarter, staggered by brand across the 3 months
5. Thematic campaigns are net-new monthly, driven by seasonal moments, new offers, or strategic pushes
6. Organic social content comes free as part of the flywheel (4 reels per flywheel = 84/quarter)
