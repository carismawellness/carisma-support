## Overview

Expand the existing 24-page Carisma Company Profile with 7 new investor-focused slides. The new slides are appended after the existing "The Way Forward" section (page 21) and before the Contact/closing pages, creating a seamless transition from brand story to investment opportunity.

**Design principle:** Match the existing profile's look and feel — clean minimalist aesthetic, muted warm tones (beige, taupe, gold), uppercase spaced headers, Carisma rose watermark, full-bleed photography between key text slides.

### NEW SLIDE 7: Why Now — Investment Thesis

**Layout:** Bullet-driven with supporting callouts on the right margin (similar to page 15 "What is Next")

**Header:**
> WHY NOW

**Six thesis bullets (main body, left 70%):**

> **1. Proven operator, proven growth**
> 50% revenue growth in 3 years, 25% EBITDA margins, 88% IRR on latest buildout — all organic. Management has demonstrated it can build and operate profitably before asking for acquisition capital.
>
> **2. Fragmented market ripe for consolidation**
> Malta's wellness market has no dominant platform player. Dozens of small single-location operators with no scale advantages. Classic roll-up opportunity.
>
> **3. Motivated sellers at attractive valuations**
> Both pipeline targets are distress or succession-driven, enabling acquisitions at 3-4x EBITDA vs. 6-8x for healthy businesses. Built-in margin of safety.
>
> **4. Vertical integration = instant value creation**
> We design, build, procure, and operate. Every acquisition immediately benefits from our supply chain, training systems, and operational playbook. Day-one cost reduction, day-one cross-sell.
>
> **5. Multiple expansion potential**
> Acquiring single-location operators at 3-4x and integrating into a multi-location platform that commands higher multiples. Each bolt-on is immediately accretive.
>
> **6. Malta market tailwinds**
> 530,000 population growing 2-3% annually (expat immigration). 2.8M+ tourists per year growing 3-4%. Highest obesity rate in Europe (slimming demand). Wellness tourism is a global megatrend. Limited competition at institutional scale.

**Right margin callouts (30%, stacked vertically in accent boxes):**

> **8** Industry Awards
>
> **99%** Customer Satisfaction
>
> **34** Years Operating
>
> **20,000** Active Subscribers

**Closing statement (bottom, centered, italic):**
> "We've spent 34 years building the operational playbook. Now we're ready to deploy it through acquisitions — and the market timing has never been better."

**Design notes:**
- Left-heavy layout with thesis bullets as the main content
- Right callouts in small gold-bordered boxes, stacked vertically
- Closing statement in elegant italic, same tone as "The Way Forward" on page 21
- This is the final slide before Contact — it should leave the reader wanting to pick up the phone
