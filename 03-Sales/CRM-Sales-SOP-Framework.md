# Part 1 — Culture, Vision & Mindset

## 1.1 Our Mission

Carisma Wellness Group helps women in Malta look and feel their absolute best — through spa experiences, aesthetic treatments, and medically supervised weight management. We are not a call centre. We are wellness advisors. Every conversation is an opportunity to change someone's day, confidence, or life.

## 1.2 The Cultural Pillars

These are non-negotiable. They define how we operate:

| Pillar | What It Means in Practice |
|--------|--------------------------|
| **Speed** | First contact within 5 minutes. Every minute of delay drops conversion by 10%. |
| **Empathy** | Listen before you pitch. Understand her motivation before recommending a treatment. |
| **Ownership** | If a lead is assigned to you, you own the outcome. No orphaned leads. |
| **Precision** | Every task completed. Every field filled. Every outcome logged. The system only works if you feed it correctly. |
| **Growth** | Learn from every call. Listen to your own recordings. Ask for feedback. Get 1% better every day. |

## 1.3 What We Believe About Sales

- **We don't sell treatments. We solve problems.** A woman calling about Botox is really calling about confidence. A woman asking about fat freezing is really asking for control over her body. Meet the real need.
- **Silence is golden.** After you reveal the price, stop talking. The next person who speaks loses.
- **Follow-up is not annoying — it's caring.** 80% of sales happen after the 5th contact. Most reps give up after 2. We don't.
- **Every "no" is a "not yet."** Unless she explicitly says "remove me from your list," she's still a prospect. Nurture her.

## 1.4 What Carisma Expects From You

1. **Respond to every new lead within 5 minutes** — Speed-to-Lead is the single most important metric
2. **Complete every task on the day it's due** — 90%+ task completion rate
3. **Log every interaction accurately** — If it's not in the CRM, it didn't happen
4. **Hit your daily dial target** — Minimum dials per day (see KPI section)
5. **Maintain brand voice** — You represent Sarah (Spa/Aesthetics) or Katya (Slimming) in every message
6. **Protect client data** — Never share client information outside authorised systems

# Part 2 — Your Daily Workflow

## 2.1 The Task-Driven System

We use a **task-driven workflow** in Zoho CRM. This means:

- You do NOT manage leads by looking at the pipeline Kanban board
- You work from **My Tasks → Due Today**, sorted by Priority
- The system automatically creates your next task based on the outcome you log
- Your only job is: **open task → execute → log outcome → repeat**

## 2.2 Start-of-Day Routine (First 10 Minutes)

| Step | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Log into Zoho CRM → Activities → My Tasks | 1 min |
| 2 | Filter: Due Date = Today, sort by Priority (Urgent first) | 30 sec |
| 3 | Check WhatsApp for any overnight messages from your assigned brand | 2 min |
| 4 | Check email inbox — aim for zero unread by end of day | 2 min |
| 5 | Review the CRM Master Sheet for today's brand assignment | 1 min |
| 6 | Open Fresha in a tab (for Aesthetics/Slimming booking) | 30 sec |
| 7 | Begin working tasks from the top | — |

## 2.3 Working Your Task Queue

**Priority order — always work top to bottom:**

1. 🔴 **Urgent** — New leads (First Contact tasks). These are live and warm RIGHT NOW.
2. 🟠 **High** — Scheduled Callbacks and Post-Interest Follow-ups. These people are expecting your call.
3. 🟡 **Normal** — Follow-up 2 and Follow-up 3 tasks. Still in the cadence.
4. ⚪ **Low** — Final Attempt tasks. Last chance before we close them out.

**For each task:**

```
1. Open the task
2. Read the related Deal to understand context (which campaign, what offer, any notes)
3. Make the call / send the message
4. Log the interaction:
   a. Mark the task as COMPLETED
   b. Select the correct TASK OUTCOME from the dropdown
   c. Add notes if relevant (e.g., "Interested in Couples package for anniversary")
5. The system auto-creates the next task — move on
```

## 2.4 Task Outcomes — What to Select

This is the most critical dropdown in the entire system. **If you select the wrong outcome, the automation chain breaks.**

| Outcome | When to Use | What Happens Automatically |
|---------|-------------|---------------------------|
| **Connected - Booked** | She confirmed an appointment | No more tasks. Move the Deal to "Booking Confirmed" stage. |
| **Connected - Interested** | She's interested but didn't book yet | System creates "Post-Interest Follow-up" due tomorrow (High priority) |
| **Connected - Not Interested** | She explicitly declined | Move Deal to "Closed Lost". Select a Loss Reason. |
| **Connected - Call Back** | She said "call me back at [time]" | **You MUST fill in the Callback Date Time field.** System creates callback task for that exact date. |
| **No Answer** | Phone rang, no pickup | System creates next follow-up in sequence (FU1 → FU2 → FU3 → Final) |
| **Voicemail Left** | You left a voicemail | Same as No Answer — next follow-up auto-created |
| **Wrong Number** | Number is invalid or wrong person | Move Deal to "Closed Lost". Loss Reason = "Wrong Number" |

## 2.5 The Follow-Up Cadence (Automatic)

When a lead doesn't answer, the system escalates through this sequence automatically:

| Task | Timing | What You Do |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **First Contact** | Immediate (within 5 min of lead arrival) | Call. If no answer, send WhatsApp intro message. |
| **Follow-up 1** | +1 day | Call again. Reference your WhatsApp if they saw it. |
| **Follow-up 2** | +2 days | Call + WhatsApp with social proof (reviews, results). |
| **Follow-up 3** | +2 days | Call + SMS. Different channel to cut through. |
| **Final Attempt** | +3 days | Call + "last chance" WhatsApp. Warm but final. |
| **After Final — No Answer** | — | Move Deal to Closed Lost (Reason: Unresponsive). |

**Total cadence: ~8 days from first contact to close-out if no response.**

## 2.6 End-of-Day Routine (Last 15 Minutes)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Check My Tasks — are there any overdue tasks? Complete or reschedule them. |
| 2 | Check unreplied WhatsApp messages from your brand |
| 3 | Check unreplied emails |
| 4 | Update any Deal stages that changed today |
| 5 | Log your daily numbers in the CRM Master Sheet (dials, bookings, connected) |
| 6 | Submit your End-of-Day report |

## 2.7 Rules — ALWAYS and NEVER

### ALWAYS:
- Work from the **Tasks view** (Activities → My Tasks → Due Today)
- Complete tasks immediately after each interaction
- Set the correct Task Outcome **every single time**
- Fill in Callback Date Time when selecting "Connected - Call Back"
- Update Deal stage when a booking is confirmed or lost
- Add notes to the Deal after meaningful conversations
- Use the correct brand voice for the brand you're assigned to today

### NEVER:
- Manually move deals between follow-up stages/pipelines — the system handles this
- Create your own follow-up reminders or calendar events — the system handles this
- Skip the Task Outcome dropdown — the entire automation chain breaks
- Use the Kanban pipeline view as your daily workspace
- Leave a task incomplete at end of day without a reason
- Contact a lead outside of your assigned brand for the day

# Part 3 — The Sales Process

## 3.1 The 10-Step Consultation Protocol

This is the gold-standard framework for every sales conversation — phone, in-person, or video consultation. Master this and your booking rate will exceed 30%.

### Step 1: Greeting & Introduction
> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] calling from Carisma [Brand]. How are you today?"

- Be warm, natural, and human
- Use her first name
- Smile — she can hear it in your voice
- **Goal:** Establish rapport in 15 seconds

### Step 2: The "Why" — Understand Her Motivation
> "I saw you enquired about [treatment]. What made you reach out today?"

- **Listen.** Do not pitch yet.
- Let her tell you her story — the real reason is always emotional
- Take notes on what she says (add to Deal notes later)
- Common motivations: upcoming event, health scare, lost confidence, friend recommended
- **Goal:** Understand the emotional driver behind the enquiry

### Step 3: The "Where" — Paint the Ideal State
> "If everything went perfectly, what would the ideal outcome look like for you?"

- Get her to visualise success
- This creates the gap between "where I am" and "where I want to be"
- Reflect back what she says: "So you'd love to feel confident enough to [X]"
- **Goal:** Create emotional investment in the outcome

### Step 4: The "What" — History, Allergies & Health
> "Have you tried anything like this before? Any allergies or health conditions I should know about?"

- Gather clinical/practical information
- For Aesthetics: previous treatments, skin sensitivity, medications
- For Slimming: diet history, medical conditions, medications (especially for MWL)
- For Spa: preferences (pressure, temperature, areas to avoid)
- **Goal:** Build trust through thoroughness and show you care about her safety

### Step 5: The "How" — Show, Don't Tell
> "Let me show you what we can achieve..."

- Share before & after photos (Aesthetics/Slimming)
- Describe the experience and what to expect (Spa)
- Use specific examples: "A client with a similar concern saw [X] result after [Y] sessions"
- Reference reviews and testimonials
- **Goal:** Build confidence through proof

### Step 6: The Prescription — Mechanism 20% / Benefits 80%
> "Based on what you've told me, I'd recommend [treatment]. Here's why..."

- Spend 20% explaining HOW it works (the science/mechanism)
- Spend 80% on the BENEFITS and how they connect to HER specific motivation (Step 2)
- Personalise: "You mentioned wanting to [her goal] — this is exactly what this achieves"
- **Goal:** Position the treatment as the bridge between her current state and ideal state

### Step 7: The Inquiry — Check Temperature
> "How does that sound to you?" / "How do you feel about what we've discussed?"

- **Pause and listen.** This is a temperature check.
- If positive → move to pricing
- If hesitant → address concerns before moving forward
- If confused → clarify and re-explain
- **Goal:** Gauge readiness and surface objections early

### Step 8: Price Reveal — Then SILENCE
> "The investment for this is [price]."

**Critical rules:**
- State the price clearly and confidently
- **Then stop talking.** Do not justify, do not discount, do not fill the silence.
- The next person who speaks loses
- Let her process. She will either say yes, ask a question, or raise an objection
- If she asks a question → answer it, then go silent again
- **Goal:** Present price with confidence and let her decide

### Step 9: Fast Action Bonus
> "I should mention — if you book today, [bonus]."

- This is your urgency lever. Use it only AFTER the price reveal.
- Examples:
  - "We have a slot available this Saturday which is usually booked weeks ahead"
  - "If you book today, I can include [complimentary add-on]"
  - "This pricing is only available for this month's campaign"
- **Goal:** Create a reason to decide NOW rather than "think about it"

### Step 10: The Close — Book It NOW
> "I have availability on [date] at [time]. Shall I lock that in for you?"

- Always offer a specific date and time — don't ask "when would you like to come?"
- If she says yes → book immediately (Fresha for Aesthetics/Slimming, direct booking for Spa)
- If she says "I'll think about it" → deploy the downsell framework (see 3.2)
- **Goal:** Walk away with a confirmed booking and deposit where applicable

## 3.2 The Downsell Framework

When a client doesn't book at Step 10, never let the conversation end with "okay, let me know." Deploy the downsell ladder:

### Scenario A: "I don't have the funds right now"

| Level | Offer | Script |
|-------|-------|--------|
| 1 | **Split payment** | "No problem at all. We can split this into 2 payments — half now, half on the day. Would that work better?" |
| 2 | **Membership / financing** | "We also have a membership option that spreads the cost. Would you like me to explain how that works?" |
| 3 | **Reduced package** | "We could start with a single session at [lower price] so you can experience the results first. How does that sound?" |
| 4 | **Skincare only** | "At minimum, I could set you up with a personalised skincare routine that starts working right away. It's a fraction of the cost." |
| 5 | **Follow-up visit** | "Why don't you come in for a free follow-up consultation? No pressure, just so you can see the clinic and feel comfortable." |
| 6 | **Follow-up call** | "I completely understand. Can I call you next week to check in? Sometimes it helps to sleep on it." |

### Scenario B: "I can't decide today"

| Level | Offer | Script |
|-------|-------|--------|
| 1 | **Skincare as deposit** | "I tell you what — why don't we start with the skincare product as a deposit? It's a small commitment and you get to start seeing results immediately." |
| 2 | **€100 deposit** | "We can secure your slot with just a €100 deposit. That locks in the price and the availability. You can always adjust later." |
| 3 | **Follow-up visit** | "How about you come in and see the clinic first? No pressure. Just so you can picture yourself here." |
| 4 | **Follow-up call** | "Of course, take your time. When would be a good day for me to follow up — Thursday or Friday?" |

**Key rule:** Always offer the NEXT step down. Never jump from the full package to "okay, goodbye." Every conversation should end with a committed next action.

## 3.3 Speed-to-Lead Protocol

**The #1 factor in converting a lead is how fast you contact them.**

| Response Time | Conversion Impact |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Under 5 minutes | **Maximum conversion** — lead is still on their phone, still thinking about it |
| 5-15 minutes | 50% drop in contact rate |
| 15-60 minutes | 80% drop in contact rate |
| Over 1 hour | Lead is essentially cold |

**Protocol:**
1. New lead arrives → CRM auto-creates "First Contact" task with **Urgent** priority
2. You see it at the top of your task queue
3. Call within 5 minutes. If no answer, immediately send a WhatsApp message.
4. Log the outcome. The system takes over from there.

**If you are on another call when a new lead arrives:**
- Finish your current call (don't rush it)
- The new lead task will still be at the top when you return
- Contact the new lead immediately after

## 3.4 Outbound Call Best Practices

**Before the call:**
- Read the Deal: which campaign brought them in? What offer did they see?
- Check if there are any notes from previous interactions
- Know the price, the package contents, and availability
- Have Fresha open if you need to book

**During the call:**
- Stand up or sit upright — posture affects voice energy
- Smile — it changes your tone
- Use her name 2-3 times during the conversation
- Mirror her pace — if she speaks slowly, slow down; if she's energetic, match it
- Never talk over her
- Take notes on what she says

**After the call:**
- Complete the task and select the correct outcome
- Add meaningful notes to the Deal (not just "called, no answer" — add "Interested in couples package for June anniversary, budget concern")
- If booked: move Deal stage, create booking in Fresha
- If not booked: the system will create the next follow-up automatically

## 3.5 Handling Inbound Calls

When a lead calls you:

1. **Answer within 3 rings**
2. **Greeting:** "Good [morning/afternoon], Carisma [Brand], [Your Name] speaking. How can I help you today?"
3. **Identify the caller:** "May I have your name please?" → Search CRM immediately
4. **If existing lead:** Open their Deal, review history, continue the conversation in context
5. **If new caller:** Create a new Lead/Deal while on the phone, capture basic info
6. **Follow the 10-Step Protocol** — even inbound calls should flow through the framework
7. **Always end with a booked action** — appointment, callback, or follow-up date

# Part 7 — Objection Handling & Customer Scenarios

## 7.1 Price Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "It's too expensive" | "I completely understand — it's an investment. Can I ask what budget you had in mind? We might have a package that fits." → Deploy downsell ladder |
| "I can get it cheaper elsewhere" | "Absolutely, there are options out there. What sets us apart is [specific differentiator — for Aesthetics: medical team, for Spa: 35 years + awards, for Slimming: doctor-led]. A lot of our clients came to us after trying elsewhere." |
| "I need to check with my partner" | "Of course! Would it help if I sent you a summary you can share with them? I can also hold the slot until [tomorrow] so you don't lose availability." |
| "Can you do a discount?" | "Our pricing reflects the quality of care and results we deliver. What I can do is [offer payment plan / starter session / complimentary add-on]." Never discount the core price. |

## 7.2 Trust Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Is it safe?" | **Aesthetics:** "Absolutely. All our treatments are performed by qualified medical professionals. We use only premium products and follow strict safety protocols. Plus, every treatment starts with a thorough consultation." **Slimming:** "Everything we do is medically supervised by our consultant, Dr. Zaid. We use clinically proven technology and monitor you throughout." |
| "Does it actually work?" | "Great question. Let me share some results from clients who had similar concerns..." → Share testimonials, before/afters, review screenshots |
| "I've had a bad experience before" | "I'm really sorry to hear that. Can you tell me what happened? [Listen genuinely.] Our approach is different because [specific differentiator]. And we always start with a consultation so there are no surprises." |

## 7.3 Timing Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I'm not ready yet" | "That's completely fine. When do you think you'd like to start? I can set a reminder to follow up with you then." → Create callback task |
| "I'll think about it" | "Of course, take your time. Just so you know, [fast action bonus — e.g., this month's pricing, limited slots]. Can I follow up with you on [specific day]?" |
| "I'm too busy right now" | "I hear that a lot! That's actually exactly why [Spa: you need this break / Aesthetics: we make it easy — sessions are only 30-60 minutes / Slimming: we design a plan that fits your real life, not an ideal one]." |

## 7.4 Specific Scenarios

### She says "just send me the price"
> Don't just send a number. Respond: "I'd love to! The pricing depends on your specific needs though — can I ask you 2 quick questions so I give you the most accurate quote?" Then use Steps 2-4 of the consultation protocol before revealing price.

### She ghosts after showing interest
> Follow the automated cadence (FU1 → FU2 → FU3 → Final). On Follow-up 2, change the channel — if you've been calling, try WhatsApp with social proof. On Follow-up 3, try SMS.

### She shows up but hasn't booked
> If someone walks in or calls without an appointment, treat them like a Hot lead. Skip to Step 4 of the consultation protocol (you're already face-to-face). Always try to book something before they leave.

### She booked but wants to cancel
> First, understand why. "I'm sorry to hear that — can I ask what changed?" If it's nerves: "That's completely normal. A lot of our clients felt the same before their first session. Would it help if I..." If it's logistics: "Let's find a better time rather than cancel altogether."

### She's comparing us with a competitor
> Never bash the competitor. Instead: "That's smart to compare — it shows you're serious about making the right choice. Here's what our clients tell us they value most about Carisma: [2-3 differentiators]."

# Appendix C — Lead Scoring Model

Every lead is automatically scored from 0-105 points across 5 factors:

| Factor | Question/Field | Answer | Points |
|--------|---------------|--------|--------|
| **Experience** | "Is this something you've experienced before?" | "Yes, I do it regularly" | +20 |
| | | "Yes, I've tried it" | +15 |
| | | "No, first time" | +5 |
| **Timeline** | "How soon would you like to get started?" | "This week" | +30 |
| | | "Within 2 weeks" | +20 |
| | | "This month" | +10 |
| **Response Time** | Auto-measured (minutes) | ≤ 60 min | +25 |
| | | 61-240 min | +10 |
| **Referral** | Lead Source | External or Employee Referral | +15 |
| **Intent** | "What would help you most right now?" | "I'm ready to book" | +15 |
| | | "I'd like to speak with someone" | +10 |
| | | "I'd like to know more" | +5 |

## Temperature Classification

| Score | Temperature | What You Do |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **60+** | 🔴 **Hot** | Book consultation SAME DAY. This lead is ready. Priority #1. |
| **30-59** | 🟡 **Warm** | Standard follow-up cadence. They're interested but need nurturing. |
| **< 30** | 🔵 **Nurture** | Longer nurture sequence. They're early in their journey. Don't push hard. |

**This scoring is automatic** — the system calculates it. You'll see the temperature on the lead card. Use it to prioritise your approach and tone.

# Appendix D — Lifecycle & Retention System

## Customer Lifecycle Stages

| Stage | Definition | Auto-Promoted? |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| **Prospect** | 0 closed won deals | Yes |
| **First-Time Client** | 1 closed won deal | Yes |
| **Repeat Client** | 2-4 closed won deals | Yes |
| **Loyal Client** | 5+ closed won deals | Yes |
| **VIP** | Top 10% by lifetime value OR 12+ months continuous | Manual only (never auto-demoted) |
| **Lapsed** | Rebooking window expired, no new deal | Yes |
| **Win-Back** | Was Lapsed, now has a new closed won deal | Yes |

## Rebooking Windows

| Brand | Window | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Spa | 90 days | If no new booking within 90 days of last treatment → Lapsed |
| Aesthetics | 120 days | If no new booking within 120 days → Lapsed |
| Slimming | 60 days | If no new booking within 60 days → Lapsed |

## Rebooking Rate Targets

| Brand | Industry Benchmark | Our Target |
|-------|-------------------|------------|
| Spa | 30-40% | **45%+** |
| Aesthetics | 50-60% | **65%+** |
| Slimming | 40-50% | **55%+** |

## Why This Matters to You

When you speak to a returning client, check their lifecycle stage:
- **First-Time Client:** "Welcome back! How was your first experience?"
- **Repeat Client:** "Great to hear from you again. Ready for your next session?"
- **Loyal Client:** "You're one of our most valued clients. We have something special for you..."
- **Lapsed Client:** "We've missed you! It's been a while since your last visit. I wanted to reach out personally..."
- **Win-Back:** "So glad to have you back! Let's make sure this experience is even better than before."

The lifecycle system also triggers automated email campaigns through Klaviyo:
- **Pre-Lapse Reminder:** 7 days before rebooking window expires
- **Lapsed Win-Back:** 3-email sequence to re-engage
- **Loyalty Recognition:** Celebrates the 5th treatment milestone
- **Win-Back Confirmation:** Welcomes back previously lapsed clients
