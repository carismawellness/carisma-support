## Call Script -- SNATCH (Inbound/Outbound)

### Step 1: Greet & Confirm Caller
**What to say:** "Hi, this is Katya from Carisma Slimming. Is this [First Name]?"
**CRM Action:** Confirm name & phone number
**Scoring note:** Must use brand name "Carisma Slimming" and persona name "Katya". Must ask to confirm caller by first name.

### Step 2: Confirm Call & Treatment History
**What to say:** "Great -- I'm calling because you enquired about weight loss / medical slimming packages. Did you want general weight loss support, targeted body contouring, or medically supervised weight management?"
**CRM Action:** Note if client is new/experienced
**Scoring note:** Must reference that the client enquired about weight loss/slimming. Must offer 3 categories to help classify the need: general weight loss, body contouring, or medical weight management.

### Step 3: Ask Goals
**What to say:** "To make sure I recommend the best plan -- what's your main goal right now? Is it overall weight loss, stubborn fat reduction, improving metabolism, or medical appetite support?"
**CRM Action:** Note main concern in CRM
**Scoring note:** Must ask about main goal with specific options to guide the conversation. The 4 options cover the full slimming spectrum.

### Step 4: Ask History
**What to say:** "[Acknowledge their concern and share we've helped solve that concern] Have you tried any slimming programs, treatments, diets, or medical support before?"
**CRM Action:** --
**Scoring note:** Must acknowledge the client's concern first. Must share that Carisma has helped with similar concerns. Must ask about previous slimming history (programs, treatments, diets, medical support).

### Step 5: Explain the Value
**What to say:** "Many clients come to us when diet and exercise aren't enough -- we take a doctor-led, structured approach to help break through plateaus and achieve lasting results. You start with a personalised assessment with our doctor to understand your body composition, metabolism, and goals. Then we tailor your program -- this may include medically supported weight loss, body contouring packages, or a combined approach."
**CRM Action:** Confirm interest
**Scoring note:** Must normalize the struggle ("diet and exercise aren't enough"). Must emphasize doctor-led and structured approach. Must mention personalized assessment covering body composition, metabolism, and goals. Must present tailored program options.

### Step 6: Ask Interest
**What to say:** "How does that sound?"
**CRM Action:** --
**Scoring note:** Simple soft close to gauge interest before moving to booking. Must not skip this step.

### Step 7: Book Appointment
**What to say:** "We're based and we're open Monday-Friday 9am till 7pm, on Saturdays 9am to 1pm. What day and time usually works best for you?"
**CRM Action:** Book slot in calendar
**Scoring note:** Must state operating hours (Mon-Fri 9-7, Sat 9-1). Must ask for preferred day/time.

### Step 8: Confirm Time & Details
**What to say:** "Great -- I've booked you for [Day, Time]. I'll send over a confirmation text shortly."
**CRM Action:** Collect details: email, DOB, address, etc.
**Scoring note:** Must confirm the booked day and time back to the client.

### Step 9: Get Deposit
**What to say:** "Wonderful! Because this is a medical weight loss consultation with the doctor, the consultation fee is 70, and we take the full payment to secure your appointment. Once I book you in you will get a notification from Fresha and can confirm your booking from there."
**CRM Action:** --
**Scoring note:** Must frame the 70 fee as a medical consultation fee (not just a deposit). Must mention Fresha notification for booking confirmation. This is higher than Spa/Aesthetics deposits because it is a doctor consultation.

### Step 10: Send Confirmation
**What to say:** "Perfect! You'll receive a confirmation text now with your appointment, our address, and contact info. Feel free to reach out anytime!"
**CRM Action:** Send confirmation text & CRM reminder
**Scoring note:** Must confirm text is being sent with appointment, address, and contact info.

### Step 11: Close Warmly
**What to say:** "You're in great hands -- we're excited to help you start your transformation! Any questions before we end?"
**CRM Action:** Add call notes in CRM
**Scoring note:** Must close warmly with transformation framing. Must ask if they have any final questions. Must add call notes to CRM after the call.

## WhatsApp/Text Scripts by Scenario

**Trigger:** New lead comes in requesting a consultation
**Script:** Refer to the separate New Leads sheet for the full consult script.
**Key elements to check:** Timely outreach, brand name used, consultation offer made.

### Scenario 2: New Leads (Offer)
**Trigger:** New lead comes in responding to a specific offer
**Script:** Refer to the separate New Leads sheet for the full offer script.
**Key elements to check:** Offer name mentioned, value framing, urgency element.

### Scenario 3: Missed Chats Follow-Ups
**Trigger:** Lead sent a message that was not responded to in time
**Script:** Not yet populated in the CRM Master sheet. Should follow the same structure as Aesthetics, adapted for Slimming brand voice (persona: Katya, tone: compassionate truth-telling).
**Key elements to check:** Acknowledges missed message, offers value, low-pressure follow-up.

### Scenario 4: No-Shows & Cancellations Recovery
**Trigger:** Client missed their consultation or cancelled
**Script:**
"Hi [First Name], this is Katya from Carisma Slimming. I noticed you missed your consultation -- I totally understand things come up!

If you're still interested, I'd love to help reschedule with our doctor so you don't delay your progress -- do you want later this week or next?"
**Key elements to check:**
- Non-judgmental tone ("I totally understand")
- Mentions rescheduling with the doctor
- Progress framing ("don't delay your progress")
- Offers specific rebooking options (this week or next)

### Scenario 5: Booking Follow-Ups
**Trigger:** Lead expressed interest but has not yet booked
**Script:** Not yet populated in the CRM Master sheet. Should follow the same structure as Aesthetics, adapted for Slimming brand voice.
**Key elements to check:** References previous conversation, soft urgency, low-pressure.

### Scenario 6: Consultation Follow-Ups
**Trigger:** Lead had a consultation but has not booked a treatment

### Scenario 7: Appointment Confirmation + Upsell

**Script:**

- Confirms doctor name, day, date, and time

### Scenario 8: Post-Treatment Check + Referral

"Hi [First Name], this is Katya from Carisma Slimming. Just checking in after your session -- how are you feeling?

Also, if someone you know could benefit, we offer 25 credit for both of you when they book -- should I send you details?"
**Key elements to check:**
- Check-in on how they are feeling post-session

- Easy next step ("should I send you details?")
