# Template: Seasonal Offer Announcement

**When to Use:** When announcing a new campaign or limited-time offer to customers (email, WhatsApp broadcast, social media)
**Channel:** Email (primary), WhatsApp broadcast, SMS
**Tone:** Warm, positioning as exclusive moment, value-first

---

## Template Text

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME or Friends],

[CAMPAIGN_NAME] is here.

[POETIC_OPENING: Paint what the experience will feel like in sensory language]

**For [DATE_RANGE], we're offering:**

[OFFER_DESCRIPTION: What's included, not just the discount]
📅 Valid: [START_DATE] to [END_DATE]
💶 [PRICE or DISCOUNT_STRUCTURE]

---

**This is for:**
[TARGET_CUSTOMER: e.g., "Couples wanting to reconnect", "Anyone who needs a real pause right now", "Friends looking for their next adventure together"]

**What makes this different:**
[DIFFERENTIATION: What makes this offer special, not generic]

---

**Ready?**
[CALL_TO_ACTION: "Let me know a date and I'll check what's available" OR "Book here: [LINK]"]

Offer ends [END_DATE], so this moment won't wait forever.

Peacefully,
Sarah

---

## How to Use This Template

1. **Fill in all [PLACEHOLDERS]:**
   - [CUSTOMER_NAME] = "Maria" (or "Friends" for broadcast)
   - [CAMPAIGN_NAME] = "Valentine's Day Romance Escapes"
   - [POETIC_OPENING] = "Imagine: champagne, soft light, two hours with someone you love, no distractions, just stillness and each other. That's what we're creating this February."
   - [OFFER_DESCRIPTION] = "Our Couples Escape: 60-min massage together in a private room, welcome champagne, dinner for two, four hours facility access"
   - [DATE_RANGE] = "1–28 February"
   - [START_DATE] = "1 February"
   - [END_DATE] = "28 February"
   - [PRICE] = "€260 per couple" or "€150 for €120 (save €30)"
   - [TARGET_CUSTOMER] = "Couples wanting to reconnect"
   - [DIFFERENTIATION] = "This isn't a discount hunt — it's a designed moment. We've curated every detail so you feel genuinely cared for."
   - [CALL_TO_ACTION] = "Reply and tell me what date calls to you"

2. **Lead with experience, not price** — customers care about what they'll feel, not the number

3. **Be honest about timing** — "offer ends [DATE]" creates urgency without hype

4. **✏️ Personalise before sending:**
   - Add personal touch: "Maria, I know you love Valentine's surprises — this one's special"
   - Adjust tone based on customer segment
   - For repeat customers: "You always choose quality moments — this is exactly your style"

5. **Send** at start of campaign, then reminder email 1 week before end

## Variations

### For Email Broadcast (Many Recipients)
Use greeting: "Hi friends," or "Hi [NAME],"
Keep subject line punchy: "Valentine's Romance Escapes (while slots remain)"

### For WhatsApp Broadcast
Shorten significantly: "Valentine's romance escapes are live 💫 Couples massage + champagne + dinner €260/couple through Feb 28. Shall I check what's available for you?"

### For First-Time Campaign
Add: "We've never run this offer before. It's designed for this moment right now."

### For Returning Campaign
Add: "This was so popular last year, we knew we had to bring it back."

### For Last-Minute Campaign
Adjust tone: "Spontaneous moment: We just opened up 5 slots for [OFFER] through [DATE]. First come, first served."

### For High-Demand Period (Black Friday, Christmas)
Add urgency appropriately: "Slots are filling fast. This won't be available once we hit capacity."

---

## Tips

✅ Always lead with the experience (what they'll feel)
✅ Show the price/discount after the value build
✅ Make the offer clearly time-limited (not vague "while supplies last")
✅ Speak to the specific customer (not generic "everyone")
✅ Explain WHY this offer exists (not just "here's a discount")
✅ Include clear call-to-action (reply, book link, phone number)
✅ Always end with "Peacefully, Sarah"

❌ Don't lead with the discount ("SAVE €30!")
❌ Don't be salesy ("Don't miss out!")
❌ Don't use hype language ("Amazing offer!", "Incredible deal!")
❌ Don't make timeline vague ("Limited time" - say when)
❌ Don't overwhelm with options (one clear offer per announcement)
❌ Don't send too many campaign emails (once at start, one reminder)

---

## Campaign Flow

**Day 1:** Announcement email + WhatsApp to past bookers
**Day 3:** Reminder to those who opened but didn't book
**Day 7:** Last-chance reminder (if campaign is 2+ weeks)
**Day before expiry:** Final call
**After expiry:** Don't pitch this offer again (remove from this template section)
