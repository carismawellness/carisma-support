# Template: Post-Visit Follow-Up

**When to Use:** 2-4 hours after customer's treatment ends (72-hour peak window for referrals)
**Channel:** WhatsApp (preferred), Email
**Tone:** Warm, personal, grateful, gentle

---

## Template Text

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

I hope you're still floating a little.

[IF_FIRST_TIME: Welcome to Carisma. I have a feeling this won't be your only visit.]

[IF_REPEAT: Thank you for coming back. We're so glad to see you again.]

**How was your experience?**
Your feedback genuinely matters. If there's anything that could have been better, I'd love to hear.

[OPTIONAL: Short feedback link or "Reply here and let me know"]

---

**One thought:**
If today felt like the pause you needed, imagine sharing this with someone you care about. Whether it's [BRING_FRIEND_OPTION / GIFT_OPTION / TELL_THEM_OPTION], we're here.

[IF_BRING_FRIEND: Know someone who deserves this? We'd love to meet them. Message me and I'll make sure you both feel welcomed.]

[IF_GIFT: This makes an unforgettable gift. Perfect for [OCCASION: birthdays, anniversaries, just because]. Want to arrange something?]

[IF_REFERRAL: Tell a friend about us? We'd be grateful. Share this with someone you think might love it.]

---

**Next step?**
Ready to book again? Just let me know. Or take time to integrate this pause into your week — whatever feels right.

Peacefully,
Sarah

---

## How to Use This Template

1. **Fill in all [PLACEHOLDERS]:**
   - [CUSTOMER_NAME] = "Maria"
   - [BRING_FRIEND_OPTION] = "bringing a friend for a couples spa day"
   - [GIFT_OPTION] = "giving a gift to someone special"
   - [TELL_THEM_OPTION] = "mentioning us to friends who've been stressed"
   - [OCCASION] = "Mother's Day, Valentine's Day"

2. **Keep only ONE referral pathway** — pick which feels most natural for this customer

3. **Remove IF_ sections that don't apply** (IF_FIRST_TIME, IF_REPEAT, IF_BRING_FRIEND, etc.)

4. **✏️ Personalise before sending:**
   - Add specific memory: "You mentioned your shoulders were tight — did that release feel good?"
   - Adjust tone based on their personality (casual vs formal)
   - If they seemed rushed or uncomfortable, be gentler

5. **Send** in the 2-4 hour window (while they're still in post-treatment bliss)

---

## Variations

### For First-Timer Who Booked Return Visit
Replace paragraph: "I know what happened today — you realized something important. You're worth this time. See you [NEXT_DATE]."

### For Repeat Customer (Long-Time Fan)
Add: "[THERAPIST/STAFF_NAME] mentioned you're one of the few who really get what this place is about. Thank you for that."

### For Customer Who Seemed Stressed
Add: "Take this feeling with you. You just proved to yourself that you're worth the pause. Come back whenever you need it."

### For Couples Who Booked
Replace referral section: "The two of you had real time together today. That's rare. Do it again soon?"

### For Group/Event
Replace referral section: "Your group was brilliant. Want to bring everyone back? I can arrange a private session designed just for you."

---

## Tips

✅ Send while they're still in post-treatment glow (2-4 hours)
✅ Make feedback easy and low-pressure ("reply here")
✅ Only suggest ONE referral option (not all three at once)
✅ Acknowledge their specific experience
✅ This is the 72-hour peak for referrals — best time to plant seeds
✅ Always end with "Peacefully, Sarah"

❌ Don't immediately ask for upsell or next booking
❌ Don't send multiple referral asks (overwhelming)
❌ Don't be salesy — be grateful
❌ Don't ask "how was it?" in a generic way
❌ Don't include discount codes in follow-up (wrong moment)
