## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS FROM THIS MODEL

### 1. **Human Capital Drives Everything**
Staffing is 41% of revenue. Your business model depends on:
- **Retention:** Losing even one therapist = €8–10K monthly revenue risk
- **Productivity:** 5 spa therapists must generate €60K/month combined
- **Incentives:** Clinic therapists' 5% revenue share aligns them with clinic success

**Action:** Lock in key staff with 18-month contracts + retention bonuses (€3–5K each) before acquisition close.

### 2. **Fixed Costs Are Your Baseline Risk**
€13,780 fixed costs every month (rent + utilities + insurance + systems + professional fees). This is what you **must cover** at minimum.
- Breakeven revenue: ~€36K/month (before variable costs)
- At current €22.5K revenue: You're losing ~€13.8K/month just on fixed costs
- **Implication:** Revenue MUST reach €35K+ to be cash-flow positive

**Action:** Renegotiate rent from €8K to €7K if possible (saves €12K annually in fixed costs).

### 3. **Margin Improves Dramatically as Revenue Scales**
- Month 1: -29% margin (€6,780 loss)
- Month 6: +16.5% margin (€9,070 profit)
- Month 12: +39% margin (€33,220 profit)

Why? Fixed costs (€13.78K) don't change, but revenue grows. Once you break even (~Month 6), each incremental euro of revenue flows to the bottom line at high margin.

### 4. **Medical Aesthetics Is the Profit Engine**
Clinic is higher-margin than spa:
- Spa: 40% contribution margin (€85 treatment price, 8% consumables cost)
- Aesthetics: 55% contribution margin (€325 treatment price, 5% consumables cost)

At stabilization:
- Spa (€60K revenue): €24K contribution to cover fixed costs + profit
- Clinic (€25K revenue): €13.75K contribution (nearly 50% of clinic revenue)
- **Clinic revenue is 2x as profitable per euro generated**

### 5. **The Revenue Share Incentive Works**
Aesthetics therapists earn:
- €2,000 base (guaranteed)
- 5% revenue share (upside participation)

At different revenue levels:
- €15K clinic revenue: €2,000 + €750 = €2,750/month per therapist
- €25K clinic revenue: €2,000 + €1,250 = €3,250/month per therapist
- €35K clinic revenue: €2,000 + €1,750 = €3,750/month per therapist

This structure:
- **Retains staff** (base salary provides security)
- **Incentivizes growth** (5% share motivates upselling)
- **Scales costs** (if clinic underperforms, therapist costs don't spiral)
