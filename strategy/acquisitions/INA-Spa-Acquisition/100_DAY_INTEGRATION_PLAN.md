# 100-Day Integration Plan: INA Spa & Wellness Acquisition

**Carisma Wellness Group — Project Spa**
**Prepared:** 6 March 2026
**Classification:** Confidential — Board & Integration Team Only

---

## Executive Summary

This plan governs the first 100 days following Carisma Wellness Group's acquisition of INA Spa & Wellness at the Barcelo/Numi Hotel, Tigne Seafront, Sliema. Total investment: EUR 300K (EUR 200-220K purchase + EUR 80K clinic capex). The plan is organized into four phases: Pre-Close preparation, Stabilize & Observe (Days 1-30), Optimize (Days 31-60), and Accelerate (Days 61-100). Success is measured by zero client disruption, full staff retention through transition, operational integration into Carisma systems, and groundwork laid for the clinic expansion that will drive the investment thesis.

**Critical success factors:**
- Landlord consents to lease assignment before close
- All 13 staff transfer smoothly under Malta's TUPE regulations (S.L. 452.85)
- Client retention exceeds 90% through Day 100
- Clinic regulatory applications filed by Day 45, soft launch by Day 80
- Wage-to-revenue ratio trajectory visible by Day 100

## Phase 1: Days 1-30 — Stabilize & Observe

**Objective:** Take control without disruption. Understand the real operation. Earn trust from staff, clients, and hotel management. Establish financial controls from hour one.

### Day 1 Takeover Checklist

**Before doors open (7:00-9:00):**

- [ ] Integration Lead and Operations Lead arrive on-site by 7:00
- [ ] Seller performs physical handover: all keys, alarm codes, safe combinations, supplier contacts
- [ ] IT switches admin credentials on all systems (booking, POS, email, Wi-Fi)
- [ ] New bank account activated; card terminals re-pointed to Carisma account
- [ ] Cash float counted and documented (two signatures)
- [ ] Insurance certificate displayed (Carisma's policy, effective Day 1)

**Staff meeting (8:00-9:00, before client arrivals):**

- [ ] Joint announcement by Integration Lead and Seller: ownership has transferred
- [ ] Distribute staff letter and FAQ
- [ ] Key messages: (1) all jobs are safe and terms unchanged, (2) we're here to invest and grow, (3) nothing changes for clients today, (4) open door policy — any concern, come to us directly
- [ ] Introduce Operations Lead as day-to-day contact
- [ ] Seller to endorse new ownership warmly and publicly
- [ ] Schedule individual 1:1 meetings with every staff member during Days 1-5

**First client shift (9:00 onwards):**

- [ ] Operations Lead present on-site for full first day
- [ ] Small welcome touch in reception (e.g., complimentary refreshments, subtle "Under New Management" tasteful signage)
- [ ] All client-facing operations continue exactly as before — zero visible changes
- [ ] Observe: arrivals, gaps between appointments, client interactions, product sales, closing procedures

## Key Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|-------|
| **Landlord refuses lease assignment** | Medium | Critical (deal-breaker) | Engage early, present Carisma's financial strength and multi-brand track record. Offer enhanced deposit or guarantees if needed. Have fallback: negotiate sublease or management agreement structure. | Legal Advisor + Integration Lead |
| **Key therapist departure** | Medium | High | Identify top 3-4 revenue-generating therapists pre-close. Offer retention bonuses (vest over 6-12 months). Demonstrate growth opportunities (training, career path to clinic services). Build personal relationships early. | Operations Lead |
| **Client attrition during transition** | Low-Medium | High | Zero visible disruption in first 30 days. Same therapists, same services, same experience. Personal outreach to top 50 clients by spend. Introductory Carisma offers to incentivize rebooking. | Marketing Lead + Ops Lead |
| **Hotel management resistance** | Low-Medium | Medium | Present as an upgrade for the hotel — stronger operator, more services for guests, better marketing. Propose concrete partnership enhancements. Never surprise them. | Integration Lead |
| **Regulatory delays for clinic** | Medium | Medium | File applications as early as possible (Day 35-45). Use Carisma's existing regulatory relationships and precedents from Aesthetics clinic. Have contingency: launch with non-regulated services first (advanced facials, body contouring), add injectables and IV when approved. | Clinical Lead + Legal |
| **Wage restructuring backlash** | Low | High | Never frame as cost-cutting. Frame as revenue growth (denominator strategy). Use roster optimization and attrition, not redundancies. Introduce performance incentives so staff earn more as business grows. | Integration Lead + Ops Lead |
| **Systems migration issues** | Low | Medium | Run parallel systems for first 2 weeks if needed. Full data backup before any cutover. Ensure booking continuity — no client loses their appointment during transition. | Marketing Lead + IT |
| **Underestimated capex** | Low-Medium | Medium | Hold EUR 15-20K contingency within EUR 80K clinic budget. Get firm quotes before committing. Phase investments — don't spend everything on Day 31. | Finance Lead + Clinical Lead |
