## Section D: Ramp Curve Assumptions

**Ramp Philosophy:** Realistic, not linear. Based on actual wellness/aesthetics clinic turnaround patterns in Malta.

**Month 1-3: Launch Phase (30% of stabilized revenue)**
- Brand awareness phase
- Staff training and onboarding
- Client acquisition beginning
- Low utilization as word spreads

**Month 4-6: Growth Phase (50% of stabilized revenue)**
- Marketing beginning to gain traction
- Client repeat bookings starting
- Staff efficiency improving
- Referral network building

**Month 7-10: Acceleration Phase (75% of stabilized revenue)**
- Reputation established
- Booking rate increasing
- High client retention
- Word-of-mouth generating inquiries

**Month 11-14: Stabilization Phase (90% of stabilized revenue)**
- Full market penetration in target segments
- Consistent booking rates
- Established pricing power

**Month 15+: Stabilized Operation (100% of stabilized revenue)**
- Mature, efficient operation
- Pricing holds
- Client lifetime value maximized
