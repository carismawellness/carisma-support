# [SKILL] Recipe Formatter — WhatsApp Community

> **Invoke When:** Generating a What's Cooking Wednesday message, or formatting any recipe for WhatsApp.

---

## Purpose
Takes a recipe from knowledge/recipe-bank.md and formats it into a WhatsApp-ready message that's easy to read, save, and screenshot.

## Format Rules
1. Recipe name as a clear header (no markdown, just text)
2. Prep time and serves on one line
3. Blank line
4. Ingredients listed with • bullets (one per line)
5. Blank line
6. Method as numbered steps (keep each step to 1-2 sentences)
7. Blank line
8. Optional: Katya's tip (one practical note)
9. Points info if relevant

## Template

💪 What's Cooking Wednesday

[HOOK LINE — something warm, inviting, about the recipe]

[RECIPE NAME]
Prep: [X] mins | Serves: [X]

Ingredients:
• [item 1]
• [item 2]
• [item 3]
• [item 4]
• ...

Method:
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

[OPTIONAL: Katya's tip — meal prep variation, swap, or why this works]

~[X] points per serving

With you every step,
Katya

## Quality Checks
- Is every ingredient available in Malta?
- Is the recipe genuinely quick (under 30 mins)?
- Is protein the star?
- Would the whole family eat this (not "diet food")?
- Is the method clear enough that someone could follow it without Googling?
