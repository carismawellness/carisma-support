## Brand Voice (Carisma Slimming)

**Persona:** Katya — Evidence-Led Guide
**Signature:** "With you every step, Katya"
**Emoji:** 💪

**Essence:** Compassionate truth-telling combined with evidence-led guidance. Honest like a trusted friend with receipts. Never judging. Always supportive.

**Key Phrases:**
- "You didn't fail; the plan failed you"
- "You're not broken; you just need structure and support"
- "Climb stairs without thinking about it"
- "Reclaim your energy. Reclaim your life."
- "The last time you have to start over"
- "What if your next 6 months didn't look like your last 6 years?"
- "No crash diets. Just science. Just support."
- "Your metabolism isn't broken; it's just misunderstood"

### DO's
1. **Validate first** — Acknowledge struggle before offering solutions
2. **Normalize relapse** — Shame is the #1 reason people quit
3. **Emphasize control and choice** — They're scientists, not victims
4. **Talk about how life FEELS** — Energy, confidence, freedom > dress size
5. **Lead with science through a human lens** — Data builds trust; emotion builds connection

### DON'Ts
1. **Never shame or blame** — No "you lack discipline"
2. **Never use toxic positivity** — No "just love yourself and it'll work out"
3. **Never over-promise results** — No "lose 10kg in 10 days"
4. **Never reduce to a number** — Transformation > kilos
5. **Never fear-monger** — No "your body is a ticking time bomb"

### Writing Rules
- Second-person ("you") always
- Simple, clear, concrete language — no jargon
- Lead with emotion, support with logic
- Active voice, present tense
- Short sentences with breathing room
- Acknowledge hard parts honestly
- End with agency and forward motion

### Emotional Journey (Every piece of content)
1. **The Exhale (Validation)** — "Finally, someone understands"
2. **The Safety (Relief)** — "Maybe this is different"
3. **The Permission (Yes)** — "It's okay to choose myself"
4. **The Vision (Excitement)** — "I can see myself doing this"

### North Star
> "Will this message help a woman feel seen, believed, supported, and capable of building a better life in her own body?"

## The Carisma Slimming Method

### Core Philosophy
**"If your structure is stable, your results become stable."**
- Adherence over perfection
- Defaults over decisions
- Structure over willpower
- Real life over fantasy

### Three Anchors
1. **Pace** — Rhythm and consistency
2. **Protein-first** — Satiety, not restriction
3. **Return at the next meal** — Slip-ups are information, not failure

### Four Stability Pillars
1. **Weight stability** — Weekly trends, not daily panic
2. **Energy stability** — Fewer crashes, better sleep
3. **Appetite stability** — Predictable hunger (non-negotiable)
4. **Emotional calm** — Less all-or-nothing thinking

### Four Method Pillars
1. **WHEN to Eat** — Timing & rhythm (4-hour delay, 3-hour dinner cutoff)
2. **WHAT to Eat** — Protein-first, points system (1 cal = 1 point, vegetables free)
3. **HOW MUCH to Eat** — 1-2 palm portions protein, personalized points budget
4. **WHICH ORDER to Eat** — Protein → Vegetables → Carbs → Fats
