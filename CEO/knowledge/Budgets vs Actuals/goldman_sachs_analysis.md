## 1. Executive Summary

Carisma Spa & Wellness Group operates an outsourced hotel spa management platform across Malta, currently managing spa operations in 10+ hotel properties (4-star to 5-star). Revenue CAGR of **15.6% over 11 years** (EUR 673,694 in 2014 to EUR 3,300,050 projected 2025).

**Key Investment Highlights:**
- Revenue grown **4.9x** from inception
- Dual engine: organic same-store growth + inorganic new location expansion
- V-shaped COVID recovery, exceeded pre-COVID by 28.9% in 2023
- 2023-2025 acceleration: ~29% YoY growth
- Revenue diversification: Aesthetics, Spa Club memberships, retail/e-commerce

**Key Concerns:**
- Revenue data not investor-ready format
- No standardized same-store vs new-store separation
- MRR not clearly isolated
- Revenue per FTE, utilization metrics absent
