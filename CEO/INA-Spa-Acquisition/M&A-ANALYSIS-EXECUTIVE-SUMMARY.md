## WHY THE RECOMMENDED STRUCTURE IS SUPERIOR

### The Five Advantages

**1. Capital Efficiency**
- Upfront: EUR 300K (not EUR 350K)
- Preserves EUR 50-100K for clinic equipment, training, working capital
- Clinic is highest-ROI activity; capital should support it
- Scenarios 1 & 6 lock up EUR 50K that could accelerate clinic launch

**2. Seller Incentive Alignment**
- Earnout tied to clinic EBITDA (actual performance), not calendar (12 months)
- Seller has EUR 100K at stake based on clinic success
- Motivates seller to support post-close: customer introductions, staff continuity, consulting
- Time-based earnouts (Scenario 2) remove this incentive entirely

**3. Acceptance Probability**
- 80-90% probability seller accepts this structure
- Seller sees "upside sharing" (not lowball)
- EUR 300K is close to his EUR 250K anchor
- EUR 100K earnout feels like participating in success, not gambling on failure
- Scenarios 1, 4, 5 have much lower acceptance (15-70%)

**4. Execution Risk Mitigation**
- If clinic underperforms by 25%, you recover EUR 30-100K via reduced earnout
- Seller has incentive to help (not to sabotage)
- Seller loses EUR 50K+ if clinic fails (skin in the game)
- Scenarios with seller financing (3, 5) create opposite risk: Seller becomes creditor who can foreclose

**5. Leverage Ratio Management**
- Only 1.27x leverage (EUR 400K total / EUR 123K EBITDA baseline)
- Even if clinic underperforms to EUR 100K total EBITDA, ratio is 1.33x (still manageable)
- Scenarios 3 & 5 exceed 1.6-2.0x (distressed territory if clinic struggles)
- Scenario 6 is 1.22x (lower, but at cost of capital efficiency)

## RISK MITIGATION: HOW RECOMMENDED STRUCTURE PROTECTS YOU

### Downside Scenarios

**Scenario A: Clinic underperforms to EUR 40K EBITDA (vs. EUR 50K plan)**
- **Earnout payout:** EUR 70K (not full EUR 100K)
- **Your benefit:** Save EUR 30K in earnout cost, offsetting clinic underperformance
- **Total deal cost:** EUR 370K (vs. EUR 400K budgeted)

**Scenario B: Clinic fails entirely (EUR 20K EBITDA)**
- **Earnout payout:** EUR 0K
- **Your benefit:** Save EUR 100K in earnout, offset clinic failure impact
- **Total deal cost:** EUR 300K (guaranteed)

**Scenario C: Revenue verification shows EUR 25K/month (not EUR 30K)**
- **Baseline EBITDA:** EUR 85K (not EUR 123K)
- **Earnout trigger adjustment:** Reduce to EUR 35K clinic EBITDA threshold
- **Renegotiation:** EUR 300K becomes EUR 270K (proportional adjustment)

**Compare to Scenario 5 (Seller Note):**
- **Same Scenario A:** You still owe EUR 250K note + EUR 17.5K annual service
- **Default probability:** 30-40%
- **Outcome:** Likely renegotiation or refinancing under stress
