# 8C — Workforce & P&L Strategy

**Date:** 2026-03-24/25
**Scope:** Full workforce model, org structure, and P&L optimization for Carisma Wellness Group (9 hotel-based spa concessions in Malta)

---

## Context

Comprehensive strategic exercise to design the optimal workforce model for Carisma's 9-spa portfolio (~€249k/mo combined revenue, €3.3M/yr group including aesthetics/slimming). Started with org structure review, progressed through revenue analysis, and culminated in a fully rostered workforce model with financial validation.

### Key Decisions Made
1. **No dedicated spa managers** — replaced by Rotational Seniors (saves ~€36k/yr)
2. **Hybrid contract model** — 33 salaried core + hourly flex/helpers/PT/seasonal
3. **Therapist counts revenue-validated** — IC needs 7 (not 5), Hugo's needs 4 (not 3)
4. **Stop leave policy** — Jul-Oct blackout preserves €15-25k revenue
5. **Spa Helper role introduced** — unlocks €96-160k therapist revenue for €25k cost
6. **3-region RM structure** — Neli (IC/Hugo's/Hyatt), Kristina (Ramla/Labranda/Odyssey), Melanie (Excelsior/Sunny/Novotel)
7. **Career ladder** — 7 levels from Spa Helper (€950) to Ops Director (€5,000)

### Grand Totals
| Period | Headcount | FTE | Monthly Cost | HC% on Revenue |
|--------|-----------|-----|-------------|----------------|
| Off-peak (Nov-Jun) | 48 | 45.6 | €84,032 | 33.7% |
| Peak (Jul-Oct) | 55 | 52.6 | €101,900 | 30.0% |
| Annual blended | — | — | ~€1,055,000/yr | 32.0% |

### Combined Lever Impact: ~€250-320k/yr in savings + unlocked revenue

## Expert Analyses Conducted

### 1. Luxury Spa VP (Mandarin Oriental perspective)
- RPTH (Revenue Per Treatment Hour) metric: target €85-95 portfolio-wide
- Dual licensing (body + aesthetics) for 1.4x revenue capacity
- Yield-based flex deployment using booking system data
- Commercial KPIs for Rotational Seniors
- IC key-person risk flagged

### 2. Uber/Platform Workforce Expert
- Shift bidding with premium tiers (1.25-1.5x for Fri-Sun/peak)
- Freelance micro-pool for surge capacity
- Real-time demand triggers from booking system
- Preference matching algorithm for scheduling
- Commission redesign for yield optimization

### 3. Toyota Lean Expert
- Takt time analysis: available minutes / treatments demanded = cadence target
- Heijunka scheduling: leveled 75-minute time-box slots
- 12-minute room turnover standard (5S methodology)
- Two-bin kanban for spa supplies inventory
- Jidoka quality triggers (stop-and-fix for quality issues)
- Self-managing team pods
