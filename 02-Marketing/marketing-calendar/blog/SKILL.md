# Blog Content Planner

## Overview

Plan weekly blog posts for all three Carisma brands. Each brand publishes **1 blog post per week**, optimized for SEO and aligned with the month's primary campaign/occasion theme.

**Core principle:** Blog content supports paid campaigns by capturing organic search traffic for the same topics. Every blog post targets 1-2 keywords relevant to the brand's core treatments.

## When to Use

- Building a monthly or quarterly marketing calendar (Phase 3 of calendar-strategy)
- Planning blog content for an upcoming period
- Deciding blog topics that align with campaign themes

## Frequency

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Posts per week** | 1 per brand |
| **Publish day** | Thursday (consistent across brands) |
| **Monthly total** | ~4 posts per brand (4-5 depending on month length) |

## Cell Format

```
SEO: [Blog Title]
```

**Examples:**
- `SEO: 5 Benefits of Deep Tissue Massage for Stress Relief`
- `SEO: What to Expect From Your First Hydrafacial`
- `SEO: CoolSculpting vs Diet: What the Science Says`

Place the cell value on the **Thursday column** of each week.

## Topic Rotation

Cycle through 4 topic categories to maintain variety:

| Week | Category | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| Week 1 | **Treatment Education** | Deep dive into a core treatment — how it works, who it's for, what to expect |
| Week 2 | **Seasonal/Occasion Guide** | Aligned with the month's occasion — gift guides, prep guides, seasonal tips |
| Week 3 | **FAQ / Myth-Busting** | Answer common questions, debunk myths about treatments |
| Week 4 | **Client Stories / Results** | Transformation narratives, testimonials, case studies (brand-voice appropriate) |

**If a month has 5 Thursdays:** Add a bonus Treatment Education post on Week 5.

## SEO Requirements

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Target keywords** | 1-2 per post, relevant to the brand's core treatments |
| **Title format** | Include primary keyword naturally (not keyword-stuffed) |
| **Cross-channel synergy** | Blog topic should support the month's primary Meta/Google campaigns |

## Planning Logic

```
For each Thursday in the target month:
  1. Determine week number (1-4)
  2. Select topic category from rotation
  3. Align with month's occasion/campaign themes:
     -> Week 2 MUST tie to the active occasion
     -> Other weeks should support the overall monthly theme where natural
  4. Select target keyword(s)
  5. Write blog title in SEO format
```

## Integration with Calendar Strategy

This skill is invoked during **Phase 3** of the `calendar-strategy` master skill, alongside email and SMM planning. Blog topics should be coordinated with:
- **Meta campaigns:** Blog supports the same treatments being promoted
- **Email:** Blog can be linked in TB_ educational emails
- **SMM:** Social posts can tease/promote blog content
- **Google Ads:** Blog pages can serve as landing pages for search campaigns

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| More than 1 blog post per week | Strictly 1 per brand per week. |
| Publishing on random days | Always Thursday. Consistency matters for SEO. |
| Blog topic doesn't match the month's campaigns | Week 2 must tie to the active occasion. Other weeks should support monthly themes. |
| No SEO keywords in title | Every title must naturally include 1-2 target keywords. |
| Generic titles | Be specific: "5 Benefits of Deep Tissue Massage" not "Massage Blog Post". |
| Writing to wrong rows | Spa=92, Aesthetics=172, Slimming=250. |
| Missing the "SEO:" prefix | Always prefix with "SEO:" for calendar clarity. |
