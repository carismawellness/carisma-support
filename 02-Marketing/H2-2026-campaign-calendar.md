### Key H2 Themes

**JULY — "Your Summer Invitation"**
All three brands launch mandatory Summer Sale campaigns under a unified "summer is yours" emotional frame. Secondary: UV/sun awareness, Isle of MTV festival energy, and International Self-Care Day / Friendship Day in the final week.

**AUGUST — "Wellness Is a Commitment"**
National Wellness Month anchors all three brands the whole month. Santa Marija PUBLIC HOLIDAY (Aug 15) is the biggest single cross-brand cultural moment of H2. Festival recovery (SummerDaze/Glitch) plays across Spa and Aesthetics. Bridal season final push.

**SEPTEMBER — "Your Time Returns"**
Back to School is the emotional spine for all three brands. Locals re-enter routine; mothers reclaim time. International Beauty Day (Sep 9), Self-Care Awareness Month, and National Skin Care Awareness Month create a permission-to-invest narrative.

**OCTOBER — "Celebration + Repair"**
Carisma Anniversary anchors all three brands. Post-summer skin repair season opens for Aesthetics (prime laser/peel month). Breast Cancer Awareness + Menopause Month add a women's health layer. Half-term creates a mum-clientele window.

**NOVEMBER — "Health + The Biggest Sale"**
BFCM is the peak commercial event of H2. Health awareness (Movember, Diabetes Day, Stress Week) builds medical credibility. Gift voucher pipeline warms through World Kindness Day before BF lands.

**DECEMBER — "Glow, Gift, Begin Again"**
Christmas gift vouchers and party-season treatments dominate. New Year programme pre-bookings for January launch in the last two weeks. All three brands pivot to 2027 messaging from Dec 26.
