# Competitor Intelligence Strategy

## Why Competitor Tracking Matters

Malta is a small, concentrated market. The wellness and aesthetics industry on the island has a limited number of serious players across three verticals: spa, aesthetics, and slimming. In this environment, competitor moves are immediately visible to customers, and pricing, offers, and creative shifts can directly impact Carisma's positioning.

Weekly competitor ad tracking provides:
- **Early warning** on new offers, price drops, or aggressive campaigns before they erode market share
- **Creative inspiration** from angles and formats that are working (or failing) in the local market
- **Pricing benchmarks** to validate Carisma's transparent pricing advantage
- **Seasonal pattern detection** to anticipate competitor moves and pre-empt them
- **Ad longevity signals** that reveal which creative concepts are genuinely profitable (long-running = likely working)

## Three Verticals, Three Competitive Landscapes

### Spa Competitors
- **Landscape:** Hotel spas, standalone day spas, wellness retreats
- **Key battleground:** Pricing transparency, package value, hotel partnerships, location convenience
- **Carisma advantage:** 5 hotel locations, 30+ year heritage, Turkish spa tradition, transparent pricing

### Aesthetics Competitors
- **Landscape:** Medical aesthetic clinics, beauty clinics offering injectables, cosmetic surgery practices
- **Key battleground:** Practitioner credentials, treatment range, before/after proof (which Carisma avoids), pricing
- **Carisma advantage:** Consultation-first approach, philosophy of restraint, no-pressure environment, free consultations

### Slimming Competitors
- **Landscape:** Weight loss clinics, body contouring providers, fitness-adjacent services
- **Key battleground:** Technology (named devices), pricing, results claims, doctor credentials
- **Carisma advantage:** Doctor-led (30+ years), FDA-cleared named technology (CoolSculpting, EMSculpt NEO), transparent EUR 199 starter pricing, shame-free messaging

## Intelligence Categories

### 1. Creative Angles
What hooks, pain points, and emotional triggers are competitors using?
- Hook types: question, bold claim, testimonial, fear-based, aspirational, educational
- Pain points targeted: ageing, confidence, time pressure, affordability, trust
- Emotional register: clinical, warm, urgent, aspirational, fear-based, shame-based

### 2. Pricing Intelligence
What are competitors charging, and how do they frame it?
- Listed prices in ad copy (extract all EUR/currency mentions)
- Framing: "from EUR X", exact pricing, "save X%", "was/now", package deals
- Free consultation offers and their positioning
- Comparison to Carisma's transparent pricing strategy

### 3. Format Trends
What ad formats and creative types are competitors favouring?
- Static image vs. video vs. carousel vs. collection
- Video length and style (talking head, B-roll, UGC, animation)
- Image composition (lifestyle, product, before/after, text-heavy)
- Use of faces, hands, treatment imagery

### 4. Seasonal Patterns
How do competitor campaigns shift with seasons and events?
- Valentine's, Mother's Day, Christmas, summer body, new year
- Launch timing relative to the event (how far ahead?)
- Seasonal pricing adjustments
- Themed creative vs. evergreen creative

### 5. Ad Longevity
Which competitor ads run longest, and what does that signal?
- Ads running 30+ days are likely profitable (Meta would kill unprofitable ads)
- Longevity distribution: % short-lived (<7 days) vs. medium (7-30) vs. long-running (30+)
- Creative attributes of long-running winners
- Budget signals from estimated spend bands

## How Insights Feed Into Carisma Strategy

### Creative Strategy
- Competitor creative angles that are new to us go into the hook bank for testing
- Formats that competitors favour but Carisma hasn't tested become creative experiments
- Competitor creative weaknesses (shame language, fear tactics, vague claims) become Carisma differentiation opportunities

### Offer Positioning
- Competitor pricing benchmarks validate Carisma's price points
- If a competitor undercuts on price, Carisma responds with value stacking (not price matching)
- Competitor offer structures (packages, bundles, free consultations) inform Carisma's offer architecture

### Campaign Timing
- If a competitor launches a seasonal campaign early, Carisma can accelerate its own seasonal push
- Gaps in competitor activity (e.g., no slimming ads in January) represent opportunities to dominate the conversation

## KPIs for Competitor Intelligence

| KPI | Target | Frequency |
|-----|--------|-----------|
| Competitor posting frequency | Track per competitor per week | Weekly |
| New ads detected per week | Track volume and category | Weekly |
| Ad longevity distribution | % in <7d / 7-30d / 30+d buckets | Weekly |
| Pricing benchmarks | Track min/max/median per vertical | Monthly |
| Creative format distribution | % static / video / carousel per competitor | Monthly |
| Time-to-insight | Report available within 24h of scan | Weekly |

## New Ad Analysis Framework

For every new competitor ad detected, classify it using this framework:

| Dimension | Options | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| **Hook type** | Question / Bold claim / Testimonial / Fear-based / Aspirational / Educational / Social proof | What stops the scroll? |
| **Pain point** | Ageing / Confidence / Time pressure / Affordability / Trust / Body image / Stress / Self-care guilt | What problem does it address? |
| **Offer type** | Discount / Free consultation / Package deal / Limited time / New service / Seasonal / No offer | What is the value proposition? |
| **Creative format** | Static image / Video / Carousel / Collection / Slideshow | What format is used? |
| **Media style** | Lifestyle / Product / UGC / Text overlay / Before-after / Animation / Talking head | How is the visual composed? |
| **Target audience** | Women 25-34 / Women 35-44 / Women 45+ / Couples / Gift buyers / Men | Who is this aimed at? |
| **Pricing visible** | Yes (exact) / Yes (from) / Yes (range) / No / Free consultation only | Is pricing transparent? |
| **CTA type** | Book now / Learn more / Send message / Call / Shop now / Get offer | What action is requested? |
| **Compliance** | Clean / Medical claims / Shame language / Before-after / Guaranteed results | Any compliance red flags? |
