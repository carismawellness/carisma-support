## Keyword Rotation Rules

1. **Never repeat the same primary keyword in consecutive posts.** Minimum 3-post gap.
2. **Include at least 1 primary keyword and 1-2 secondary keywords per post.**
3. **Use at least 1 trust/intent keyword per week** to capture high-anxiety searchers.
4. **Use location keywords in at least 50% of posts.**
5. **Rotate through all treatment areas** (Botox, lips, cheeks, jawline, skin) to avoid over-indexing on any single service.
6. **Long-tail keywords appear in body text**, not forced into opening lines.
7. **Track keyword usage** in the post log (Google Sheets) to ensure even rotation.
