## Keyword Rotation Rules

1. **Never repeat the same primary keyword in consecutive posts.** Minimum 3-post gap.
2. **Include at least 1 primary keyword and 1-2 secondary keywords per post.**
3. **Rotate through all service areas** (CoolSculpting, EMSculpt, VelaShape, Medical Weight Loss) to avoid over-indexing on any single treatment.
4. **Include the EUR 199 price point in at least 50% of posts.** Transparent pricing is Carisma Slimming's biggest trust differentiator.
5. **Use location keywords in at least 50% of posts.**
6. **GLP-1/Ozempic keywords** should appear in at least 1 post per month to maintain first-mover advantage.
7. **Long-tail keywords appear in body text**, not forced into opening lines.
8. **Seasonal keywords only during the relevant period.** Do not use "summer body Malta" in December.
9. **Track keyword usage** in the post log (Google Sheets) to ensure even rotation.
