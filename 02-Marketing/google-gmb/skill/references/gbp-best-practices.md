# Google Business Profile Posting -- Best Practices

Reference document for the GBP posting skill. Covers posting strategy, format guidelines, local SEO signals, and Malta-specific considerations.

---

## Post Frequency

- **Minimum:** 1-2 posts per week per brand for meaningful local SEO benefit
- **Optimal:** 3-4 posts per week per brand for competitive dominance
- **Context:** Most Malta spa/aesthetics/slimming competitors post monthly at best. Consistent weekly posting is a massive competitive advantage
- **Diminishing returns:** Beyond 5 posts per week, engagement per post drops. Quality over quantity

---

## Post Types and When to Use Each

### Update (What's New)
- **Best for:** Keyword targeting, educational content, trust building, brand storytelling
- **When to use:** Default post type. Use for service descriptions, wellness tips, team introductions, behind-the-scenes content, seasonal messaging
- **SEO value:** Highest. Google indexes Update post text and uses it for local search ranking
- **Example angles:** "spa day Malta" service description, treatment education, guest testimonial stories

### Offer
- **Best for:** Driving immediate bookings with transparent pricing
- **When to use:** When promoting a specific package with pricing, limited-time deals, seasonal promotions
- **SEO value:** High. Offer posts appear prominently on the GBP profile and in local search results
- **Unique advantage:** Offers display with a special "View offer" CTA in search results -- more eye-catching than standard posts
- **Required fields:** Offer name, start date, end date, terms/conditions, redemption link
- **Example angles:** EUR 89 Spa Day, EUR 199 CoolSculpting Starter Pack, Valentine's Couples Package

### Event
- **Best for:** Time-bound occasions with specific dates
- **When to use:** Open days, seasonal events (Mother's Day Spa Day, Summer Solstice Wellness Day, Christmas gifting launch), workshops, special evenings
- **SEO value:** Moderate. Events appear on the profile but have shorter visibility windows
- **Required fields:** Event name, start date/time, end date/time, description
- **Example angles:** Mother's Day Spa Day (specific date), Valentine's Couples Evening, New Year Wellness Reset Launch

## Character Limits and Optimal Length

- **Hard limit:** 1,500 characters maximum (Google truncates beyond this)
- **Optimal range:** 400-700 characters
- **Preview cutoff:** First ~100 characters are visible in search results before "Read more". Make them count
- **Why shorter often wins:** Mobile users scroll quickly. Posts that communicate value in 400-500 characters get higher completion rates than 1,200-character walls of text
- **When longer works:** Educational content, detailed offer breakdowns, and brand storytelling can justify 700-1,000 characters if the content is engaging throughout

## CTA Buttons

### Available Options
| Button | Best For | Carisma Usage |
|--------|----------|---------------|
| **Book** | Service bookings, appointment-driven CTAs | Spa Day, massage, treatment bookings |
| **Call now** | Urgency, complex enquiries, older demographic | Couples packages, group bookings, Slimming consultations |
| **Learn more** | Awareness, education, gift vouchers | First-time visitors, treatment explainers, gift voucher pages |
| **Order online** | E-commerce, product purchases | Gift voucher online purchase |
| **Sign up** | Newsletters, memberships | Wellness membership, loyalty programme |
| **Get offer** | Promotional offers | Seasonal deals, starter pack promotions |

### CTA Best Practices
- Every post MUST have a CTA button -- posts without CTAs get significantly less engagement
- Match the CTA to the user's likely intent at that stage
- Always verify the link URL is correct and leads to the right landing page
- For Slimming: "Book" links to free consultation page, not direct treatment booking

## Images and Visual Content

- **Posts with images get 2x engagement** compared to text-only posts
- **Recommended dimensions:** 1200x900 pixels (4:3 ratio) for optimal display
- **What to include:** Facility photos (pools, treatment rooms, reception), food/drinks, Mediterranean views, team photos
- **What to avoid:** Stock photography (Google's algorithm may penalise it), before/after images (compliance risk), text-heavy graphics
- **Geo-tagging:** If possible, geo-tag photos with the business location for additional local SEO signals
- **Future iteration:** Add image upload step to the Playwright posting flow

## Keyword Strategy for GBP Posts

### Keyword Placement
- Include the primary keyword in the **first 100 characters** (visible in preview)
- Use the primary keyword once more in the body text (naturally)
- Weave secondary keywords into the body without forcing them
- Always include "Malta" as a location keyword
- Add specific location references: "St Julian's", "near Sliema", "Paceville", "central Malta"

### Keyword Density
- **Target:** 1-2% keyword density. Natural integration, never stuffing
- **Never:** Repeat the same keyword more than 3 times in a single post
- **Test:** Read the post aloud. If any keyword sounds forced or repetitive, rewrite

### Keyword Rotation
- No same primary keyword within 3 consecutive posts for the same brand
- Rotate through the full keyword bank over a 4-week cycle
- Track keyword usage in the post log to ensure coverage
- Seasonal keywords take priority during their relevant periods

## Local SEO Signals Beyond Posts

### Review Management
- **Respond to every review within 24 hours** -- review response rate is a confirmed ranking factor
- Use the brand voice in review responses (warm for Spa, clinical-warm for Aesthetics, compassionate for Slimming)
- Reference specific services or experiences mentioned in the review
- Thank positive reviewers by name
- For negative reviews: acknowledge, apologise specifically, offer resolution privately

### Q&A Section
- **Pre-populate with 10-15 FAQs** per brand targeting long-tail keywords
- Answer in the brand voice with keyword-rich responses
- Monitor for new questions weekly and respond promptly
- Example Q&As:
  - "Do I need to bring anything to the spa?" (Spa)
  - "Is the consultation really free?" (Aesthetics)
  - "What is CoolSculpting and how does it work?" (Slimming)

### Profile Completeness
- All services listed with individual descriptions and pricing
- Business hours accurate and updated for holidays
- All attributes filled (accessibility, amenities, payment methods)
- Website, phone, and address (NAP) consistent across all online directories
- 20+ photos uploaded, refreshed monthly
- Products/menu items added for key packages

## GBP Post Lifespan and Timing

- **Visibility:** Posts remain visible on the profile for approximately 6 months
- **Peak engagement:** First 7 days after publishing receive the most views and clicks
- **Best posting days for Malta:** Thursday (weekend planning), Saturday (impulse bookings), Tuesday (mid-week recovery seekers)
- **Best posting times:** 10:00-12:00 Malta time (CET/CEST) for highest local visibility
- **Seasonal peaks:**
  - January: New Year reset, wellness resolutions
  - February: Valentine's Day couples packages
  - March-April: Easter, spring renewal, wedding season prep
  - May: Mother's Day (critical for Spa gift vouchers)
  - June-August: Summer preparation, beach confidence, holiday bookings
  - September-October: Autumn restoration, back-to-routine wellness
  - November-December: Christmas gifting, festive packages, year-end self-care

## Multi-Location Strategy

### Carisma Spa (5 Hotel Locations)
- **Same post, copied to all locations** is the baseline strategy
- **Slight variations improve authenticity:** Where possible, reference the specific hotel name or nearby landmark in the post text
- **Copy dialog:** After posting to the primary location, the GBP "Copy post" dialog allows selecting additional locations to copy the post to. Always select all remaining Spa locations
- **Future iteration:** Create location-specific post variations (e.g., "Carisma Spa at the InterContinental, overlooking St George's Bay" vs "Carisma Spa at the Westin, steps from the Golden Mile")

### Carisma Aesthetics and Slimming (Single Locations)
- No multi-location posting needed
- Skip the "Copy post" dialog after publishing

## Compliance Guidelines

### Medical Claims
- **Never** claim a treatment "cures", "eliminates", or "permanently fixes" a condition
- **Allowed:** "Clinically shown to reduce", "FDA-cleared for", "Studies demonstrate"
- **For Slimming:** Always include "Suitability subject to doctor approval"
- **For Aesthetics:** Always reference "qualified practitioners" and "personalised assessment"

### Pricing
- Always use "from" prefix: "from EUR 89", "from EUR 199"
- Never imply pricing is the final cost without qualification
- Include any significant terms (e.g., "value EUR 625" for Slimming starter packs)

### Before/After Content
- **Never** reference before/after photos or transformations in GBP posts
- Text-based testimonials (anonymised) are permitted
- Use phrases like "our guests tell us" rather than "see the results"

### No Shame Language
- Especially critical for Slimming brand
- **Never:** "fix your body", "problem areas", "stubborn fat you hate", "get your body back"
- **Instead:** "areas that matter to you", "changes you have been working towards", "feel comfortable in your skin"

## Malta-Specific Considerations

### Maltese Language Touches
- **Mela** -- Conversational filler meaning "so/well then". Adds local warmth
- **Prosit** -- "Cheers/congratulations". Good for celebratory CTAs
- **Grazzi** -- "Thank you". Natural closing touch
- **Il-jum (Friday)** -- Can reference specific days in Maltese
- **Ghaziza** -- "Dear/darling" (feminine). Intimate, use sparingly
- **Sabiha** -- "Beautiful" (feminine). Natural complement
- Use sparingly and naturally -- 1-2 Maltese touches per post maximum

### Local Landmarks and References
- **St Julian's:** Spinola Bay, Portomaso, Paceville, Bay Street
- **Sliema:** The Promenade, Tower Road, Tigne Point
- **Valletta:** Republic Street, Grand Harbour, Upper Barrakka
- **Gozo:** Victoria, Cirkewwa ferry, Mgarr Harbour
- **Beaches:** Golden Bay, Ghajn Tuffieha, Blue Lagoon
- Reference travel times: "25 minutes from Cirkewwa ferry", "15 minutes from Valletta"

### Phone Numbers

- Spa: referenced in location data
- Aesthetics: referenced in location data
- Slimming: +356 2780 2062

### Cultural Calendar
- **Festa season (May-September):** Town patron saint festivals. Reference local celebrations
- **Carnival (February):** Pre-Lent celebrations
- **Easter (March/April):** Important in Catholic Malta
- **Christmas (December):** Major gifting period -- push gift vouchers heavily
- **Summer (June-September):** Beach season, tourism peak, summer confidence messaging

## Measurement and Iteration

### Metrics to Track (via GBP Insights)
- **Views:** How many times the post was shown in search results and Maps
- **Clicks:** Click-through to website, phone, directions
- **Actions:** Bookings, calls, direction requests attributed to the post
- **Photo views:** If images are included

### Iteration Rules
- After 4 weeks, review which post types and keywords generated the most clicks
- Double down on high-performing angles
- Retire or revise consistently low-performing templates
- Update keyword banks quarterly based on Search Console and GBP Insights data
- Seasonal content should be refreshed annually -- never reuse last year's seasonal posts verbatim
