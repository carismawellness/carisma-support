## PLAN OVERVIEW

**Cadence:** 3 emails per week (Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday)
**Alternation:** Design promo > TB_ relationship > Design promo > TB_ (never 3 promos in a row)
**Total Emails:** 39 (13 per month)
**Content Type Rotation:** All 7 universal content types appear each month

### Monthly Arc Structure

Each month follows a four-week emotional arc:
- **Week 1:** Awareness / Teaser -- introduce the theme or upcoming occasion
- **Week 2:** Education / Value -- deepen engagement with tips, stories, benefits
- **Week 3:** Offer / Promotion -- the main promotional push
- **Week 4:** Urgency / Last chance -- final call, wrap-up

### Content Type Key
