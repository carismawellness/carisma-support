## 📊 KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

### The Recommendation

**Structure: EUR 300K Cash at Close + EUR 100K Performance Earnout (tied to clinic EBITDA)**

**Why This Wins:**
1. ✓ Lowest capital commitment (EUR 300K upfront preserves capex capital)
2. ✓ Highest seller acceptance (80-90% vs. 15-70% for alternatives)
3. ✓ Best seller alignment (EUR 100K at stake on clinic success)
4. ✓ Manageable leverage (1.27x vs. 2.0x+ in other structures)
5. ✓ Flexible total value (EUR 300K-400K based on performance)

**Seller Motivation:**
- EUR 300K is close to his EUR 250K anchor
- EUR 100K earnout feels like "upside sharing"
- Incentivizes him to support post-close clinic launch
- Clear, transparent measurement = low dispute risk

## 🎓 CONSULTANT METHODOLOGY

This analysis follows BCG deal structuring best practices:

1. **Pressure Test Each Scenario** — Financial stress testing, risk assessment, acceptability analysis
2. **Rank by Risk/Reward** — Multi-dimensional comparison (capital, leverage, acceptance, alignment)
3. **Identify Gaps** — What alternatives haven't been considered?
4. **Recommend Single Optimal** — Not "here are pros and cons"; rather "here's the best structure"
5. **Provide Execution Plan** — Timeline, talking points, objection handlers, decision gates
