## Red Flags & Contingencies

### If nervous system diagnostic is not triggering...
- **Problem:** Agents not asking discovery question 7b
- **Solution:** Role-play training, add diagnostic to quick reference card, reminder in daily standup
- **Timeline:** Re-assess by Week 4

### If cancellation recovery messaging sounds pushy...
- **Problem:** Agents are using pressure language despite guidelines
- **Solution:** Review all scripts together, highlight "flexibility first" language, role-play difficult scenarios
- **Timeline:** Immediate correction before next cancellation

### If belonging-anchor conversions are not lifting...
- **Problem:** Messages are too generic (agents not observing specifically)
- **Solution:** Therapist training on "what to observe," review of first 20 messages, examples of strong vs. weak observations
- **Timeline:** Correction by Week 3

### If agents resist the new skills...
- **Problem:** Feels like more work, or inconsistent with existing workflows
- **Solution:** Demonstrate ROI (revenue impact), show customer feedback, make it a team success (not individual burden)
- **Timeline:** Address in Week 1 team meeting
