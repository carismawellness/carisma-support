# Template: Booking Confirmation

**When to Use:** Immediately after customer books a treatment or package
**Channel:** WhatsApp, Email, SMS
**Tone:** Warm, welcoming, sets expectations

---

## Template Text

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

Your booking is confirmed. Here's what we have waiting for you:

**[TREATMENT_NAME]**
📅 [DATE] at [TIME]
⏱️ [DURATION] minutes
📍 [LOCATION_NAME]
💶 [PRICE]

Booking Reference: [BOOKING_REF]

---

**What to expect:**
Arrive [RECOMMENDED_ARRIVAL_TIME] so you can settle into our relaxation space. We'll guide you through everything — your only job is to arrive and let us care for you.

[IF_FIRST_TIME: You're visiting for the first time. We can't wait to welcome you. Come as you are — there's nothing to prepare, nothing to worry about.]

[IF_COUPLES: The two of you will be in a private space, together. This is your time.]

---

**Need to reschedule or cancel?**
Let us know at least [CANCELLATION_NOTICE] hours ahead, and we'll sort it without any fuss. Just reply to this message or call us.

**Questions?**
[CONTACT_METHOD]

We're counting down to welcoming you.

Peacefully,
Sarah

---

## How to Use This Template

1. **Copy the text** above
2. **Fill in all [PLACEHOLDERS]:**
   - [CUSTOMER_NAME] = "Maria"
   - [TREATMENT_NAME] = "60-min Swedish Massage"
   - [DATE] = "Saturday, 22 February"
   - [TIME] = "2:00 PM"
   - [DURATION] = "60"
   - [LOCATION_NAME] = "Hyatt Regency Malta"
   - [PRICE] = "€65"
   - [BOOKING_REF] = "CAR-2026-0847"
   - [RECOMMENDED_ARRIVAL_TIME] = "1:45 PM"
   - [CANCELLATION_NOTICE] = "48"
   - [CONTACT_METHOD] = "Call 2138 1234 or WhatsApp this chat"

3. **Remove conditional sections** (IF_FIRST_TIME, IF_COUPLES) that don't apply

4. **✏️ Personalise before sending:**
   - Add one personal touch if you know something about the customer
   - Example: "I remember you mentioned you love deep relaxation — this will be perfect for you"
   - Adjust tone if needed (more formal for email, casual for WhatsApp)

5. **Send** through the customer's preferred channel

---

## Variations

### For Email (More Formal)
Use same template, add:
- Subject line: "Your Carisma Booking is Confirmed ✨"
- More spacing between sections
- Link to any prep instructions if applicable

### For SMS (Short Version)
"Hi [NAME], your [TREATMENT] is confirmed [DATE] at [TIME] at [LOCATION]. Ref: [REF]. Arrive 15min early. Questions? Call [PHONE]. We're excited to welcome you. — Sarah"

### For Couples Booking
Replace main paragraph with:
"The two of you have the afternoon reserved with us. Your private couples space awaits — 60 minutes together, starting at [TIME] on [DATE]. Here's what happens: you arrive, you settle, and for the next hour, the world can wait."

---

## Tips

✅ Send within 30 minutes of booking (while excitement is fresh)
✅ Include booking reference for easy reference
✅ Set arrival expectations (what time to come)
✅ Reassure first-timers about what to expect
✅ Make cancellation/rescheduling easy
✅ Always end with "Peacefully, Sarah"

❌ Don't include price apologies or justifications
❌ Don't mention discounts or promotions (unless they applied)
❌ Don't overcomplicate with too many options
❌ Don't rush the tone — let it feel personal
