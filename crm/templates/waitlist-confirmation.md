# Template: Waitlist Confirmation

**When to Use:** Customer asks to be added to a waitlist for a fully-booked slot
**Channel:** WhatsApp, Email
**Tone:** Reassuring, hopeful, practical

---

## Template Text

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

You're on the list.

**Your Waitlist Entry:**
- Treatment: [TREATMENT_NAME]
- Preferred date/time: [DATE] at [TIME]
- Location: [LOCATION_NAME]
- Your contact: [PHONE_NUMBER]

**Here's how this works:**
If someone cancels and a slot opens up, we'll reach out to you first — usually within minutes. You'll get [CONTACT_METHOD: WhatsApp/call/email] with the confirmation.

You have [TIME_WINDOW: e.g., "2 hours"] to confirm before we move to the next person on the list.

---

**In the meantime:**
While you wait, other slots might open up. Want me to check alternative times?

**Let me know if:**
- Your availability changes
- You'd prefer a different time
- You want to book a different date instead (no pressure)

We'll keep trying for your preferred slot. But if timing shifts, just say the word.

Peacefully,
Sarah

---

## How to Use This Template

1. **Fill in all [PLACEHOLDERS]:**
   - [CUSTOMER_NAME] = "Maria"
   - [TREATMENT_NAME] = "60-min Swedish Massage"
   - [DATE] = "Saturday, 22 February"
   - [TIME] = "2:00 PM"
   - [LOCATION_NAME] = "Hyatt Regency Malta"
   - [PHONE_NUMBER] = "[Their number]"
   - [CONTACT_METHOD] = "WhatsApp"
   - [TIME_WINDOW] = "2 hours"

2. **Verify all details are correct** before sending

3. **✏️ Personalise before sending:**
   - Add encouragement: "I have a good feeling about this one"
   - Be honest about chances: "Saturday is popular, so keep alternatives in mind"

4. **Send** immediately when adding to waitlist

---

## Variations

### If Waitlist is Long
Be honest: "You're #3 on the waitlist. Cancellations do happen, but also consider these alternatives [ALTERNATIVES]."

### If This Slot Never Opens Up
Preempt: "Saturdays book solid. If this doesn't open by [DATE], shall we move you to Sunday? Just let me know."

### If Alternative Slots Are Available
Add: "While you're on the waitlist for Saturday, these times are open right now [LIST]. Want either of those as backup?"

### For VIP or Repeat Customers
Add: "You're actually first on our list — we'll do everything we can to make this happen for you."

---

## Tips

✅ Confirm their details clearly (no mistakes)
✅ Explain the process simply (people get confused)
✅ Set realistic expectations (be honest about chances)
✅ Include time window for confirmation (so you know they're still interested)
✅ Offer alternatives while they wait (don't leave them hanging)
✅ Make it easy to cancel if they change mind
✅ Always end with "Peacefully, Sarah"

❌ Don't guarantee anything ("we'll definitely find something")
❌ Don't let them wait forever — set a cutoff
❌ Don't contact them too late at night or early morning
❌ Don't forget to move to next person if they don't confirm in time
