# Template: No Availability — Redirect with Alternatives

**When to Use:** Customer wants a specific date/time but it's fully booked
**Channel:** WhatsApp, Email
**Tone:** Apologetic but helpful, offers genuine alternatives

---

## Template Text

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

I've checked and [DATE] at [TIME] is fully booked for [TREATMENT_NAME]. I'm sorry about that — that date seems popular.

**Here's what I can offer instead:**

**Option 1: Different time same day**
[DATE] at [ALT_TIME] is available. Does this work?

**Option 2: Different day, same time**
[ALT_DATE] at [TIME] is open. Would this suit you?

**Option 3: Waitlist**
If you're set on [DATE] at [TIME], I can add you to our waitlist. If someone cancels, we'll reach out immediately.

**Option 4: Nearby location**
[DATE] at [TIME] is available at our [OTHER_LOCATION] — [DISTANCE] away. Worth considering?

---

**Can't wait? Same-day options**
If you need something urgently, we often have last-minute slots. Call us at [PHONE] and we'll see what we can find.

What works best for you?

Peacefully,
Sarah

---

## How to Use This Template

1. **Fill in all [PLACEHOLDERS]:**
   - [CUSTOMER_NAME] = "Maria"
   - [DATE] = "Saturday, 22 February"
   - [TIME] = "2:00 PM"
   - [TREATMENT_NAME] = "60-min Swedish Massage"
   - [ALT_TIME] = "3:30 PM"
   - [ALT_DATE] = "Sunday, 23 February"
   - [OTHER_LOCATION] = "InterContinental Malta"
   - [DISTANCE] = "10 minutes away"
   - [PHONE] = "2138 1234"

2. **Only include options that are actually available** — don't offer fake options

3. **✏️ Personalise before sending:**
   - Sympathize: "I know you had your heart set on that time"
   - Build confidence in alternatives: "The 3:30 slot is actually in our quietest part of the day — often a better experience"

4. **Send** immediately when they ask about unavailable time

---

## Variations

### If Different Location is Much Better
Lead with that: "Saturday is booked at Hyatt, but our InterContinental location has the exact same time — it's actually right by the beach if that appeals."

### If Only Waitlist is an Option
Be honest: "Saturday is fully booked and no nearby slots are open. Honestly, I'd recommend trying Sunday morning (8:30am is available) — you'll have the spa almost to yourself. Or waitlist for Saturday?"

### If Only Far-Away Alternative Exists
Offer honestly: "The nearest available slot is Wednesday at [TIME] at [LOCATION]. I know it's not ideal. Should we add you to Saturday's waitlist instead?"

### For Repeat Customers
Add: "I know you usually come on Saturdays — let me see if there's anything special I can find for you. Give me an hour and I'll check with our manager about opening a slot."

---

## Tips

✅ Apologize genuinely (no defensive "sorry but...")
✅ Offer multiple real alternatives, not fake ones
✅ Be honest about waitlist realistic-ness
✅ Suggest nearby locations without pushiness
✅ Remind them same-day phone calls might work
✅ Always end with "Peacefully, Sarah"

❌ Don't say "I'll try to find something" if you can't
❌ Don't pressure toward more expensive options
❌ Don't make them feel like an inconvenience
❌ Don't offer options outside their stated preference without clear value
