## The Carisma Slimming Method (4 Pillars)

### 1. WHEN — Timing & Rhythm
- Default: 4-hour delay from waking before first meal
- Finish dinner 3hrs before sleep
- Two tracks: Fasting (default) or Non-Fasting (medical/personal reasons)
- Morning tools: sparkling water, black coffee, plain tea

### 2. WHAT — Nutrition
- Protein first at every meal (1-2 palm portions)
- Points system: 1 calorie = 1 point; non-starchy vegetables = 0 points
- Fibre floor: 25-30g daily
- No forbidden foods — everything has points
- Watch liquid points (sweet coffees, juices, alcohol)

### 3. HOW MUCH — Amounts
- Protein: 1.4-2.0g per kg target body weight (clinic-set)
- Points budget: Set by clinic (typically 1200-1800 range)
- Vegetables: 2 fists per main meal (free volume)

### 4. WHICH ORDER — Plate Formula
- **Build:** Protein → Vegetables → Carbs → Fats (purposeful)
- **Eat:** Protein & veg first, then carbs (blood sugar stability)
- One main carb source per meal (don't stack bread + pasta + rice)

## Core Philosophy (For Customer Responses)

**"Adherence over perfection."**

Three anchors to return to:
1. **Pace** — Keep rhythm consistent
2. **Protein-first** — Anchor every meal
3. **Return at the next meal** — Slip-ups are normal; returning is what counts

**Key phrases:**
- "You didn't fail; the plan failed you"
- "Slip-ups are information, not failure"
- "One meal doesn't break the system"
- "Structure over willpower"

## Safety — Who Should NOT Fast

- Pregnant or breastfeeding
- Type 1 diabetes / insulin-dependent
- History of disordered eating
- Regularly dizzy/faint without breakfast
- Under 18
- Doctor has advised against fasting
- Taking medications requiring food

**Rule:** If you feel unwell, eat. Safety first. Then tell the clinic.

## GLP-1 Quick Reference

**Eligible:** BMI >= 27, insulin resistance, emotional eating history, menopause-related weight gain
**Not eligible:** Eating disorders, very lean, unwilling to attend check-ins, pregnant/trying to conceive

**Common side effects & management:**
- Nausea → slow titration, protein-first meals, hydration
- Constipation → fibre targets, fluids, movement, magnesium
- Fatigue → calorie floors, protein monitoring, strength habits

**Key message:** "GLP-1 supports but doesn't replace structure. Medication + nutrition + movement + accountability."

## Refer to Clinic For

- Personalized protein/calorie targets
- Medical conditions affecting intake (diabetes, kidney, liver, thyroid)
- Medications affecting metabolism
- Pregnancy/breastfeeding
- Eating disorder history
- Persistent dizziness or feeling unwell
- Progress plateau with unclear cause
- Exercise prescription
- Emotional eating/mental health

**Rule:** Don't diagnose. Don't personalize targets. Always refer to clinic.
