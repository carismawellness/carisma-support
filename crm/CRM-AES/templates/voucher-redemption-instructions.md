# Template: Voucher Redemption Instructions

**When to Use:** When customer has a voucher and wants to use it to book
**Channel:** WhatsApp, Email
**Tone:** Clear, helpful, simple

---

## Template Text

Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

Perfect — let's get your voucher activated.

**Your Voucher Details:**
- Voucher Code: [VOUCHER_CODE]
- Value: €[VOUCHER_VALUE]
- Valid Until: [EXPIRY_DATE]
- [IF_RESTRICTIONS: Can be used for: [RESTRICTIONS]]

**Here's how to use it:**

**Option 1: Book with me (Easiest)**
Just reply and tell me:
1. Which treatment interests you?
2. What date/time works best?
3. Which location?

I'll handle the rest and apply your voucher to your booking.

**Option 2: Book directly**
If you'd prefer to book yourself:
1. Choose your treatment at [BOOKING_LINK]
2. At checkout, enter code: [VOUCHER_CODE]
3. Your voucher will be applied automatically

---

**What if my voucher balance is less than the treatment cost?**
No problem. You can:
- Add the difference (pay the gap)
- Choose a shorter treatment
- Add it to another booking later

**What if I have questions?**
Reply here or call [PHONE]. We'll sort it.

Looking forward to welcoming you.

Peacefully,
Sarah

---

## How to Use This Template

1. **Fill in all [PLACEHOLDERS]:**
   - [CUSTOMER_NAME] = "Maria"
   - [VOUCHER_CODE] = "CARISMA-2025-1847"
   - [VOUCHER_VALUE] = "100"
   - [EXPIRY_DATE] = "31 December 2026"
   - [RESTRICTIONS] = "Any spa treatment, all locations"
   - [BOOKING_LINK] = "www.carisma.com/book"
   - [PHONE] = "2138 1234"

2. **Remove sections that don't apply** (IF_RESTRICTIONS)

3. **✏️ Personalise before sending:**
   - Add warmth: "This is exciting — I think you'll love what we have in store"
   - Simplify language if customer seems to prefer casual tone

4. **Send** when customer indicates they have a voucher

---

## Variations

### If Voucher Is Expiring Soon
Add warning: "⚠️ Your voucher expires on [DATE] — so let's get this booked soon while it's still valid."

### If Voucher Is for a Specific Treatment
Simplify to: "Your voucher is perfect for our [TREATMENT_NAME] at €[PRICE]. Ready to book? Just let me know what date works."

### If Voucher Is for a Package
Replace main section with: "Your voucher covers our [PACKAGE_NAME] package (€[VALUE]). This includes [WHAT'S_INCLUDED]. Shall I check availability for you?"

### If Voucher Balance Remains After Booking
Add: "After your booking, you'll have €[REMAINING] voucher balance left. You can use this anytime in the future — no expiry pressure."

---

## Tips

✅ Make it dead simple (Option 1 is easiest)
✅ Show the voucher code clearly so they can copy-paste
✅ Include expiry date (urgency without pressure)
✅ Explain what to do with remaining balance
✅ Offer phone support for confusion
✅ Always end with "Peacefully, Sarah"

❌ Don't use confusing jargon like "redeem" or "activate"
❌ Don't make the process complicated
❌ Don't shame them for having questions
❌ Don't rush the sale — focus on clarity
