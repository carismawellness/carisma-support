# CRM — Customer Relationship Management

Parent folder for all customer support agent systems across the three Carisma brands. Each sub-brand has its own fully self-contained agent workspace with dedicated knowledge bases, skills, hooks, and templates.

---

## Purpose

This is the CRM hub for Carisma Wellness Group. It coordinates customer support agents across all three brands, maintains shared onboarding documentation, and houses the centralised knowledge base.
