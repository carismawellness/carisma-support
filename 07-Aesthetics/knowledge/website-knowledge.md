## 12. Malta Regulatory Notes

Medical aesthetics advertising in Malta operates within a regulatory framework:

- **Medical practitioner advertising** must be factual, ethical, and not deceptive
- **Avoid** anything that reads as deceptive certainty (guarantees, absolutes, "100% results")
- Malta has regulatory framework governing medicinal product advertising
- **Best practice:** Use "education-first" creative. Tasteful before/after imagery. No unrealistic promises.
- **Disclaimer requirements:** Treatment results vary, medical consultation required, etc.

**Impact on content:** All marketing materials, ads, and patient-facing copy must avoid:
- Guaranteed outcomes
- Absolute claims ("permanent," "100%," "guaranteed")
- Misleading before/after representations
- Unauthorized medical claims
