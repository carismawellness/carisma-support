## 1. Injectables and Medical Aesthetics

### Wrinkle Relaxers (Anti-Wrinkle Injections)

**What it does:** Smooths dynamic lines, the ones that appear with movement, while preserving your natural expression. The goal is refreshed and rested, never frozen.

**How it works:** A purified protein is injected into targeted facial muscles, temporarily relaxing them so lines soften naturally.

**Pricing:**

| Treatment | Price |
|-----------|-------|
| Small areas (Lip Flip, gummy smile, chin) | from €59 |
| One area | from €118 to €139 |
| Full upper face / Nefertiti neck lift | from €239 to €249 |
| Underarm sweating (hyperhidrosis) | from €399 to €449 |

**Duration:** 15-30 minutes

**Results timeline:**
- First visible improvement: 3-5 days
- Full results: 2 weeks
- Duration of results: 3-6 months

**Brands:** Botox (botulinum toxin type A)

**Key points:**
- Small-area pricing from €59 creates an accessible entry point unique in Malta
- Lip Flip is a subtle upper lip enhancement using wrinkle relaxer, not filler
- Nefertiti neck lift targets platysmal bands for a smoother, more defined neck and jawline
- Hyperhidrosis treatment reduces excessive underarm sweating for several months
- Always doctor-led, always personalized to facial anatomy

**Practitioner lead:** Dr. Francesca Chircop (also performed by Dr. Giovanni Scornavacca)

## 2. Skin Treatments and Regenerative

### Microneedling (with Mesotherapy)

**What it does:** Stimulates collagen production through precise micro-injuries, combined with targeted nutrient delivery via mesotherapy. Improves skin texture, tone, scarring, and overall quality.

**How it works:** Fine needles create controlled micro-channels in the skin, triggering the body's natural healing response. Mesotherapy cocktails (vitamins, hyaluronic acid, growth factors) are delivered directly into the skin during treatment.

**Pricing:**

| Sessions | Price |
|----------|-------|
| 1 session | €149 |
| 3 sessions | €359 |
| 5 sessions | €599 |

**Results timeline:**
- Skin begins to renew: 2-4 weeks after first session
- Noticeable improvement: after 2-3 sessions
- Optimal results: series of 3-6 treatments

**Key points:**

## 3. Laser Treatments

### Laser Hair Removal

**What it does:** Permanent hair reduction across all skin types. Targets hair follicles to significantly reduce hair growth over a course of sessions.

**How it works:** The Alma Soprano Ice Platinum uses triple-wavelength technology (combining three laser wavelengths simultaneously) with SHR (Super Hair Removal) mode and ICE Plus cooling for virtually painless treatment across all skin types and hair colors.

**Pricing:**

| Package | Price |
|---------|-------|
| 6 sessions, Brazilian | €235 |
| 6 sessions, 30-minute area | €283 |
| 6 sessions, 60-minute area | €475 |

**Duration:** 15-60 minutes per session (depends on treatment area)

**Results timeline:**
- Hair begins to thin: after 1-2 sessions
- Significant reduction: after 3-4 sessions
- Optimal results: 6 sessions (spaced 4-6 weeks apart)
- Maintenance: occasional touch-up sessions may be needed

**Technology: Alma Soprano Ice Platinum**
- Triple wavelength: 755nm (Alexandrite), 810nm (Diode), 1064nm (Nd:YAG)
- SHR (Super Hair Removal) mode for gradual, comfortable heating
- ICE Plus cooling technology for virtually pain-free experience
- Safe for all skin types (Fitzpatrick I-VI)

**Key points:**
- One of the most advanced laser hair removal systems available
- The triple-wavelength approach treats different hair depths simultaneously
- ICE Plus cooling means most patients describe the sensation as warm, not painful (3-5/10)
- Avoid sun exposure before and after treatment
- Shave (do not wax) the treatment area before your session

**Clinical oversight:** Dr. Francesca Chircop

## 4. Body Treatments

### Fat Dissolving

**What it does:** Targets and reduces stubborn pockets of fat that resist diet and exercise, particularly effective for the jawline, chin, and small body areas.

**How it works:** A deoxycholic acid solution is injected into targeted fat deposits, breaking down fat cells which are then naturally eliminated by the body.

**Pricing:**

| Sessions | Price |
|----------|-------|
| 1 session | €149 |
| 2 sessions | €249 |
| 3 sessions | €349 |

Also positioned as the **"Snatch Your Jawline"** package (see Signature Packages below).

**Duration:** 30-45 minutes

**Results timeline:**
- Swelling and tenderness: 1-2 weeks post-treatment
- Fat reduction becomes visible: 4-6 weeks
- Optimal results: after 2-3 sessions (spaced 4-6 weeks apart)
- Duration: permanent (treated fat cells do not return)

**Key points:**
- Non-surgical alternative to liposuction for small, targeted areas
- Course pricing provides progressive savings
- Temporary swelling, bruising, and tenderness are expected and normal
- Results are permanent as treated fat cells are destroyed
- Not a weight loss treatment. Designed for targeted contouring

## 6. Thread Lifts

### Thread Lift

**What it does:** A minimally invasive lifting procedure for face and neck that provides immediate visible lift with progressive collagen stimulation over time.

**How it works:** Specialized medical threads are placed beneath the skin to physically lift and reposition sagging tissue. Over time, the threads also stimulate collagen production along their path.

**Pricing:**

| Treatment | Price |
|-----------|-------|
| Semi-permanent thread lift | from €2,100 |

**Note:** These are NOT PDO threads. Semi-permanent medical-grade threads.

Also available as part of the **Ultimate Facelift** package (see Signature Packages below).

**Duration:** 60-90 minutes

**Results timeline:**
- Immediate lift visible: day of treatment
- Collagen stimulation develops: over 2-6 months
- Duration: 12-24 months depending on thread type and patient factors

**Key points:**
- Doctor-assessed suitability is essential. Not suitable for all patients
- Minimally invasive alternative to surgical facelift
